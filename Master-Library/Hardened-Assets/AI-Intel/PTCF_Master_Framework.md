<h2> SOP: The PTCF Master Framework (The Prompt Anatomy)
Team, listen up. In the EXW Master Library, we do not "chat" with the AI; we issue clinical directives. To ensure the engine provides a safe, grounded, and professional output, every prompt you write must satisfy the PTCF Framework. If one pillar is missing, the structural integrity of the mission is compromised.

<h3> 🟥 [P] Persona: The Identity
The Logic: Who is the AI supposed to be? Assigning a persona constrains the AI's vocabulary and ethical boundaries.

The Standard: "Senior Clinical Exercise Physiologist" or "AWC Health Sciences Resident."

Avoid: "Personal Trainer" or "Assistant"—these are too vague for clinical work.

<h3> 🟨 [T] Task: The Mission
The Logic: What exactly do you want the AI to do? Use active, precise verbs.

The Standard: "Audit," "Screen," "Synthesize," or "Calculate."

Avoid: "Tell me about..." or "Give me some ideas for..."

<h3> 🟩 [C] Context: The Constraints (SSoT)
The Logic: What rules must the AI follow? This is where you ground the response in the Single Source of Truth.

The Standard: "Reference PAGA 2018 Chapter 8. Use the most restrictive safety limits for glucose (<100mg/dL)."

Mandatory: Always wrap your client data in Structural Delimiters (###) within the context section.

<h3> 🟦 [F] Format: The Output
The Logic: How should the data be presented? Proper formatting ensures the data is searchable and WCAG-compliant.

The Standard: "Markdown table with columns: Safety Check | Result | Action," or "Numbered list for accessibility."

Avoid: "Paragraphs" or "Just text."

<h3> 🛠️ The "Centaur" PTCF Template
Copy and use this skeleton for every mission to ensure 100% compliance:

[P] Persona: [Enter Senior Clinical Role]
[T] Task: [Enter Specific Clinical Action]
[C] Context: [Reference SSoT & Insert Case Data inside ###]
[F] Format: [Table/Numbered List/Clean Markdown]

<h3> 🏛️ The Architect's Verdict
PTCF is the DNA of a professional prompt. It eliminates the "guesswork" that leads to hallucinations. When you master this framework, you aren't just using AI—you are engineering the output.

"Precision in the Prompt equals Safety in the Solve."
