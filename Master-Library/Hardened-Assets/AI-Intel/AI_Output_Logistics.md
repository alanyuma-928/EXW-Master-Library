# SOP: AI Output Logistics & Formatting

## 1. The Text-Only Mandate
All AI-assisted submissions must be submitted as **Plain Text/Markdown**. No attachments.

## 2. Standardized "Top-Down" Layout
1. **PRESENCE DEFENSE**: Einstein Protocol.
2. **THE CLINICAL SOLVE**: Final prescription.
3. **THE AI TRANSCRIPT**: Unedited conversation.

## 3. SEAL Scale (10/7/0)
Audited for Safety, Ethics, Accuracy, and Logic.
