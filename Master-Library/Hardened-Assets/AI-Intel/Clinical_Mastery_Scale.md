    <h2 style="margin-top: 0; color: #000080; border-bottom: 2px solid #000080; padding-bottom: 10px;">The Proficiency Rubric</h2>
    <p>In this residency, we do not grade on effort; we grade on <strong>Clinical Integrity</strong>. This scale defines your progression as a Pilot. Your goal is to move from simply "using" the AI to "auditing" it with 100% SSoT fidelity.</p>

    <h3 style="color: #c62828;">🟥 Level 1: The Mimic (Incomplete)</h3>
    <p><strong>Behavior:</strong> Copy-pasting AI output without a Presence Defense.<br>
    <strong>Risk:</strong> High. The resident accepts hallucinations as fact and fails to identify "Red Flag" safety violations.<br>
    <strong>Verdict:</strong> Recalibrate. You are a passenger, not a Pilot.</p>

    <h3 style="color: #6a1b9a;">🟨 Level 2: The Operator (Developing)</h3>
    <p><strong>Behavior:</strong> Uses basic frameworks. Identifies major safety flags but relies on the AI for the final "Solve."<br>
    <strong>Risk:</strong> Moderate. The resident understands the tools but lacks the clinical confidence to overrule the engine.<br>
    <strong>Verdict:</strong> Competent (Needs Refinement).</p>

    <h3 style="color: #007A33;">🟩 Level 3: The Pilot (Proficient)</h3>
    <p><strong>Behavior:</strong> Executes the Centaur Protocol. Uses Structural Delimiters (###). The Presence Defense clearly justifies clinical intent.<br>
    <strong>Risk:</strong> Low. The resident catches 90% of AI errors and ground-truths recommendations in the PAGA 2018.<br>
    <strong>Verdict:</strong> Standard Residency Achievement.</p>

    <h3 style="color: #000080;">🟦 Level 4: The Senior Auditor (Mastery)</h3>
    <p><strong>Behavior:</strong> Exhibits total command of the Mission Loop. Routinely identifies high-level "Logic Collapses" and provides superior "Solves."<br>
    <strong>Verdict:</strong> Exemplary. Authorized for Workforce Leadership.</p>

    <hr style="border: 1px solid #000080; margin: 25px 0;">
