<h2> SOP: Clinical Mastery Scale (The Proficiency Rubric)
Team, listen up. In this residency, we do not grade on effort; we grade on Clinical Integrity. This scale defines your progression as a Pilot. Your goal is to move from simply "using" the AI to "auditing" it with 100% SSoT fidelity.

Behavior: Copy-pasting AI output without a Presence Defense.

Risk: High. The student accepts hallucinations as fact and fails to identify "Red Flag" safety violations.

Verdict: Fail. You are a passenger, not a Pilot.

<h3> 🟨 Level 2: The Operator (Developing)
Behavior: Uses the P-T-C-F Framework and includes the Top-Down Layout. Identifies major safety flags but relies on the AI for the final "Solve."

Risk: Moderate. The student understands the tools but lacks the clinical confidence to overrule the engine.

Verdict: Pass (Needs Improvement).

<h3> 🟩 Level 3: The Pilot (Proficient)

Verdict: Standard Residency Achievement.

<h3> 🟦 Level 4: The Senior Auditor (Mastery)
Behavior: Exhibits total command of the Mission Loop. The student routinely identifies high-level "Logic Collapses" in the AI output and provides a superior "Solve" based on deep SSoT knowledge.
