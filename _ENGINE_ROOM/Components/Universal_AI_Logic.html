🧩 Universal AI Logic: The Stochastic Engine

To be an effective **Pilot**, you must understand how your **Engine** (AI) processes data. AI models are stochastic—they do not "think" in the human sense; they calculate the statistical probability of the next word.

Temperature

A parameter that controls the "creativity" or randomness of the output. Higher temperature results in more diverse (but potentially less accurate) responses.

Stochasticity

The element of chance. Because the engine is probabilistic, the same prompt can yield different results. Always audit for consistency.

The Architect's Mantra: Trust the probability for structure; trust the human for truth.
