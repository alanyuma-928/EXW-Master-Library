🚀 Launching Your AdaptiveFit Assistant

In this phase, you will deploy a custom AdaptiveFit assistant using Google Gemini. This tool is designed to automate routine clinical data processing while you maintain total architectural control.

Step 1: System Instruction

Copy the "Architect's Directive" from the current Module into the Custom Instructions field of your Gemini assistant.

Step 2: Grounding

Upload the relevant SSoT (PAGA/ACSM) PDF to the assistant's knowledge base.

AWC Principle: Custom assistants must never process sensitive student or patient PII. Verification remains the responsibility of the human pilot.
