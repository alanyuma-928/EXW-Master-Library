📊 The 10-7-0 SEAL Grading Protocol

This residency uses a Competency-Based scale. We do not award partial credit for unsafe or unverified clinical data.

10-7-0 Mastery Scale
Status Criteria
🟢 Exemplary (10) Human-led audit is flawlessly grounded in the SSoT. No safety errors.
🔵 Competent (7) Audit identifies primary hallucinations but may lack specific technical depth.
🔧 Recalibrate (0) Structural Failure. Missing Audit, HIPAA leak, or unsafe clinical data.

Note: A "Recalibrate" score is an invitation to fix the error and resubmit for full competency.
