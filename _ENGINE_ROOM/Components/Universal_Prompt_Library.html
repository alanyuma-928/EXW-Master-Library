📚 Universal Prompt Library & Iteration Lab

Mastering AI requires more than a single "Ask." It requires Iteration and Meta-Prompting. Use these verified templates to refine your clinical engine.

🔄 The Iteration Loop

If the first response is too generic, use this follow-up:

"That response was a good start, but it lacks specific safety gates for [Insert Context]. Rewrite the plan, ensuring every recommendation is cited against [Insert SSoT]."

🧠 Meta-Prompting (The Architect's Edge)

Ask the AI to critique its own instructions to improve accuracy:

"I am about to give you a task. Before I do, look at my prompt: [Insert Your Prompt]. Identify any ambiguities that might lead to a clinical 'hallucination' and suggest a better version of this prompt."

AWC Alignment: This iterative process ensures that the Pilot (Student) remains in control of the Engine (AI) at all times.
