AWC AI for All: Immediate Assessment

"AI as Quiz Master: The Clinical Checkride"

🌟 Institutional Guardrails

This exercise utilizes the AWC "AI for All" Guiding Principles to move beyond simple recall and toward Clinical Mastery:

  • Immediate Feedback Loop: Use the AI's explanations to understand the logic behind the correct answer.
  • The Auditor's Edge: If the AI's explanation contradicts your understanding of the SSoT (PAGA/ACSM), audit the response. This is where real learning happens.

🧠 Goal: Verify Mastery

Before you finalize your Lab submission, use the Quiz Master to identify any "blind spots" in your understanding of the current topic.

🛠️ Launching the Checkride

  1. Copy the Master Prompt in the black box below.
  2. Replace [TOPIC/SOURCE] with your subject or paste the text you want to be tested on.
  3. Paste the customized prompt into Google Gemini.

"Act as a quiz master. Create a 5-question multiple-choice quiz to gauge my immediate mastery of [TOPIC/SOURCE]. Ensure each question has four distinct answer choices and a clear correct answer. Present the full quiz first. After I submit my answers, provide the correct answers and a brief explanation for why each correct answer is accurate, and why the best incorrect choices were wrong."

🚀 AI Life Skills: Self-Directed Assessment

The ability to generate your own assessments is a powerful AI Life Skill. It shifts you from a passive consumer of information to an active Pilot of your own professional development.
