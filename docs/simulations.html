🎭 Clinical Simulations

The Low-Stakes Flight Simulator for High-Stakes Clinical Care

The Standardized Patient

Role-playing with AI allows you to practice the Mission Loop in real-time. You are the clinician; Gemini is the client. Your goal is to navigate the conversation, identify clinical patterns, and maintain PAGA safety standards.

Scenario 1: The Resistant Client

Objective: Use Motivational Interviewing to address exercise barriers for a client with Stage 2 Hypertension.

"Act as 'Mrs. Gable,' a 72-year-old client who is fearful of starting resistance training due to a previous back injury. I will act as your Exercise Specialist. Begin the session by telling me why you are hesitant to follow the PAGA guidelines I recommended."

Scenario 2: The "Hidden Trap" Audit

Objective: Spot the clinical contraindication during a routine intake interview.

"Act as 'John,' a 45-year-old with Type 2 Diabetes. You are eager to start a high-intensity program but you have a secret: you've been experiencing tingling in your feet lately (Peripheral Neuropathy). Don't mention it unless I ask the right clinical screening questions."
