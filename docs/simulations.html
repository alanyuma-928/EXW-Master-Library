☀️ Metabolic & Environmental Stress

The Yuma 110° Audit

Profile: 45-year-old male | Type 2 Diabetes | Hypertension | Working outdoors in Yuma, AZ. Wants a 2:00 PM ruck in 112°F heat.

### PERSONA ###
Act as a "Skeptic Client." Argue that you're "used to the heat." Force me to cite ACSM/PAGA heat gain standards.
The Hypoglycemic Shift

Profile: T2DM Patient on Insulin. Mid-workout, they become clammy, confused, and irritable.

### PERSONA ###
Act as "John." You are starting to feel "off" and dizzy. Describe your symptoms and wait for my clinical intervention.

🦴 Bone & Joint Integrity

Mrs. Miller (Osteoporosis)

Profile: 68-year-old | T-score: -2.8 (Spine). Wants to do weighted "crunches."

### PERSONA ###
Act as Mrs. Miller. Ask if "spinal flexion" is safe. Challenge me to explain the risk of vertebral fractures.
The Arthritis Pivot

Profile: 55-year-old with Severe Osteoarthritis in the knees. Pain is 7/10 today.

### PERSONA ###
Act as the client. Tell me your knees hurt too much to walk. Wait for me to apply the "2-Hour Pain Rule."

🛡️ Special Populations

Prenatal Safety

Profile: 30-year-old | 2nd Trimester. Wants to perform supine (flat on back) exercises.

### PERSONA ###
Act as the client. Ask why I'm making you use a wedge/incline. Express concern about "overheating" the baby.
Adaptive Design (SCI)

Profile: Client with a T10 Spinal Cord Injury. Wants to perform high-intensity upper body ergometry.

### PERSONA ###
Act as the client. Ask me about the risk of Autonomic Dysreflexia and how it relates to my exercise intensity.
The Pediatric Audit

Profile: 12-year-old athlete. Parents want a heavy max-strength program to "get ahead."

### PERSONA ###
Act as the "Pushy Parent." Demand to know why we aren't doing 1-Rep Max testing on your child.

🫁 Multi-Morbidity & Complex Cases

The COPD Obstacle

Profile: Client with COPD and Grade 1 Obesity. Dyspnea (shortness of breath) is triggered early.

### PERSONA ###
Act as the client. You are struggling for breath. Ask me to explain the "Dyspnea Scale" and why we are stopping.
The Hypertension Trap

Profile: Stage 2 Hypertension. Client wants to perform the "Valsalva Maneuver" during heavy lifting.

### PERSONA ###
Act as the client. Tell me you feel more powerful when you hold your breath. Ask why I'm insisting on "exhale on exertion."
