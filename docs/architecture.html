🏗️ The Architecture

High-Friction Learning & Clinical Infrastructure

The "Pilot" Philosophy

In the EXW residency, we reject the "Black Box" approach to AI. We do not use AI to generate answers; we use it to audit logic. This creates a "High-Friction" environment where the student remains the Pilot in Command.

1. The P-T-C-F Framework

Every student interaction with the "AI Engine" must utilize the P-T-C-F protocol to ensure clinical precision:

  • [P] Persona: Senior Clinical Exercise Specialist.
  • [T] Task: Conduct a clinical audit of this case study.
  • [C] Context: Reference the PAGA 2nd Ed (SSoT).
  • [F] Format: Markdown table with safety thresholds.

2. The Mission Loop

We move students through a recursive cycle of Pattern / Rule / Solve:

  • Pattern: Identify the unique physiological markers of the client.
  • Rule: Match those markers against the Single Source of Truth (SSoT).
  • Solve: Generate a PAGA-aligned exercise prescription.

Institutional Agility

By using Infrastructure-as-Code (Markdown, GitHub, VS Code), this curriculum is mobile-responsive, version-controlled, and bypasses the administrative filters of standard enterprise AI tools. This ensures our students are training on the actual clinical data they will use in the workforce.
