Ensuring High-Fidelity Clinical AI Logic

📐 What is the P-T-C-F Framework?

In the **AWC Exercise Science** program, we don't just "chat" with AI; we engineer our interactions. Use this checklist before every prompt to ensure you are maintaining the Pilot in Command status and anchoring your logic to the SSoT.

🛡️ The 4-Point Audit

P — Persona

Did you assign a specific clinical or academic role?
Example: "Act as a clinical exercise physiologist specializing in geriatrics."

T — Task

Is your request specific and actionable?
Example: "Audit the safety of a high-load leg press for a client with Osteoporosis."

C — Context

Did you anchor the prompt to the SSoT?
Example: "Based exclusively on the ACSM 12th Ed guidelines for bone health."

F — Format

How do you want the "Solve" delivered?
Example: "Provide the results in the Mission Loop format: Pattern, Rule, Solve."

🚀 The "Master" Prompt Example

When all four elements are combined, your "Flight Plan" looks like this:

"Act as a Mastery Tutor [P]. Explain the cardiovascular contraindications for a client with Stage 2 Hypertension [T] using the PAGA 2nd Ed standards [C]. Use a 3-step bulleted list for the final Solve [F]."
