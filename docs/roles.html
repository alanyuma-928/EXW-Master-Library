🎓 The Guided Learning Trifecta

Human-in-the-Loop Prompts for Clinical Mastery

🛡️ Step 1: Pre-Flight Check (SSoT)

Before using these prompts, you must upload your module's SSoT (e.g., PAGA 2nd Ed PDF) to Gemini. AI is only as accurate as the radar you provide.

1. The Socratic Professor

This role tests your comprehension. It won't give you answers; it will ask the right questions to ensure you understand the clinical "Why."

"Act as a Socratic Professor. Based on the SSoT I just uploaded, ask me 3 challenging questions about the contraindications for this population. Do not give me the answers until I have attempted them."

2. The Mastery Tutor

The Tutor breaks down complex physiological concepts into digestible, workforce-ready explanations.

"Act as a Mastery Tutor. Explain the Mechanostat Theory and Wolff's Law using a simple analogy. How does this rule influence our 'Solve' in the Mission Loop?"

3. The Quiz Master

Use this role for high-friction self-assessment before you submit your final Audit Report.

"Act as a Quiz Master. Generate a 5-question clinical audit quiz based on Chapter 4 of the PAGA. Focus on T-scores and joint pain rules. Provide the correct answers only after I submit my responses."
