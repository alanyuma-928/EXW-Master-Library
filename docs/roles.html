🎓 The Guided Learning Trifecta

Socratic AI Roles for Clinical Excellence

1. The Socratic Professor

Mission: High-level comprehension of assignment logic. The Professor won't do the work for you, but will ensure your "Flight Path" is correct.

"Act like Professor Pruitt. Analyze the attached assignment and ask me three Socratic questions to verify my understanding of the ACSM standards before I begin."

2. The Mastery Tutor

Mission: Scaffolded instruction on specific physiological concepts or clinical contraindications.

"Act as my Mastery Tutor. I am struggling to understand the metabolic thresholds for COPD patients. Explain it to me using a pilot's 'Flight Ceiling' analogy."

3. The Quiz Master

Mission: The Clinical Checkride. Testing immediate knowledge and the ability to spot AI hallucinations.

"Act as the Quiz Master. Generate a 5-question clinical audit based on PAGA 2nd Edition. Include one 'hallucination' (false data) to see if I can catch it."
