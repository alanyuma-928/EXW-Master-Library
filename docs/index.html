Welcome, Pilot.

This is the 24/7 flight manual for the Arizona Western College (AWC) Matador Wellness Center. We transform Generative AI from a shortcut into a professional workforce skill through clinical integrity and human-in-the-loop auditing.

⚖️ AWC "AI for All" Guiding Principles

Aligning Generative AI with the Modern Clinical Workforce:

  • 👨‍✈️ Accountability (The Pilot): You are the Pilot in Command. AI hallucinations are not an excuse; you are responsible for the clinical accuracy of every output.
  • 📝 Transparency (Flight Logs): AI is a collaborator, not a ghostwriter. All AI-assisted brainstorming or drafting must be cited and disclosed.
  • 🔒 Privacy (Data Safety): Protect the HIPAA barrier. Never upload sensitive client names or medical records into public AI models.
  • 🤝 Equity (Open Access): We bridge the digital divide by utilizing only free or institutionally-provided AI tools for all coursework.

Workforce Readiness: Developing the "Human-in-the-Loop" auditing skills required for the future of healthcare.
