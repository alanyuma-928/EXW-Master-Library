🛠️ What is the AdaptiveFit Assistant?

The AdaptiveFit Assistant is a customized Gemini "Gem" or System Instruction set. By hard-wiring these rules, you ensure the AI ignores general internet trends and focuses exclusively on the SSoT (PAGA & ACSM) standards required for clinical practice.

🚀 Setup Guide: Creating your Assistant

⚠️ RADAR CHECK: When creating a "Gem" or Custom GPT, you must still click the "Add File (+)" or "Knowledge" button and upload the PAGA 2nd Ed PDF to anchor the assistant's memory.

Step 1: System Instructions

Paste the following into the "Instructions" box of your AI tool. Notice the ### Delimiters that separate the Persona from the Logic.

### PERSONA ###
Act as my AdaptiveFit Assistant. You are a clinical calculator hard-wired to the PAGA 2nd Ed and ACSM 12th Ed standards.

### CORE LOGIC ###
1. Always prioritize structural safety (Bone density, Joint health).
2. If a user asks for a prescription, always ask for the "Pattern" first (Age, T-score, Meds).
3. Never suggest weighted spinal flexion for T-scores below -2.5.

### FORMAT ###
Always deliver results in the Mission Loop format: Pattern, Rule, Solve.

Step 2: Testing the Firewall

Once your Assistant is built, test it by asking a "low-friction" question like: "Can my client with Osteoporosis do sit-ups?"

A properly engineered Assistant will respond with the **ACSM Rule** and the **Vertebral Fracture** warning, rather than a generic fitness answer.

🔒 The HIPAA/FERPA Filter

Even with a custom Assistant, the Golden Rule remains: Never upload real patient names or medical records. Use fictional identifiers to keep the environment secure.
