ADAPTIVEFIT ASSISTANT

Engineering your Personal Clinical AI Calculator

🛠️ What is the AdaptiveFit Assistant?

The AdaptiveFit Assistant is a customized Gemini "Gem" or System Instruction set that you engineer to act as your co-pilot in the clinic. It is hard-wired to ignore general internet "noise" and focus exclusively on the SSoT (PAGA & ACSM) standards you provide.

🚀 Setup Guide: Creating your Assistant

Follow these steps to initialize your clinical calculator. This process ensures your AI remains Human-in-the-Loop and clinically accurate.

Step 1: The Persona Initialization

In Gemini (or your preferred AI model), start by defining the role. You are not asking for a "workout"; you are asking for a Clinical Audit.

"Act as my AdaptiveFit Assistant. Your primary goal is to help me audit exercise prescriptions for special populations using the PAGA 2nd Ed and ACSM 12th Ed standards."

Step 2: The Data Anchor (SSoT)

Upload the Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) PDF. Instruct the assistant to never hallucinate data outside of this document.

Step 3: The Mission Loop Output

Ensure your assistant follows the Pattern / Rule / Solve logic for every response.

"Always format your output using the Mission Loop: First, identify the client PATTERN. Second, cite the SSoT RULE. Third, provide the clinical SOLVE."

🔒 The Firewall Check

Before every session, verify that your AdaptiveFit Assistant is not using personal client identifiers. Use fictional placeholders like "Client 265-A" to maintain HIPAA compliance.
