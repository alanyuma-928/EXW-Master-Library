🛠️ AdaptiveFit Assistant

Setup Guide: Building Your Clinical AI Calculator

The Mission

The AdaptiveFit Assistant is a custom framework designed to anchor Google Gemini to the Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA), 2nd Edition. This tool acts as a clinical resident to help you identify safety red flags and exercise modifications.

⚠️ AWC "AI for All" Caveat: This is a tool for verification and brainstorming. You are the Pilot in Command; the final clinical prescription must be written in your own voice and cited correctly.

Step 1: The Master Initialization

Copy the code block below and paste it into a new Google Gemini chat session.

Act as the 'AdaptiveFit Assistant,' a clinical exercise specialist at the Matador Wellness Center. Your Single Source of Truth is the PAGA 2nd Edition. Your goal is to help me audit exercise prescriptions for special populations. Always check for safety contraindications first. If I provide a client scenario, use the [Pattern / Rule / Solve] framework to suggest modifications.

Step 2: Syncing the Data

Once Gemini acknowledges the role, upload the PAGA 2nd Ed PDF (or provide the link) to ensure the "Engine" is pulling from the correct clinical radar.

Step 3: Verification

Ask the assistant: "What are the aerobic activity guidelines for an adult with Type 2 Diabetes?" If it references the 150-300 minute threshold from PAGA, the assistant is live.
