# 🎓 The Guided Learning Trifecta
In the EXW residency, we utilize Google Gemini as a dynamic learning partner. Below are the three primary role-playing frameworks used to ensure clinical mastery.

---

## 1. The Socratic Professor (Comprehension)
**Goal:** Ensure deep understanding of complex assignments before execution.
> **The Master Prompt:** > "Act like Professor Pruitt and engage in a dialogue with me about the attached assignment. Read the assignment and ask me questions to check my comprehension..."

---

## 2. The Mastery Tutor (Instruction)
**Goal:** Personalized, scaffolded coaching on specific clinical concepts.
> **The Master Prompt:** > "My name is [your name], and I would like you to act as my personal tutor and teach me about subject X..."

---

## 3. The Quiz Master (Simulation)
**Goal:** Live "Clinical Checkrides" to test immediate mastery and audit logic.
> **The Master Prompt:** > "Act as a quiz master. Create a 5-question multiple-choice quiz to gauge my immediate mastery of [TOPIC/SOURCE]..."
