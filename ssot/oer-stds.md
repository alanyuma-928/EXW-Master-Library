# OERizona Open Educational Resources (OER) Standards
# Category: SSoT / Licensing / Shareability

## 1. Licensing & Accessibility
- Licensing: CC BY, CC BY-NC, CC BY-SA, CC BY-NC-SA, or Public Domain. [Requirement]
- Accessibility: Meets WCAG 2.0 Level AA (Note: Coach Alan uses 9:1 Contrast). [Requirement]

## 2. Quality & Clarity
- Currency: Content is up-to-date and reflects recent scholarship. [1-5 Scale]
- Clarity: No grammatical errors; understandable without augmentation. [1-5 Scale]

## 3. Source & Attribution
- Attributions: Proper citing of shared or adapted openly licensed content. [Y/N]
- Copyright: Links out to all copyrighted content; follows Educational Fair Use. [Y/N]

## 4. Technical & Modularity
- Modularity: Easily divisible into smaller, reorganizable sections. [Y/N]
- Technical Quality: High resolution and mobile-ready design. [Y/N]
