# ADA Standards for Accessible Design (SSoT)
# Institutional Authority: DOJ 2010 Standards

## 🏗️ Core Architectural Standards
- Doorway Clearance: Minimum 32 inches (815 mm) clear width. [2.1]
- Path of Travel: Minimum 36 inches wide; firm, stable, and slip-resistant. [2.1/2.4]
- Machine Access: 30 x 48 inches clear floor space for transfer at one of each machine type. [2.2]
- Turning Space: 60-inch diameter circle required at the end of equipment rows. [2.4]

## 🛡️ The Clinical Intersection
- Note: Physical access does not equal thermal safety. 
- Yuma Protocol: Even ADA-compliant paths must be audited for unshaded asphalt/thermal stress. [3.0]

## 🏁 Compliance Guardrail
- Status: Any failure of these metrics constitutes a Federal Civil Rights Violation. 
- Auditor Action: "Grounded" status in Pilot's Flight Log. [5.0]
