# ACSM Clinical Redlines Matrix (SSoT)
# Source: ACSM 12th Ed | Effective July 10, 2025

## 🛑 The Redline Rule
Safety contraindications for Condition B ALWAYS take precedence over prescription benefits for Condition A.

## Multi-morbidity Matrix
| Condition Pair | The Collision | Resolution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| HTN + Osteoporosis | Heavy Loading vs. Valsalva Risk | Open-mouth breathing; moderate-load. |
| T2DM + Neuropathy | HIIT Clearance vs. Blunted HR | Use RPE (0-10) instead of %HRR. |

## Yuma Regional Context: Thermal Safety
- Beta-Blockers/Diuretics: Accelerated dehydration and heat exhaustion risk.
- Local Arbiter: Clinical judgment takes precedence to preserve hemodynamic stability.
