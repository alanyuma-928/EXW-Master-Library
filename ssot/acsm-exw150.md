# ACSM's Guidelines for Exercise Testing & Prescription (12th Ed)
## Secondary SSoT for EXW150: Foundations of Nutrition

This document defines the clinical screening rules applicable to nutritional assessment and exercise referral.

### 01. Pre-Participation Screening

**Activity Status**:
* **Active**: 30 min+ moderate activity, 3+ days/week, for last 3 months.
* **Sedentary**: Does not meet active criteria.

**Key Clinical Signs (The Redlines)**:
*   Chest pain/discomfort suggestive of ischemia.
*   Dyspnea (shortness of breath) at rest or mild exertion.
*   Syncope/Dizziness.
*   Orthopnea or Paroxysmal Nocturnal Dyspnea.
*   Ankle Edema.
*   Palpitations/Tachycardia.
*   Intermittent Claudication.
*   Unusual fatigue with usual activities.

### 02. Medical Clearance Rules

*   **Sedentary + Asymptomatic + No Disease**: No clearance needed for light/moderate activity.
*   **Sedentary + Known Disease or Symptoms**: Clearance IS recommended.
*   **Active + Asymptomatic**: Continue current intensity. Clearance needed for vigorous if disease is known.
*   **Active + Any Symptoms**: STOP exercise; seek medical clearance.
