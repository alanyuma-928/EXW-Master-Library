# ACSM's Guidelines for Exercise Testing & Prescription (12th Ed)
## Secondary SSoT for EXW101: Foundations of Wellness

This document defines the "Rules" for pre-participation screening and medical clearance safety.

### 01. The Pre-Participation Screening Algorithm

**STEP 1: Current Activity Status**
* **Active**: Performing planned, structured physical activity at least 30 min at moderate intensity on at least 3 days/week for at least the last 3 months.
* **Sedentary**: Not meeting the "Active" threshold.

**STEP 2: Known Disease**
* **Cardiovascular Disease (CVD)**: Cardiac, peripheral vascular, or cerebrovascular disease.
* **Metabolic Disease**: Type 1 or Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus.
* **Renal Disease**: Chronic kidney disease.

### 02. Pre-Participation & Medical Clearance Safety Rules

###
**Major Signs or Symptoms (The Redlines)**
Presence of any of the following triggers mandatory medical clearance:
1. **Pain/Discomfort**: In the chest, neck, jaw, or arms suggestive of myocardial ischemia.
2. **Dyspnea**: Shortness of breath at rest or with mild exertion.
3. **Syncope**: Fainting or dizziness, especially during exercise.
4. **Orthopnea**: Dyspnea when lying flat.
5. **Ankle Edema**: Swelling in the lower extremities.
6. **Palpitations/Tachycardia**: Forcible or irregular heartbeat.
7. **Intermittent Claudication**: Pain in the lower limbs due to inadequate blood supply.
8. **Known Heart Murmur**.
9. **Unusual Fatigue** with usual activities.
###

###
**Medical Clearance Logic**
* **Sedentary + No Disease + No Symptoms**: Medical clearance NOT necessary. Start light-to-moderate.
* **Sedentary + Known Disease + Asymptomatic**: Medical clearance IS recommended.
* **Sedentary + Any Symptoms**: Medical clearance IS recommended.
* **Active + No Disease + No Symptoms**: Medical clearance NOT necessary. Continue current.
* **Active + Known Disease + Asymptomatic**: Medical clearance NOT necessary for moderate intensity; IS recommended for vigorous intensity.
* **Active + Any Symptoms**: DISCONTINUE exercise and seek medical clearance.
