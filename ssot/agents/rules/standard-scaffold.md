# **Standard EXW Lesson Scaffold Model (Modules 1–16)**

This model defines the "Gold Standard" structural integrity for all courses in the **Wellness & Physical Education Department**. Every Module must contain exactly these nine components in this numerical order to ensure institutional agility, high-fidelity workforce training, and maximum grading efficiency via Canvas SpeedGrader.

## **📂 The 00–08 Scaffold Sequence**

### **00-overview.html | The Launchpad**

* **Purpose:** State the **Operational Objective** and provide the weekly mission checklist.  
* **Theme:** Creme (9:1 High Contrast).  
* **Key Element:** Direct mapping of the 00–08 sequence for the week.

### **01-narrative.html | The Context**

* **Purpose:** The "Narrator" voice provides professional context for the weekly topic.  
* **SSoT:** Must cite the course-specific authority (PAGA 2018, DGA 2025-2030, or ACSM 12th Ed).  
* **Framework:** Establishes the **Pattern** stage of the Mission Loop.

### **02-case-study.html | The Pattern**

* **Purpose:** Provide raw, clinically relevant data or population profiles.  
* **Requirement:** All case data, client profiles, or medical metrics must be wrapped in **\#\#\# triple-hashes \#\#\#** to ensure mastery of delimiter protocol and data integrity.

### **03-rules.html | The Authority**

* **Purpose:** Explicitly list the SSoT Rules (federal or clinical benchmarks) applicable to the week.  
* **Action:** Includes a direct high-contrast button to download the SSoT PDF.

### **04-lab-part-a.html | AI Guided Audit (Part A)**

* **AI Persona:** **Socratic Tutor**  
* **Protocol:** Instructions must include the **"Add File (+)"** command to ground the Gemini interaction in the official SSoT PDF.  
* **Interaction:** Students wrap data from File 02 in **\#\#\# triple-hashes \#\#\#** and ask the Socratic Tutor to audit the pattern against the attached Rules.  
* **Note:** This is the setup phase for the File 05 "Solve."

### **05-lab-part-b.html | Practical Solve (Part B)**

* **Purpose:** The clinical "Solve." Real-world application and manual execution.  
* **Submission Type:** **Text Only** (No attachments permitted).  
* **SpeedGrader Optimization (Top-Down Layout):**  
  1. **Presence Defense** (Einstein Protocol: 2-3 sentences of human logic justifying the result).  
  2. **The Solve** (The actual clinical result, math, or prescription).  
  3. **AI Transcript** (Full copy/paste of the Socratic Tutor conversation from File 04).  
* **Mastery Scale:** Complete/Incomplete using the **10/7/0 SEAL scale** (Exemplary/Competent/Recalibrate).  
* **Exemplar:** Must include a high-fidelity **10/10 Student Exemplar**.

### **06-discussion.html | Pilots' Lounge (Mission Loop)**

* **Purpose:** The professional network briefing and peer audit.  
* **Rhythm:** Thursday Initial Post (Initial Briefing) | Sunday Peer Responses (Peer Audit).  
* **Einstein Protocol:** Initial briefings must include a clinical rationale justifying the findings.  
* **\#\#\# Protocol:** All raw clinical data in posts must remain wrapped in delimiters.  
* **Mastery Scale:** Complete/Incomplete using the **10/7/0 SEAL scale**.  
* **Exemplar:** Must include a high-fidelity **10/10 Student Exemplar**.

### **07-readiness-gate.html | Flight Manual Check**

* **AI Persona:** **Quiz Master**  
* **Purpose:** The weekly competency check and technical protocol gate.  
* **Submission Type:** **Text Only**. Students copy/paste the full Quiz Master transcript into the submission box.  
* **Mastery Scale:** Complete/Incomplete using the **10/7/0 SEAL scale**.

### **08-summary.html | Workplace Intelligence**

* **AI Persona:** **Master Tutor**  
* **Purpose:** Mission recap and a "Workplace Intelligence" briefing.  
* **Intelligence:** The Master Tutor connects the weekly lesson to a career skill (e.g., HIPAA, Privacy, Data Integrity).  
* **Submission Type:** **Text Only**. Students copy/paste the Master Tutor briefing transcript into the submission box for audit.

## **📜 Global Institutional Rules**

1. **Text-Only Mandate:** All graded assignments are **Text Only**. Attachments (Word/PDF) are strictly prohibited to maximize SpeedGrader utility and mobile-first accessibility.  
2. **SEAL Mastery Scale:** We utilize the **10/7/0 scale** (Exemplary/Competent/Recalibrate). All assignments are marked as **Complete/Incomplete** based on these thresholds.  
3. **The Einstein Protocol:** All AI-assisted assignments require a written **Presence Defense** at the TOP of the text submission, followed by the raw **AI Conversation Transcript**. This proves the human "Head" exercised clinical judgment over the AI's "Hooves."  
4. **The Delimiter Protocol:** Students must wrap all raw clinical data, medical metrics, or client profiles in **\#\#\# triple-hashes \#\#\#** in all submissions.  
5. **Add File (+) Protocol:** All AI prompts must explicitly instruct students to upload the course SSoT PDF to ground the AI's logic.  
6. **SpeedGrader Optimization:** Instructions must enforce a standardized "Top-Down" text layout so instructors can audit cognitive presence in under 30 seconds.  
7. **High-Fidelity Exemplars:** Every graded component must provide a **10/10 Student Exemplar** to clarify performance expectations.
