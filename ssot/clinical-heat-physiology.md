# **Local Authority: Yuma Thermal Safety Gate**

## **Supplemental SSoT for Environmental Risk Management**

This document serves as the mandatory safety protocol for all physical activity prescriptions and onsite labs conducted at Arizona Western College (Yuma Campus).

### **01\. THE THERMAL REDLINE**

In the Yuma environment, environmental heat stress can override clinical clearances.

* **The Redline:** All outdoor physical activity missions are **TERMINATED** if the ambient temperature reaches **110°F (43.3°C)** or the **WBGT (Wet Bulb Globe Temperature)** exceeds **90°F (32.2°C)**.  
* **The Clinical Solve:** If the Redline is met, the practitioner must move the mission to a climate-controlled environment (e.g., GY 116\) or reschedule to a "Low-Stress Window" (before 08:00 AM).

### **02\. WBGT RISK CATEGORIES (The Rules)**

Practitioners must audit the current WBGT before any outdoor session:

| WBGT Index | Risk Level | Operational Rule |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **\< 82.0°F** | **Green** | Normal operations; maintain standard hydration. |
| **82.1 – 86.0°F** | **Yellow** | **Caution:** Increase rest breaks; 10 mins rest per 45 mins work. |
| **86.1 – 89.9°F** | **Red** | **High Risk:** 20 mins rest per 40 mins work. Unacclimatized individuals must stop. |
| **\> 90.0°F** | **Black** | **GATE CLOSED:** No outdoor activity. Move to indoor logic. |

### **03\. THE HYDRATION PROTOCOL**

For audits involving Sarah (150), Alex (101), or Mr. Jackson (245/265) in a Yuma context:

* **The 1:1 Rule:** For every 1 hour of activity in Yellow/Red zones, the client must consume at least **1 Liter** of fluid.  
* **The Electrolyte Gate:** Activity exceeding 60 minutes in thermal stress requires sodium replacement (per DGA/ACSM standards) to prevent hyponatremia.

### **04\. CLINICAL SIGNS OF THERMAL FAILURE (Stop Mission)**

The mission is immediately terminated if any "Pattern" of heat illness is identified:

1. **Heat Exhaustion:** Heavy sweating, rapid pulse, dizziness, fatigue.  
2. **Heat Stroke (Emergency):** Altered mental status, core temp \> 104°F, lack of sweating.
