- 1.1 Home Page: Visual, navigation-ready, and brief instructions. [Foundational]
- 1.3 Standards/Outcomes: Measurable and cited with year adopted. [Foundational]

## 2. Course Structure
- 2.3 Consistent Formatting: predictable directions and technical support areas. [★]
- 2.7 Intro/Summary: Overview to activate knowledge; Summary to close. [★★]

- 7.3 Contrast: 9:1 minimum threshold (Exceeds WCAG 2.1). [★]

- 7.7 Media: Audio requires transcripts; Video requires captions. [★]
