Welcome to the **Matador Wellness Center Clinical AI Residency**. You are not just a student in this course; you are an Architect and a Pilot.

The Analogy: You are the Pilot (Judgment); AI is the Engine (Processing). A pilot doesn't let the engine fly the plane alone, and an architect doesn't let the calculator design the bridge. You will use AI to enhance your clinical reach, but you remain the final authority on safety and accuracy.

What to Expect

  • Clinical Scaffolding: We move through 5 distinct phases each week, from initial briefing to final inspection.
  • AI-Human Hybridization: You will learn to engineer prompts that drive safe, evidence-based results.
  • Workforce Ready: Everything we do mirrors the real-world documentation and audit standards of modern healthcare.
