EXW 245: Guidelines for Exercise Testing & Prescription

🏗️ Navigating the Evidence

In this residency, precision is mandatory. For all clinical protocols and metabolic math, your primary authorities are:

Primary: ACSM 12th Ed.

ACSM's Guidelines for Exercise Testing and Prescription (2025)

Focus: Clinical protocols, risk stratification, and metabolic equations.

Secondary: PAGA 2018

Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans

Focus: Public health standards and general population safety.

The Clinical Audit Requirement

AI "Engines" frequently struggle with metabolic math. Every assignment in EXW 245 requires:

  1. The Engine Output: Raw AI-generated calculations or prescriptions.
  2. The 150-Word Clinical Audit: A manual verification of the math using the ACSM metabolic equations.

⚠️ Mandatory Standard:

Submissions containing math errors or lacking an audit against ACSM 12th Edition will receive a **0** (Recalibrate).
