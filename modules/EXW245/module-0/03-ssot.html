✅ P-T-C-F Engine Checklist

Before you "Launch" your prompt, verify it against these four flight controls:

  • 🟢 Persona: Did you tell the AI who to be? (e.g., "Act as a Physiologist").
  • 🔵 Task: Is your command specific? (e.g., "Calculate VO2 cost").
  • 🟠 Context: Did you include the clinical environment? (e.g., "Cycle Ergometry").
  • 🟣 Format: Did you request a specific layout? (e.g., "Show all math steps").

🔗 Advanced Maneuver: Prompt Chaining

Do not ask the AI to do everything at once. Use Prompt Chaining to ensure clinical safety:

  1. Step 1: Ask the AI to define the ACSM math standards for your calculation.
  2. Step 2: Ask the AI to calculate the metabolic cost using the standards it just defined.
  3. Step 3: Ask the AI to perform a "Self-Audit" for calculation errors.

🧱 The Structural Delimiter

Use delimiters to keep the AI "Engine" organized. When providing large datasets or specific guidelines, wrap them in clear markers:

### BEGIN SOURCE OF TRUTH ###
[Insert ACSM Metabolic Text Here]
### END SOURCE OF TRUTH ###

The Clinical Audit Requirement

AI "Engines" frequently struggle with metabolic math. Every assignment in EXW 245 requires:

  1. The Engine Output: Raw AI-generated calculations or prescriptions.
  2. The 150-Word Clinical Audit: A manual verification of the math using the ACSM metabolic equations.
