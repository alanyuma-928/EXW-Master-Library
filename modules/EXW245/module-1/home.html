🎯 Mission Objectives

By the end of this module, Clinical AI Residents will be able to:

  • Differentiate between medical clearance requirements for sedentary vs. active individuals per ACSM 12th Ed.
  • Identify the "Big Three" clinical signs and symptoms that trigger immediate physician referral.
  • Architecture a Risk Stratification prompt using the AdaptiveFit assistant to filter patient intake forms.
  • Audit an AI-generated health history summary for accuracy against the ACSM contraindications list.

📝 Technical Briefing

Welcome to EXW 245. This module focus on the Safety Gate. Before we move a patient, we must clear them. We use the ACSM 12th Edition (Effective July 10, 2025) as our primary clinical filter. In this course, a failure in pre-screening is a structural failure of clinical care.

Your AdaptiveFit Assistant must be "Wired" with the ACSM 12th Ed pre-screening algorithm before attempting Lab 1.
