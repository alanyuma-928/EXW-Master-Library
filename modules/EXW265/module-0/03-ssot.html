In this residency, we specialize in clinical safety for complex cases. For all adaptive prescriptions, your primary authority is:

Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA)

2nd Edition (2018)

Primary Focus: Chapters 5 & 6 (Chronic Conditions and Older Adults)

🔗 Open Google Gemini

✅ P-T-C-F Engine Checklist

Before you "Launch" your prompt, verify it against these four flight controls:

  • 🟢 Persona: Did you tell the AI who to be? (e.g., "Act as a Physiologist").
  • 🔵 Task: Is your command specific? (e.g., "Draft an adaptive plan").
  • 🟠 Context: Did you include the specific population? (e.g., "Type 2 Diabetes").
  • 🟣 Format: Did you request a specific layout? (e.g., "Safety table").

🔗 Advanced Maneuver: Prompt Chaining

Do not ask the AI to do everything at once. Use Prompt Chaining to ensure clinical safety:

  1. Step 1: Ask the AI to define the PAGA Chapter 6 standards for your population.
  2. Step 2: Ask the AI to draft the adaptive plan based on the standards it just defined.
  3. Step 3: Ask the AI to audit its own plan for safety hallucinations.

🧱 The Structural Delimiter

Use delimiters to keep the AI "Engine" organized. When providing large datasets or specific guidelines, wrap them in clear markers:

### BEGIN SOURCE OF TRUTH ###
[Insert PAGA Chapter 6 Text Here]
### END SOURCE OF TRUTH ###

🧪 Practice Challenge

Try your first "Centaur" maneuver. Copy the following prompt into Gemini and observe the output:

"Act as a Clinical Exercise Architect. Using PAGA 2018 Chapter 6, identify two safety 'gates' or contraindications for an older adult with hypertension exercising in a high-heat environment like Yuma, AZ."

The Clinical Audit Requirement

Working with special populations requires high-fidelity judgment. Every assignment in EXW 265 requires:

  1. The Engine Output: Raw AI-generated prescriptions for a specific chronic condition.
  2. The 150-Word Clinical Audit: A critical analysis where you hunt for "Safety Hallucinations" (e.g., ignoring contraindications) using PAGA Chapter 6.
