9.2: Clinical Arthritis Management

⚖️ The Two-Hour Pain Rule

In clinical exercise prescription for arthritis, we must distinguish between "good" structural stress and "bad" inflammatory triggers. The primary clinical filter we use is the Two-Hour Pain Rule.

🛡️ The Golden Rule

If joint pain is higher two hours after exercising than it was before starting, the intensity or duration of the next session must be reduced.

📋 Exercise Modifications by Arthritis Type

Table: Exercise Modifications for Osteoarthritis vs. Rheumatoid Arthritis
Clinical Variable Osteoarthritis (OA) Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA)
Primary Focus Joint alignment & localized strength. Systemic inflammation management.
Loading Strategy Progressive loading as tolerated. Strict avoidance of loading during "flares."
