12.0: Mission Briefing

Final Project Launch: Clinical Lab Simulation

The Capstone Sortie

Welcome to the final mission of the Clinical AI Residency. In Module 12, we transition from auditing individual prescriptions to managing high-fidelity, multi-morbidity patient simulations. This is where your mastery of the ACSM 12th Ed, PAGA 2nd Ed, and the Centaur Protocol converges.

🎯 Learning Objectives (LO 2.1, 2.4)

  • Synthesize conflicting clinical data into a unified, safe exercise prescription.
  • Audit AI-generated metabolic math for structural and physiological errors.
  • Defend clinical decisions in a simulated environment using localized Yuma safety protocols.

The Clinical Lab Simulation is designed to test your ability to maintain Structural Integrity under pressure. You will be provided with complex data sets where thermal stress, medication interactions, and conflicting pathology create high-risk "Interference Patterns."

"In the simulation lab, there are no 'average' patients. There are only pilots counting on your ability to enforce the Redline." — Coach Alan.
