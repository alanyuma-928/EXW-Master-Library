Clinical Audit: Osteoporosis Management

🏁 The Mastery Standard (10/7/0)

  • 10 Points (Mastery): All 3 parts submitted; Audit cites specific PAGA benchmarks; Audio Podcast is defensible.
  • 7 Points (Developing): Missing 1 part or Audit is generic/under 150 words.
  • 0 Points (Incomplete): Missing SSoT citations or failure to submit Canvas Audio Podcast.

🎯 Mission Task

Objective (LO 2.2): Architecture a weight-bearing, low-impact exercise routine for a 68-year-old female client with a T-score of -2.8 (Osteoporosis).

Constraint: The routine must focus on axial loading while minimizing vertebral fracture risk (no high-impact jumping or explosive spinal flexion).

📤 Submission Requirements

Part 1 (The Engine): Submit your P-T-C-F Prompt used to generate the initial routine.

Part 2 (The Audit): Submit a 150-word Clinical Audit. You must explicitly critique the AI's output against PAGA 2018 Chapter 6 standards for bone health.

Part 3 (The Presence-Check): Submit a 2-minute Canvas Audio Podcast defending your P-T-C-F Clinical Prescription. You must justify why your selected exercises are clinically superior to non-weight-bearing alternatives for this specific patient. Include the AI-generated transcript of your audio below the recording.

AI Disclosure: This assignment was structured using Google Gemini. Residents must audit all clinical claims against the Source of Truth.
