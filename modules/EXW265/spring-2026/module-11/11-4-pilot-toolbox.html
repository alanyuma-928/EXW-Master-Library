💡 Pro-Tip: The "Audit First" Rule

Before applying any AI-generated exercise intensity, check the patient's medication list. If you see Atenolol or Metoprolol, immediately pivot from %HRR to RPE (6-20 Scale). AI often misses this critical safety switch.
