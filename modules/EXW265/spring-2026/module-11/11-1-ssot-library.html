Clinical Priority: ACSM 12th Edition

In clinical exercise physiology, we rarely encounter a "single-diagnosis" patient. The ACSM 12th Edition (Effective July 10, 2025) emphasizes the prioritization of clinical redlines when multiple chronic conditions coexist.

🛑 The Redline Rule

If the prescription for Condition A (e.g., Osteoporosis/Heavy Loading) contradicts the safety contraindications for Condition B (e.g., Stage 2 Hypertension/Valsalva Risk), the safety contraindication ALWAYS takes precedence.

The Multi-morbidity Matrix

Use the following matrix to identify interference patterns in your clinical synthesis.
