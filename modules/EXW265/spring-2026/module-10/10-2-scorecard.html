10.2: Thermal Integrity Scorecard

Quantitative Clinical Architecture

Scoring the Environment

A facility might pass an ADA inspection but fail a Thermal Audit. Use this scorecard to quantify the risk level for heat-sensitive clients (MS, Cardiac, Elderly) in the Yuma summer.

Architectural Feature Clinical Metric Points
Shaded ADA Path Continuous canopy/shadow from parking to door. +3
Cooling Vestibule Functional air-lock entry (Interior staging area). +3
Hydration Radius Water station within 15ft of entrance. +2
Solar Gain Control UV/Thermal tinting on all South/West windows. +2
Maximum Score: 10/10

📡 GO / NO-GO STATUS

  • 🌟 8-10 Points: Clear for Takeoff. Safe for all clinical populations.
  • ⚠️ 7 Points: Restricted Sortie. Requires high-frequency hydration breaks.
  • 🛑 0-6 Points: Grounded. Structural failure. Facility is unsafe for heat-sensitive clients.
