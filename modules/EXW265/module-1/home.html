Primary Source of Truth (SSoT)
Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA), 2nd Edition (2018)

🎯 Mission Objectives

By the end of this module, Clinical AI Residents will be able to:

  • Categorize "Special Populations" according to PAGA clinical chapters (Older Adults, Pregnancy, Chronic Conditions).
  • Evaluate the "Risk vs. Reward" ratio of physical activity for individuals with multi-morbidities.
  • Architecture a Tier 1 prompt to identify regional health disparities and barriers in the Yuma/Imperial Valley area.
  • Conduct a baseline Clinical Audit of AI-generated exercise safety advice against PAGA 2018 Chapter 6 standards.

📝 Technical Briefing

Welcome to EXW 265. In this course, we move beyond the "average healthy adult." We focus on those whom the fitness industry often overlooks. We use the PAGA 2nd Edition (2018) as our primary clinical filter to ensure that exercise is both safe and effective for individuals with unique health challenges.

Your AdaptiveFit Assistant must be "Primed" with the full PAGA 2018 PDF before you begin your first Clinical Audit.
