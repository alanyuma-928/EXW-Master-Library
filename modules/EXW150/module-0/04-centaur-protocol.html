🏗️ Engineering the Engine: P-T-C-F

In this residency, we use the Centaur Protocol. You are the "Pilot" who steers the AI "Engine" to clinical success. To get safe, high-quality results, we use the P-T-C-F Prompting Method.

Persona

"Act as a Clinical Exercise Physiologist."

Task

"Draft a 3-day aerobic plan for a senior."

Context

"Yuma, AZ climate; PAGA 2018 standards."

Format

"Markdown table with intensity zones."

Why P-T-C-F?

Generic prompts yield generic (and often dangerous) advice. By defining the **Persona** and **Context**, you "lock" the AI into a clinical mindset, making your **Audit** phase much more effective.

🤝 The Architect's Rule

Never copy-paste an AI response without viewing it through the P-T-C-F lens. If the AI ignores your context (like Yuma's heat), it is your job as the Pilot to catch the error.
