🏗️ Navigating the Evidence

In this residency, we rely on the Single Source of Truth (SSoT). For all nutritional prescriptions and audits, your primary authorities are:

Primary: Dietary Guidelines

Dietary Guidelines for Americans 2025-2030

Focus: Healthy eating patterns and nutrient density across the lifespan.

Secondary: PAGA 2018

Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans

Focus: The intersection of caloric expenditure and physical health.

The Clinical Audit Requirement

You must verify all AI-generated meal plans or nutritional claims against these standards. Every submission requires:

  1. The Engine Output: Raw AI data.
  2. The 150-Word Clinical Audit: Evidence-based critique citing specific chapters from the 2025-2030 Guidelines.

⚠️ Mandatory Standard:

Submissions without a Clinical Audit citing the SSoT will receive a **0** (Recalibrate).
