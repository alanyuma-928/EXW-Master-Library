🏗️ Navigating the Evidence

In this residency, we rely on the Single Source of Truth (SSoT). For all nutritional prescriptions and audits, your primary authorities are:

Primary: Dietary Guidelines

Dietary Guidelines for Americans 2025-2030

Focus: Healthy eating patterns and nutrient density across the lifespan.

Secondary: PAGA 2018

Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans

Focus: The intersection of caloric expenditure and physical health.

✅ P-T-C-F Engine Checklist

Before you "Launch" your prompt, verify it against these four flight controls:

  • 🟢 Persona: Did you tell the AI who to be? (e.g., "Act as a Dietitian").
  • 🔵 Task: Is your command specific? (e.g., "Analyze this menu").
  • 🟠 Context: Did you include the clinical environment? (e.g., "Yuma heat").
  • 🟣 Format: Did you request a specific layout? (e.g., "A numbered list").

🔗 Advanced Maneuver: Prompt Chaining

Do not ask the AI to do everything at once. Use Prompt Chaining to ensure clinical safety:

  1. Step 1: Ask the AI to define the PAGA/Dietary standards for your population.
  2. Step 2: Ask the AI to draft the plan based on the standards it just defined.
  3. Step 3: Ask the AI to audit its own plan for safety hallucinations.

🧱 The Structural Delimiter

Use delimiters to keep the AI "Engine" organized. When providing large datasets or specific guidelines, wrap them in clear markers:

### BEGIN SOURCE OF TRUTH ###
[Insert Dietary Guidelines Text Here]
### END SOURCE OF TRUTH ###

🧪 Practice Challenge

Try your first "Centaur" maneuver. Copy the following prompt into Gemini and observe the output:

"Act as a Clinical Nutrition Architect. Using the 2025-2030 Dietary Guidelines, identify three nutrient-dense breakfast options for a student in Yuma, AZ. Provide the answer in a professional bulleted list."

The Clinical Audit Requirement

You must verify all AI-generated meal plans or nutritional claims against these standards. Every submission requires:

  1. The Engine Output: Raw AI data.
  2. The 150-Word Clinical Audit: Evidence-based critique citing specific chapters from the 2025-2030 Guidelines.
