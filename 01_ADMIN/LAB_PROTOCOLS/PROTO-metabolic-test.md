### Overview
This module explores the intersection of thermal regulation and metabolic health. As specified in **PAGA (2018)**, individuals with higher adiposity may face unique challenges in heat dissipation during exercise.

* **Objective 2.1:** Identify metabolic markers.
* **Objective 2.2:** Formulate heat safety protocols for specialized populations.

> **Note:** Always align prescriptions with ACSM 11th Edition standards.
