# 🏛️ ADA Standards for Accessible Design (SSoT)
**Single Source of Truth (SSoT) for EXW265: Exercise for Special Populations**
**Institutional Authority:** Department of Justice (DOJ) / 2010 ADA Standards

---

## 🏛️ 1. Mission Intent
The Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) ensures that individuals with disabilities have the same opportunities as everyone else to enjoy the benefits of fitness and wellness facilities. For the **Clinical Auditor**, these standards are the non-negotiable legal floor for "Clear for Takeoff" status.

---

## 🏗️ 2. Core Architectural Standards (Fitness Specific)

### 2.1: Accessible Routes & Ingress
* **Doorway Clearance:** Minimum **32 inches (815 mm)** clear width when the door is open 90 degrees.
* **Thresholds:** If existing, must not exceed **1/2 inch (13 mm)** in height.
* **Path of Travel:** Must be firm, stable, and slip-resistant.



### 2.2: Exercise Machine Access (Section 1004)
At least **one of each type** of exercise machine must be on an accessible route.
* **Clear Floor Space:** Each accessible machine must have a clear floor space of **30 x 48 inches (760 x 1220 mm)**.
* **Positioning:** This space must be positioned for a user to transfer from a wheelchair or mobility device.

### 2.3: Reach Ranges & Controls (Section 308)
* **Unobstructed Forward Reach:** Maximum **48 inches (1220 mm)** high; minimum **15 inches (380 mm)** low.
* **Controls:** Operable parts (buttons, weight pins) must be usable with one hand without tight grasping or twisting.

## 🛡️ 3. The Clinical Intersection
While these standards ensure **Physical Access**, they do not account for **Thermal Stress**.

> **Auditor Note:** A facility may pass every ADA check in this document but remain **Grounded** if it fails the **Yuma Thermal Safety Protocol** (e.g., an unshaded 60ft asphalt path leading to an ADA-compliant door).

## 🧪 4. Quantitative Scorecard Integration
Use these checkpoints to verify the "Legal Anchor" of your Facility Audit:
1. **Entrance Clearance (32"):** Required.
2. **Path Width (36" min):** Required between equipment rows.
3. **Machine Transfer Space:** Required for at least one of every machine type.
4. **Turning Space (60" circle):** Required at the end of equipment rows.
