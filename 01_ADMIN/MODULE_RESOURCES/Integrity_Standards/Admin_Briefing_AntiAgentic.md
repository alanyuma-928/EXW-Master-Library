# 🏛️ Admin Briefing: The Anti-Agentic Protocol
**Audience:** Instructional Designers & Department Leads

## 🚀 Overview
The **Anti-Agentic Protocol** is a design framework specifically engineered to prevent students from "outsourcing" their cognitive workload to generative AI. In the Exercise Science (EXW) curriculum, this is not just an academic integrity concern—it is a **clinical safety mandate**.

## 🛠️ Key Guardrails
1. **SSoT Binding:** Content is "grounded" in specific federal standards (PAGA/Dietary Guidelines) that AI often generalizes or hallucinates.
2. **Clinical Audit (High-Friction):** Every assignment forces a manual fact-check of AI output. This ensures the student is performing the role of an **Auditor**, not just a prompter.
3. **P-T-C-F Requirement:** We require "Prompt Engineering" transparency. By mandating Persona, Task, Context, and Format, we ensure students are "Architecting" their clinical care.

## 🏁 Goal
To produce workforce-ready graduates who can safely pilot AI engines while maintaining total clinical authority.
