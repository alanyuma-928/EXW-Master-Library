# 🛡️ Anti-Agentic Architecture: Resistance Protocol
**Version:** 1.0 (The Einstein Protocol)

## 🧠 Philosophy
We actively resist "Agentic Offloading"—the tendency for students to let AI perform the thinking rather than the processing. Our architecture forces human presence through "High-Friction" design.

## 🛠️ Design Mandates
1. **The Human-in-the-Loop (Centaur):** AI is the Engine; the Student is the Pilot. The Engine never flies alone.
2. **Clinical Audit Friction:** No AI output is accepted without a mandatory 150-word human-led audit against the **Source of Truth (SSoT)**.
3. **The SEAL Standard:** We grade on competency and safety. A failure to audit is a structural failure (0 pts).
4. **Contextual Binding (P-T-C-F):** Prompts must be bound by specific clinical and environmental context (e.g., "Yuma heat") to prevent generic, low-effort outputs.

## 🏁 Objective
To ensure that Residents graduate as **Architects** of clinical care, not just consumers of AI data.
