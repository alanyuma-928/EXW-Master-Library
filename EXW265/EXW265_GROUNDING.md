# EXW 265: Exercise for Special Populations (SSOT)
**Single Source of Truth:** Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA), 2nd Edition, 2018.

## PAGA Safety Gates
* **Aerobic:** 150-300 mins moderate or 75-150 mins vigorous per week.
* **Resistance:** 2+ days/week for all major muscle groups.
* **Special Pop:** Specific guidelines for chronic conditions (Diabetes, CVD, etc.) and Older Adults (balance + multicomponent physical activity).
