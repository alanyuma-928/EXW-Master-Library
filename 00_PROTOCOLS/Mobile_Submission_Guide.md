### 🏗️ Submission Pillars (Zero-File Protocol)
1. **The DTK Export:** Complete your diagnostic audit in the **WPE DTK App**. Use the "Copy to Clipboard" function to capture your results.
2. **The Canvas Entry:** Open the **Canvas Student App**. Navigate to your specific Module Lab and select "Text Entry."
3. **The Einstein Defense:** Immediately below your pasted DTK data, type your mandatory **3-sentence Einstein Protocol Defense**.

  A logic collapse occurs when a student attempts to upload a screenshot of the DTK App as their submission. In the WPE ecosystem, <strong>Text is Data</strong>. Screenshots are unsearchable and violate the Zero-File Protocol. If you upload a file instead of using Text Entry, the audit fails.
