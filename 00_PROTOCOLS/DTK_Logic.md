2. **The Logic Loop:** Auditing the relationship between the **Pattern** (the data), the **Rule** (the standard), and the **Solve** (the intervention).
3. **The Sovereignty Pattern:** Prioritizing human clinical judgment over raw AI output through the mandatory application of the Einstein Protocol.
4. **Environmental Integrity:** Mapping real-time ambient stressors (WBGT/UV) to clinical thresholds to ensure participant safety in high-heat environments.
5. **Food-First Nutriton:** Prioritizing USDA Dietary Patterns over commercial supplementation or fad-based interventions.
