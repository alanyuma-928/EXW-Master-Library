📖 Instructional Content

In the **Centaur Protocol**, the AI provides the draft, but the **2025-2030 Dietary Guidelines** provide the law. For residents of the Imperial Valley, where food insecurity and chronic disease are key variables, specific guideline adherence is mandatory.

Key Concept: Guideline Hallucination

A guideline hallucination occurs when the AI misinterprets or "averages" clinical recommendations. Common errors include:

  • Exceeding the 10% ceiling for added sugars.
  • Confusing "Plant-Based" with "Ultra-Processed" vegan options.
  • Suggesting sodium levels that exceed the 2,300 mg safety cap for adults.
