📖 Instructional Content

In the Centaur Protocol, security is maintained through Iterative Verification. Nutritional misinformation often creeps in through "unconstrained optimization"—where the AI attempts to solve for a specific goal (e.g., rapid fat loss) while ignoring safety baselines.

Key Concept: Nutritional Drift

Nutritional drift occurs when the AI gradually deviates from the SSOT to provide more "marketable" but less safe advice. Signs include:

  • Recommending excessive protein levels that exceed clinical safety caps.
  • Suggesting "workarounds" for established nutritional redlines.
  • Ignoring localized food safety and demographics.
