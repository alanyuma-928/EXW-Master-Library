EXW265: Exercise for Special Populations

Activity Overview: The Fall 2026 Lead Consultant Audit

Congratulations! For Week 14, you are being promoted from student to Lead Curriculum Consultant.

Coach Alan is currently in the "Architect Phase" for the Fall 2026 version of EXW265. To ensure the next cohort receives the most effective instruction, we need your unfiltered insights. Instead of a boring survey, you will use the AI Tool Box to conduct a professional audit of this course.

Your Mission

You will use a specialized prompt to engage with the AI. Your goal is to identify what worked, what didn't, and how well we stuck to our Single Source of Truth: PAGA 2018.

The Protocol: Add File (+)

To provide high-quality feedback, the AI needs to see the actual work you've done. Follow these steps precisely:

  1. Download Your Evidence: Download your Syllabus and one of your Lab Part B: Practical Application files from Canvas.
  2. Initialize the AI Tool Box: Open Gemini.
  3. The Add File (+) Step: Click the plus (+) icon in the chat bar and upload both the Syllabus and your Lab file.
  4. Apply the Prompt: Copy and paste the audit prompt below into the same chat window.
  5. Conduct the Audit: Answer the AI's questions. Remember to wrap any specific clinical reflections in triple-hashes (###).
  6. Submit: Ask the AI to "Generate my Fall 2026 Strategy Memo" and paste the result into this assignment.

🛠️ The AI Tool Box Prompt

Copy and paste the entire block below AFTER you have uploaded your files:

### FALL 2026 CURRICULUM AUDITOR PROMPT ###

Role: You are a Senior Instructional Designer interviewing a Lead Consultant (the student).
Data Source: Analyze the uploaded Syllabus and Lab files to provide context-aware questions.
Tone: Professional, supportive, and analytical (The Narrator).
SSoT: PAGA 2018 (2nd Ed.).

Instructions:
1. Acknowledge the uploaded files. 
2. Interview the student to gather feedback for Coach Alan using the following questions:
   - "Based on the syllabus modules, which area felt most grounded in PAGA 2018 standards?"
   - "Looking at your uploaded Lab Part B, did the 'Mission Loop' framework help you audit for safety, or was it a hurdle?"
   - "If you were the Architect, what specific change would you make to the Lab requirements to improve the experience?"
3. Ensure the student wraps all clinical examples in ### delimiters.

Final Output: Create a "Fall 2026 Strategy Memo" for Coach Alan summarizing the student's feedback into: STRENGTHS (Keep), FRICTION POINTS (Fix), and INNOVATIONS (Add).

### END PROMPT ###

Coach's Note: By uploading your actual work, you are helping the AI perform a "Stress Test" on the course architecture. Let’s build something better for Fall 2026!
