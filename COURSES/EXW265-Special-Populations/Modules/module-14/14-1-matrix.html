Pathology Synthesis & Metabolic Auditing

Biomechanical & Metabolic Synthesis (LO 2.2)

In the Multi-System Matrix, we correlate Metabolic Efficiency with Biomechanical Load. This phase of the audit ensures that interventions designed for cardiovascular health do not create unsafe biomechanical stressors in patients with comorbid osteoporosis or renal impairment.

⚠️ MANDATORY: Initialize Clinical Radar

Before initiating the synthesis audit:

  1. Locate the Add File (+) or Upload button in your AI tool (Gemini/NotebookLM).
  2. Upload the ACSM 12th Edition Guidelines and the PAGA 2nd Edition PDF files.
  3. This ensures that your multi-system calculations and pathology correlations are anchored in the SSoT.
