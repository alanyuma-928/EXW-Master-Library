📖 Instructional Content

In the **Specialist Phase**, we ensure that **Physical Activity is Inclusive**. Universal Design is the framework of creating environments and programs that are usable by all people, to the greatest extent possible, without the need for adaptation.

Key Concept: The 60-Inch Rule

For individuals using mobility devices (wheelchairs, walkers), the ADA Standard requires specific spatial tolerances:

  • Turning Space: A circular space with a 60-inch minimum diameter is required for a 180-degree turn.
  • Clear Floor Space: A minimum of 30 inches by 48 inches is required for a stationary mobility device.
  • Path of Travel: Must be at least 36 inches wide and free of obstructions (like loose weights or improperly stored benches).
