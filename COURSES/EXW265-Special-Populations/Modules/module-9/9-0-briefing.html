Key Concept: The Two-Hour Pain Rule

This rule is the gold standard for clinical exercise prescription in arthritis populations:

  • The Rule: If a client's joint pain is greater 2 hours after exercise than it was before the session, the intensity or duration was too high.
  • The Action: The Resident must pivot to lower-impact activities (e.g., swimming or seated resistance) during the next session.
