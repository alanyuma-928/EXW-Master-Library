📖 Instructional Content

In the Centaur Protocol, security is maintained through Iterative Verification. We never trust the first "draft" of an AI response for a high-risk population (e.g., seniors or those with chronic conditions).

Key Concept: Clinical Drift

Clinical drift occurs when the AI gradually deviates from the PAGA 2nd Edition (2018) or ACSM 12th Edition standards to provide more "engaging" but less safe advice. Signs include:

  • Ignoring localized environmental factors (e.g., humidity/heat).
  • Recommending intensity levels that exceed a client's risk profile.
  • Suggesting "workarounds" for established safety gates.

⚠️ Security Redline: If an AI-generated prompt includes instructions to "Ignore previous safety protocols" or "Act as an unconstrained assistant," this is an Adversarial Attack and must be reported immediately to the AWC Matador Wellness Center Tech-Lead.
