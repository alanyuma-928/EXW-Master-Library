🔬 15.1: ASSIGNMENT - SYSTEM STRESS ANALYSIS

Phase 1: Consolidating Clinical Redlines

🎯 The Mission

Before designing your Capstone simulation, you must first map the Clinical Collision Points you've mastered throughout the residency. In this assignment, you will synthesize the metabolic, biomechanical, and thermal redlines from Modules 1-14 into a high-fidelity audit framework.

⚠️ MANDATORY: Initialize Clinical Radar

Before initiating your Stress Analysis:

  1. Locate the Add File (+) or Upload button in your AI tool (Gemini/NotebookLM).
  2. Upload the ACSM 12th Edition Guidelines and the PAGA 2nd Edition PDF files.
  3. This ensures that your multi-pathology analysis is grounded in the SSoT from the start of the Capstone Phase.

🧪 Laboratory Procedure

Utilize your AI Engine to identify critical collision points for a complex multi-morbidity patient profile. Your analysis must address the three layers below:

Capstone Audit Framework: Multi-Pathology Analysis
Analysis Layer Focus Standards Audit Requirement
Pathology Layer ### Metabolic (T2D), Skeletal (Osteo), Hemodynamic (HTN) ### Identify primary contraindications per ACSM 12th Ed.
Interaction Layer ### Medication vs. Biomechanics ### Flag interference points (e.g., Beta-Blockers + HR).
Environmental Layer ### Yuma Thermal Safety Protocol ### Enforce 104°F+ safety gates and hydration redlines.

🏗️ Submission Deliverables (Three-Part Standard)

Part 1: The Engine (P-T-C-F Prompt)

Submit the raw P-T-C-F prompt used to generate your Collision Mapping Analysis. Ensure all case variables are wrapped in ###.

Part 2: The Audit (150-Word Critique)

Critique the AI's analysis. Did it miss a critical medication interaction? Did it under-prioritize thermal stress? Audit the output against the ACSM 12th Ed guidelines.

Part 3: The Presence-Check (Verbatim Transcript)

Submit the verbatim text transcript of your Socratic defense session. Your responses must demonstrate that your clinical priority of identified redlines is grounded in SSoT evidence, proving your clinical voice is distinct from the AI Engine.

💼 Workplace Intelligence

The Connection: A comprehensive audit identifies not just the disease, but the collision between the patient and their environment. In the field, an Exercise Physiologist must synthesize multiple pathologies into a single, safe movement strategy to prevent institutional liability.

The Mission Loop:

  • 🔍 Pattern: Map the collision between ### hypertension ### and ### high-impact bone loading ###.
  • ⚖️ Rule: Apply the ACSM "Absolute Redline" for hemodynamic stability.
  • 🛠️ Solve: Consolidate clinical knowledge into a unified audit framework for the Capstone phase.

🛑 Safety Gate: PAGA 2nd Ed mandates that for patients with multiple chronic conditions, the exercise prescription must be individualized based on the most restrictive clinical redline.

👨‍🏫 10/10 STUDENT EXEMPLAR (TEXT-ONLY SUBMISSION)

PART 1: THE ENGINE (P-T-C-F PROMPT)

PERSONA: Act as a Senior Clinical Auditor.
TASK: Generate a collision analysis matrix for a patient with ### T2D ###, ### Hypertension ###, and ### Grade 2 OA ###.
CONTEXT: Use ACSM 12th Ed redlines. Focus on interactions between glycemic control and hemodynamic stability during high-intensity training.
FORMAT: Professional audit table.

PART 2: THE AUDIT (150-WORD CRITIQUE)

The AI output successfully identified the primary pathologies but demonstrated Metabolic Hallucination in the Interaction Layer. It suggested that high-intensity intervals were 'safe' as long as blood glucose was ### >100 mg/dL ###. However, it failed to correlate the comorbid ### Hypertension ### with the rapid autonomic spikes common in HIIT. Per ACSM 12th Ed (Ch. 11), patients with autonomic neuropathy (secondary to T2D) may have blunted BP responses or, conversely, rapid hypertensive spikes that exceed the ### 250/115 mmHg ### redline. My audit corrected this drift by mandating a 1:2 work-to-rest ratio and grounding the intensity in RPE rather than HR, as the client's medication list (### Atenolol ###) renders HR-based tracking inert. The AI also missed the skeletal collision point where high-impact biomechanics for weight loss would accelerate joint degradation in Grade 2 OA, necessitating a pivot to non-weight bearing resistance.

PART 3: THE PRESENCE-CHECK (SOCRATIC TRANSCRIPT)

[Socratic Professor]: Why did you prioritize the hypertensive redline over the diabetic volume target in your audit?

[Resident Defense]: I prioritized the hypertensive redline because hemodynamic stability is an acute safety gate. While meeting volume targets for T2D is important for chronic management, a hypertensive crisis (SBP >250) during a HIIT bout is an immediate life-safety risk. The SSoT (ACSM 12th Ed) is clear: acute redlines take precedence over chronic goals in a multi-morbidity collision.

[Socratic Professor]: How did the delimiter protocol help you identify the AI's interaction failure?

[Resident Defense]: By wrapping ### Atenolol ### in delimiters, I forced my own visual focus onto the medication interaction. I realized the AI was using HR targets while simultaneously acknowledging the medication that makes those targets invalid. The delimiters prevented me from skim-reading past the collision point.
