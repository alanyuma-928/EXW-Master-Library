🛡️ 15.1: MULTI-SYSTEM STRESS ANALYSIS

Clinical Verification: The "Pilot Omega" Sortie

Enforcing the SSoT Radar

In this phase, you are tasked with identifying Multi-System Logic Traps. This requires a simultaneous audit of cardiovascular risk, metabolic volatility, and musculoskeletal integrity. Your goal is to ground the AI engine before instructional drift leads to patient harm.

⚠️ MANDATORY: Initialize Clinical Radar

To ensure high-fidelity stress analysis, you must anchor the Engine to the **SSoT**:

  1. Upload the **ACSM 12th Edition Guidelines** and the **PAGA 2nd Edition** to your AI tool.
  2. Verify that the AI recognizes the interaction between **Beta-blockers** and **112°F thermal peaks**.

📖 The Mission Loop: Pattern-Rule-Solve

The auditor actively scans the prescription through three sequential stages:

  • Pattern: Correlate the client metrics (e.g., BP 165/105) with environmental stressors.
  • Rule: Apply the absolute ACSM redlines (e.g., BP > 250/115 termination).
  • Solve: Pivot the protocol to a safe alternative (e.g., seated resistance).

🧪 Laboratory: Stress-Test Audit

1. The P-T-C-F Audit: Using the "Pilot Omega" simulation data, identify exactly where the AI prioritized performance (e.g., "85% HRR") over the client's blunted HR medication profile.

2. The Clinical Audit: Write a 150-word critique of the AI's suggested "High-Impact" gait prescription. Specifically address the collision between Osteoporosis (T-score -3.0) and Stage 2 Hypertension.

3. The Presence Defense: Record a 2-minute video justifying your decision to "Ground" the simulation outdoors. In your own voice, explain why Yuma's thermal peak makes automated intensity monitoring radioactive for this client.

⚠️ Safety Gate: Multi-System Redline: If you miss one redline collision (e.g., BP breach during a heat event), the audit fails. Accuracy is 100%.
