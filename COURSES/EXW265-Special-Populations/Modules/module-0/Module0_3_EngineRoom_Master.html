📘 THE ENGINE ROOM: SSoT & AI LOGIC

"The Centaur Protocol: Human Judgment + AI Processing"

🏗️ Navigating the Evidence

In this residency, we rely on the Single Source of Truth (SSoT). For all clinical prescriptions, your primary authority is the PAGA 2018 (2nd Edition) or ACSM 12th Ed.

🤖 Accessing Your Engine

We use Google Gemini as our clinical engine. Access the tool at gemini.google.com using your AWC or personal Google account.

✅ P-T-C-F Engine Checklist

  • 🟢 Persona: "Act as a Clinical Exercise Architect."
  • 🔵 Task: "Draft a 3-day aerobic plan."
  • 🟠 Context: "Yuma, AZ climate; PAGA standards."
  • 🟣 Format: "A professional Markdown table."

🎭 Guided Learning Deployment

In the Engine Room, we do not just 'chat' with AI. We deploy specific Guided Learning Roles from the Trifecta (See 0.3 AI Logic) to validate our work:

  • Cross-Examine your drafts with the Socratic Professor.
  • Translate complex theory with the Mastery Tutor.
  • Verify page-references with the Quiz Master.

🔗 Advanced Maneuver: Prompt Chaining

Do not ask the AI to do everything at once. Use Prompt Chaining to ensure clinical safety:

  1. Step 1: Ask the AI to define the standards for your population.
  2. Step 2: Ask the AI to draft the plan based on those standards.
  3. Step 3: Ask the AI to audit its own plan for safety hallucinations.

🧱 The Structural Delimiter

Wrap large datasets or guidelines in clear markers to keep the Engine organized:

### BEGIN SOURCE OF TRUTH ###
[Insert PAGA or ACSM Text Here]
### END SOURCE OF TRUTH ###

⚖️ The Clinical Audit (The Einstein Defense)

Because AI can "hallucinate," every assignment requires a 150-Word Clinical Audit. This is where you, the Pilot, verify the AI's claims against the SSoT. An assignment without an audit is a structural failure (0 pts).
