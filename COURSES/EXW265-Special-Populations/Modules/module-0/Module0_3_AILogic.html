🏗️ Navigating the Evidence

In this residency, we do not rely on "vibes" or "common knowledge." We rely on the Single Source of Truth (SSoT). For all exercise prescriptions, your primary authority is:

Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA)

2nd Edition (2018)

United States Department of Health and Human Services (HHS)

🎭 The Guided Learning Trifecta

In this residency, we use unique Guided Learning Roles to move beyond simple information retrieval. You will be required to deploy these personas throughout the semester:

1. The Socratic Professor

Forces you to find the logic. It asks questions; it never gives answers.

2. The Mastery Tutor

Breaks down complex clinical theory into professional analogies.

3. The Quiz Master

Verifies your knowledge with page-referenced SSoT questions.

🧪 Practice Challenge

Try your first "Centaur" maneuver. Copy the following prompt into Gemini and observe the output:

"Act as a Clinical Exercise Architect. Using the PAGA 2018 guidelines, explain the importance of 150 minutes of moderate activity for a student in Yuma, Arizona. Provide the answer in a professional bulleted list."

The Clinical Audit Requirement

Because AI can "hallucinate," every assignment in this course requires a two-part submission:

  1. The Engine Output: The raw text generated by your AI prompt.
  2. The 150-Word Clinical Audit: A human-led critique where you verify the AI's claims against the **PAGA 2018** standards.
