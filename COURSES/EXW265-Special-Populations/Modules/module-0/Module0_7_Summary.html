Mission Debrief

You have successfully completed the Module 0 Orientation. You have configured your flight controls, identified your Single Source of Truth (SSoT), and established the ethical framework for your Clinical AI Residency in Special Populations.

  • The Centaur Protocol: You are the Pilot (Judgment). The AI is the Engine (Processing). Never swap these roles.
