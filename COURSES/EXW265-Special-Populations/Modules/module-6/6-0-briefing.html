📖 Instructional Content

In the **Specialist Phase**, we must account for the **Hemodynamic Response** to exercise. For clients with hypertension, aerobic exercise is the primary recommendation, but the "dosage" must be precision-engineered to avoid hypertensive crisis or syncopal episodes.

Key Concept: Post-Exercise Hypotension (PEH)

PEH is a significant drop in blood pressure following acute exercise. In Yuma, the risk of PEH is heightened by:

  • Heat-induced vasodilation.
  • Dehydration (decreased blood volume).
  • Abrupt cessation of activity (blood pooling).
