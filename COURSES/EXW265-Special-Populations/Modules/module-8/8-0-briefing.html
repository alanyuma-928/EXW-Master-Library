📖 Instructional Content

In the **Specialist Phase**, we manage **Interrelated Pathologies**. Metabolic Syndrome is diagnosed when a client presents with at least three of the following:

  1. Abdominal Obesity: Waist circumference >102 cm (men) or >88 cm (women).
  2. Triglycerides: ≥150 mg/dL.
  3. HDL Cholesterol: <40 mg/dL (men) or <50 mg/dL (women).
  4. Blood Pressure: ≥130/85 mmHg.
  5. Fasting Glucose: ≥100 mg/dL.
