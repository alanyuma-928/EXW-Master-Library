Start With Why

In this Lab, we don't use AI to find shortcuts; we use it to find certainty. The High-Friction steps ahead are designed to turn an automated machine into a clinical mirror—reflecting your ability to lead, audit, and protect. Are you ready to be the Pilot in Command?

⚠️ MANDATORY: Initialize Clinical Radar

Before using any code blocks or simulations:

  1. Locate the Add File (+) or Upload button in your AI tool (Gemini/NotebookLM).
  2. Upload the Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) 2nd Edition.
  3. This anchors the AI to the SSoT and prevents clinical hallucinations.

⚖️ AWC "AI for All" Guiding Principles

  • 👨‍✈️ Accountability: You are the Pilot in Command. You are responsible for every clinical output and decision.
  • 🔒 Privacy: Protect the HIPAA/FERPA barrier. Never upload sensitive identifiers or PII to AI models.
  • 🤝 Equity: Success is based on effort. We utilize only institutional or free AI tools to ensure a level playing field.
  • 📡 Transparency: All AI-assisted work must be explicitly disclosed and cited in your mission flight logs.

🛡️ The Agentic AI Firewall

We blunt automated academic exploits through High-Friction Architecture: Contextual Anchoring, Logic Auditing, and Human-in-the-Loop oversight. In other words, we stop AI cheating by requiring proof of genuine human effort, auditing the logic of submissions for AI-like patterns, and having humans check the work.

⚖️ Institutional Compliance: HIPAA & FERPA

THE GOLDEN RULE: NO SENSITIVE DATA UPLOADS.

Arizona Western College anchors all AI usage to federal privacy standards. Failure to de-identify data is a violation of professional ethics.
