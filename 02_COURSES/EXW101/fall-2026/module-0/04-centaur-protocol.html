04: The Centaur Protocol

A "Centaur" is a human working in tandem with Artificial Intelligence. AI is incredibly fast, but it doesn't understand context or nuance. You do. In this course, you are the Pilot. The AI is your Engine.

The Einstein Protocol (Presence Defense)

Because you are allowed to use AI systems to help structure your assignments, you must prove you are actually driving the logic. Every lab submission requires a Presence Defense at the top.

This is a 2-3 sentence human-authored rationale explicitly explaining why you chose your clinical intervention before presenting the AI-assisted output.

Never Submit Raw Output

If you copy and paste an AI response without auditing it against the PAGA, you will crash the machine. Pilot the engine; don't let it pilot you.
