The Hybrid Practitioner

In EXW 101, we utilize the Centaur Approach. This is a workforce methodology where the human provides the clinical intuition and sociological context (the "Head"), while the AI provides data-processing speed and logic verification (the "Hooves").

This protocol ensures you are not "outsourcing" your thinking to technology, but rather leveraging it to achieve higher-fidelity movement audits.

Rules of Engagement

Your interaction with the AI Scaffolder must adhere to these three clinical standards:

01. The Auditor

You are the final Auditor. You are professionally responsible for every claim you submit. Never "blindly" accept an AI output without verifying it against the SSoT (PAGA 2018).

02. Socratic Logic

AI is your Socratic Scaffolder. Use it to verify your bionergetic math or sociological reasoning—not to bypass the cognitive load of the mission.

03. Data Integrity

You must protect data integrity by using the ### Delimiter Protocol. This separates your clinical data from your instructional intent.
