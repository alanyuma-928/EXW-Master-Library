03: The Single Source of Truth (SSoT)

We do not guess in this class. We audit against the rules. Our primary clinical rulebook for EXW101 is the Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) 2018, 2nd Edition.

⚓ SSoT Active: PAGA 2018 (Federal Clinical Standard)

Why SSoT Matters

If you prescribe exercise to a client, you need a legal and scientific basis for it. The PAGA provides the exact thresholds (e.g., 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity per week) required to secure cardiovascular health. Every case study in this course will be filtered through this document.
