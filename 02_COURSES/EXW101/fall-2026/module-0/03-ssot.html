The Single Source of Truth (SSoT)

Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) 2018

Operational Authority

Professional credibility in Kinesiology is built on the ability to distinguish evidence-based science from fitness marketing. For EXW 101, our Single Source of Truth (SSoT) is the Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA), 2nd Edition (2018).

This federal standard provides the clinical Rules we use to determine if a population's movement Pattern is sufficient for health. Any claim made in this course must be traceable back to these guidelines.

Technical Requirement: The ### Protocol

When auditing client data against the SSoT, you must wrap all medical inputs or client movement patterns within triple-hashes (###). This ensures the Centaur Protocol (AI + Human) maintains data integrity.
