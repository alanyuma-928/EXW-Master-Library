        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-bottom: 2px solid #000080; padding-bottom: 10px;">The Centaur Architecture</h3>
        <p>In this residency, we do not outsource thinking to models. We operationalize <strong>Centaur Intelligence</strong>—the seamless integration of high-level human judgment (The Pilot) and high-speed machine processing (The Engine).</p>
        
        <h3 style="color: #000080; margin-top: 40px;">The Operating Rules</h3>
        <ul style="line-height: 1.8;">
            <li><strong>You are the Pilot:</strong> Every AI output must be audited against the SSoT by a human professional.</li>
            <li><strong>The AI is the Engine:</strong> Use the model to process patterns and draft solutions, but never to make final clinical calls.</li>
            <li><strong>Socratic Oversight:</strong> If the Engine provides a weak solve, you must "Recalibrate" it using the Mission Loop.</li>
        </ul>
    </section>
