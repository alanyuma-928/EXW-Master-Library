    <section style="padding: 25px;">
        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-bottom: 2px solid #000080; padding-bottom: 10px;">Operational Logic</h3>
        <p>In this course, all clinical work follows a specific, three-step logic sequence known as the <strong>Mission Loop</strong>. This ensures that every recommendation is grounded in factual standards and clinical integrity.</p>
        
        <ol style="line-height: 1.8;">
            <li><strong>Pattern:</strong> Identify the clinical observation or data point (The Evidence).</li>
            <li><strong>Rule:</strong> Cite the specific SSoT authority that governs the observation (The Authority).</li>
            <li><strong>Solve:</strong> Provide the hardened clinical prescription or action (The Solution).</li>
        </ol>
    </section>

    <section style="background-color: #e6e9ed; padding: 25px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 20px; border-left: 10px solid #000080;">
        <h4 style="color: #000080; margin-top: 0;">🧭 Navigational Hint</h4>
        <p>Never provide a "Solve" without first identifying the "Pattern" and citing the "Rule." If you skip to the solve, you have failed the mission-critical audit.</p>
    </section>
