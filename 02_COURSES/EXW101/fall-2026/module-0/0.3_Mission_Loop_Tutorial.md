## 📡 The Operational Logic

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ The Three Pillars
1. **Pattern:** Identify the evidence (What did you see?).
2. **Rule:** Cite the SSoT authority (What is the clinical law?).
3. **Solve:** Provide the prescriptive action (What is the fix?).

&nbsp;

### 📂 Logic Collapses
A "Logic Collapse" occurs when a resident skips Step 2 (The Rule) and provides a "Solve" based on opinion. In this residency, opinion is deprecated. We only apply the Law.
