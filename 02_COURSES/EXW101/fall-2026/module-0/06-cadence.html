The Mission Rhythm

To maintain high-fidelity performance, EXW 101 operates on a standardized weekly Cadence. Unless otherwise noted, each Module opens on Tuesday at 12:00 AM and concludes the following Monday at 11:59 PM.

EXCEPTION: Module 0 begins on Monday at 12:00 AM on the first day of the semester to allow for immediate orientation.

Your workweek is structured into three specific operational stages. Use the 00–08 file sequence as your checklist:

01. PATTERN

02. RULE

THURS-SAT

Files 03-04: Apply the SSoT (PAGA 2018), perform AI audits using the ### Protocol, and post initial briefing.

03. SOLVE

SUN-MON

Files 05-08: Execute practical labs, conduct peer audits, and pass the final readiness gate.
