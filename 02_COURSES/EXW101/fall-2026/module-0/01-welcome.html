The Movement Reset

Welcome, Practitioners. You are beginning your journey into the foundational study of human movement. In EXW 101, we look beyond sports scores and workout trends to understand the physiological, sociological, and historical Patterns that define how and why humans move.

In this clinical workspace, we prioritize the Single Source of Truth (SSoT)—the Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) 2018. This federal standard provides the clinical Rules we use to audit physical activity levels and design community health interventions.

As we move through the semester, remember our core operational framework: Pattern, Rule, and Solve. We don't guess; we audit data to find evidence-based solutions for a more active society.

What to Expect

This course is designed to transition you from a student to a Kinesiology professional. To succeed, you must commit to the following:

  • Accuracy: Anchor every claim in the PAGA 2018 SSoT.
