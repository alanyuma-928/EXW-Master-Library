01: Welcome Briefing

Welcome, students. This is not a standard lecture class. If you are here to just memorize bone structures for an hour and then forget them, you are in the wrong place.

In this course, we treat Kinesiology as a clinical tool. We use movement to fix humans who are broken by occupational fatigue, sedentary lifestyles, and extreme environmental stress.

The Expectations

We operate on a military-grade standard of professionalism. You are expected to:

  • Communicate clearly and concisely.
  • Base all of your solutions on our Federal Single Source of Truth (SSoT).
  • Utilize the AI securely and effectively, driving it as the Pilot without letting it do the work for you.

Slow is Smooth, Smooth is Fast

This is our motto. If you rush through the clinical baselines, you will crash during the execution phase. Take your time to read the patterns before you attempt to solve them.
