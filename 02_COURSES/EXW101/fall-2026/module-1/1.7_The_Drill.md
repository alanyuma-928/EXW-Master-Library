### 🏗️ The Scenario
A client walks their dog for 10 minutes every morning (zero other movement). They are 45 years old with no known metabolic conditions.

&nbsp;

### 📂 Submission Requirements
1. **The Pattern:** Identify the Inactivity Gap (minutes per week).
2. **The Rule:** Cite the exact PAGA aerobic mandate.
3. **The Solve:** Propose a weekly schedule that bridges the gap.
