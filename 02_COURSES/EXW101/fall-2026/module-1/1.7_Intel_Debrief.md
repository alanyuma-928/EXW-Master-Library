### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article related to **The History of Physical Activity** or **Modern Kinesiology Standards**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform a "Research Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the primary movement trend identified in the research?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with PAGA 2018 (2nd Ed) standards?
   * **Solve:** How does this research inform a modern clinical intervention?
