## 📡 Identifying the Pattern
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Mission Loop Step 1: Physiological Decay</strong><br>
  The Inactivity Gap is the measurable distance between a sedentary state and metabolic health requirements.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Clinical Observations
Notice the pattern: as activity is decoupled from daily survival, we observe a cascade of clinical logic collapses—insulin resistance, cardiorespiratory fitness decay, and chronic disease onset.

&nbsp;

### 📂 Operational Task
Observe the client data. Record the deficit in movement duration and intensity. Do not interpret yet—simply identify the gap.

&nbsp;

<div style="background-color: #FFFDD0; color: #000080; padding: 15px; border: 2px solid #000080; border-radius: 8px;">
  <h4>🔎 Pattern Recognition</h4>
  Identifying the gap is the foundation of the audit. Without an accurate pattern identification, the "Solve" will be flawed.
</div>
