    <section style="padding: 25px;">
        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-bottom: 2px solid #000080; padding-bottom: 10px;">The Pattern: Physiological Decay</h3>
        <p>In modern society, we observe a consistent clinical pattern: **The Inactivity Gap**. This is the measurable distance between a resident's current sedentary behavior and the physiological requirements for metabolic health. </p>
        
        <h3 style="color: #000080; margin-top: 40px;">Clinical Observations</h3>
        <p>When physical activity is decoupled from daily survival, we observe a cascade of "Logic Collapses" in human health, including increased insulin resistance, decreased cardiorespiratory fitness, and the eventual onset of preventable chronic diseases.</p>
    </section>

    <section style="background-color: #e6e9ed; padding: 25px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 20px; border-left: 10px solid #000080;">
        <h4 style="color: #000080; margin-top: 0;">🔎 Mission Loop: Pattern</h4>
        <p>Your first task as a Curriculum Architect is to identify this gap in student profiles. Observe the data—do not interpret yet. Simply record the deficit in movement duration and intensity.</p>
    </section>
