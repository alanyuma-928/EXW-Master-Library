### 🏗️ The Great Decoupling
With the rise of automation, movement and labor were decoupled. This clinical shift necessitated the creation of **Kinesiology** to combat the sedentary decay of modern environments.

&nbsp;

### 📂 Operational History
1. **The Survival Era:** Movement integrated into daily existence.
2. **The Industrial Shift:** Sedentary behavior becomes the societal norm.
3. **The Clinical Era:** Movement must be audited and prescribed with precision.
