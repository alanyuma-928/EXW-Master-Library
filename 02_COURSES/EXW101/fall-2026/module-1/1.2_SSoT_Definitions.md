### 🏗️ Core Terminology
1. **Physical Activity:** Any bodily movement produced by skeletal muscles that results in energy expenditure.
2. **Exercise:** Planned, structured, repetitive movement with a final objective of improvement.
3. **Kinesiology:** The study of physical activity and its impact on health and quality of life.
