    <section style="padding: 25px;">
        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-bottom: 2px solid #000080; padding-bottom: 10px;">The Single Source of Truth (SSoT)</h3>
        <p>In this residency, we do not guess. We audit. The <strong>SSoT</strong> is the immutable authority used to verify clinical claims. For this course, our primary SSoT is the <em>Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) 2018, 2nd Edition</em>.</p>
        
        <h3 style="color: #000080; margin-top: 40px;">Core Terminology (PAGA Standards)</h3>
        <ul style="line-height: 1.8;">
            <li><strong>Physical Activity:</strong> Any bodily movement produced by skeletal muscles that results in energy expenditure.</li>
            <li><strong>Exercise:</strong> A subset of physical activity that is planned, structured, repetitive, and has a final or intermediate objective the improvement or maintenance of physical fitness.</li>
            <li><strong>Kinesiology:</strong> The study of physical activity and its impact on health, society, and quality of life.</li>
        </ul>
    </section>

    <section style="background-color: #e6e9ed; padding: 25px; border-radius: 4px; margin: 20px; border-left: 10px solid #000080;">
        <h4 style="color: #000080; margin-top: 0;">⚠️ The Precision Gate</h4>
        <p>Using these terms correctly is the first step in the <strong>Mission Loop</strong>. A "logic collapse" occurs when a resident confuses general activity with prescribed exercise.</p>
