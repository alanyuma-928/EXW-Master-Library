### 🏗️ Core Terminology (PAGA Standards)
1. **Physical Activity:** Any bodily movement produced by skeletal muscles that results in energy expenditure.
2. **Exercise:** Physical activity that is planned, structured, and repetitive and has as a final or intermediate objective the improvement or maintenance of physical fitness.
3. **Kinesiology:** The study of physical activity and its impact on health, society, and quality of life.
