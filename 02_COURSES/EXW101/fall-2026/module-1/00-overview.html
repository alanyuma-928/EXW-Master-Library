Module 1: The Movement Reset

Launchpad: Foundational Concepts of Kinesiology

Operational Objective

By the end of this Module, you will be able to distinguish between physical activity, exercise, and sport using the SSoT (PAGA 2018). You will apply the Mission Loop to identify basic movement Patterns and audit them against federal health guidelines to Solve for baseline wellness gaps.

Course Alignment

  • CLO 2.1: Analyze the history and philosophy of Physical Education and Kinesiology.
  • CLO 2.2: Identify the physiological benefits of movement based on the 2018 PAGA standards.
