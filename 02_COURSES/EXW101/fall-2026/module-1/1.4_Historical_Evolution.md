### 🏗️ Evolutionary Pillars
1. **The Ancient Mandate:** Movement as survival (Ancient Greece, Rome, and Indigenous traditions).
2. **The Industrial Redline:** The shift from labor-intensive lifestyles to sedentary occupation.
3. **The Clinical Turn:** The emergence of Kinesiology as a scientific discipline and the integration of AI-driven prescriptions.
