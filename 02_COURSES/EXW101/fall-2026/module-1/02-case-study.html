Mission Briefing

As a Kinesiology practitioner, your first task is to identify the "Movement Pattern" of a specific population. You are auditing a 42-year-old remote graphic designer named "Alex." Alex spends the majority of their day at a desk and reports feeling "sluggish" and "stiff." To design a community-level intervention, you must first determine if Alex meets the federal thresholds for health.

Your goal is to extract the raw data below and prepare it for a clinical audit against the SSoT (PAGA 2018).
