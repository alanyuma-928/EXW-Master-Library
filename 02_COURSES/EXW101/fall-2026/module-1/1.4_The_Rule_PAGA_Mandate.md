### 🏗️ The Adult Aerobic Mandate
For substantial health benefits, adults should achieve at least:
1. **150 to 300 minutes** of moderate-intensity aerobic physical activity per week.
2. **OR 75 to 150 minutes** of vigorous-intensity aerobic physical activity per week.

&nbsp;

### 📂 Strengthening Mandate
Muscle-strengthening activities should be performed on **2 or more days** per week.
