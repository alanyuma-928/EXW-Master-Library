        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-bottom: 2px solid #000080; padding-bottom: 10px;">The Authority: PAGA 2018 (2nd Ed)</h3>
        <p>Once a pattern is identified, we apply the <strong>Rule</strong>. In the WPE Department, our rules are derived directly from the <em>Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) 2018</em>. These are not suggestions—they are the clinical minimums for longevity.</p>
        
        <h3 style="color: #000080; margin-top: 40px;">The Adult Aerobic Mandate</h3>
        <p>For substantial health benefits, adults should do at least:</p>
        <ul style="line-height: 1.8;">
            <li><strong>150 to 300 minutes</strong> of moderate-intensity aerobic physical activity per week; OR</li>
            <li><strong>75 to 150 minutes</strong> of vigorous-intensity aerobic physical activity per week.</li>
        </ul>
        <p>Additionally, muscle-strengthening activities should be performed on <strong>2 or more days</strong> per week.</p>
