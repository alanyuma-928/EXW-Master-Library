        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-left: 5px solid #000080; padding-left: 15px;">The Development Filter</h3>
        <p>Your movement mission is dictated by your stage of development:</p>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The Youth Rule (Ages 6–17):</strong> 60 minutes or more of moderate-to-vigorous physical activity **DAILY**. Must include muscle and bone-strengthening 3 days/week.</li>
            <li><strong>The Adult Rule (Ages 18–64):</strong> At least 150–300 minutes of moderate-intensity (or 75–150 min of vigorous) activity per week + 2 days of muscle strengthening.</li>
            <li><strong>The Older Adult Mandate (65+):</strong> All adult rules apply, but must include **balance training** and multi-component activities to prevent falls and preserve the "Chassis" (Module 3).</li>
        </ul>
        
        <div style="background-color: #fefce8; padding: 20px; border-left: 5px solid #ca8a04;">
            <strong>Lifespan Logic:</strong> For youth, movement is for <strong>Growth</strong>. For adults, it is for <strong>Maintenance</strong>. For older adults, it is for <strong>Independence</strong>.
        </div>
