### 🏗️ Philosophical Pillars (PAGA Alignment)
1. **The Dualism Gate:** Identifying the legacy "Mind-Body" split that treats the physical body as a machine separate from the mental/emotional self.
2. **The Holistic Rule:** Recognizing that physical activity influences mental health, cognitive function, and emotional resilience (as mandated by PAGA 2nd Ed).
3. **The Value Pattern:** Auditing how intrinsic values (e.g., joy, play, mastery) drive long-term adherence more effectively than extrinsic "drills."
