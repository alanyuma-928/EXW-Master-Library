<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Behavioral Audit</strong><br>
  Movement does not happen in a vacuum. We audit the sociological patterns of inactivity to identify structural redlines and ensure community-wide health sovereignty.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Sociological Pillars (PAGA Standards)
1. **The Environmental Gate:** Auditing the physical environment (e.g., access to parks, sidewalks, and safe transit) as a primary determinant of movement patterns.
2. **The Socioeconomic Rule:** Identifying the correlation between income/education and the ability to meet the PAGA aerobic and muscle-strengthening gates.
3. **The Cultural Pattern:** Understanding how community traditions and social norms influence the "Mission Loop" of daily activity.
