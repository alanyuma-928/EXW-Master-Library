### 🏗️ Historical Pillars (Foundational Context)
1. **The Conscription Gate:** Understanding how high rejection rates during military drafts (e.g., WWI/WWII) catalyzed the national mandate for Physical Education in schools.
2. **The Calisthenic Rule:** Identifying the transition from rigid military-style drills to the modernized PAGA aerobic and strength-training standards.
3. **The Institutional Pattern:** Auditing how the US government used physical activity as a tool for national sovereignty and public health defense.
