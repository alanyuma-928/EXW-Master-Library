### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article from the last 5 years related to **Sociology of Movement** or **History of Physical Education**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform a "Sociological Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the specific sociological trend or historical shift identified?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with the PAGA 2018 (2nd Ed) standards?
   * **Solve:** How does this research improve the accuracy of your community interventions?
