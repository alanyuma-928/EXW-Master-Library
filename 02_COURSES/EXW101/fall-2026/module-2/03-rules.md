    <section id="metabolic-rules">
        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-left: 5px solid #000080; padding-left: 15px;">The Bioenergetic Filter</h3>
        <p>Every movement is powered by a specific "Rule" of energy production:</p>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The ATP-CP Rule (Power):</strong> For high-intensity bursts lasting < 10 seconds (e.g., a sprint or a jump). High force, low capacity.</li>
            <li><strong>The Glycolytic Rule (Speed):</strong> For moderate-to-high intensity lasting 30 seconds to 2 minutes (e.g., a 400m run). Uses glucose; produces lactic acid.</li>
            <li><strong>The Oxidative Rule (Endurance):</strong> For low-to-moderate intensity lasting > 3 minutes (e.g., walking, jogging). Uses oxygen to produce massive amounts of ATP over time.</li>
        </ul>
        
        <div style="background-color: #eef4f8; padding: 20px; border-left: 5px solid #000080;">
            <strong>System Logic:</strong> You don't "choose" a system; the <strong>Intensity</strong> of your movement selects it for you.
        </div>
    </section>
