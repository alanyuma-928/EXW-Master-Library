# 📂 EXW101 Module 2: Sociological Case Studies

---
**SSoT:** PAGA 2018 (2nd Ed) | **Context:** Community Movement Audits
---

## 🏗️ Case Study A: The Industrial Corridor
**Location:** Urban Industrial Zone  
**Population:** Shift workers in heavy manufacturing.  
**Pattern:** High levels of occupational labor but near-zero leisure-time physical activity.  
**Barrier:** Lack of green space; high traffic density; 12-hour shifts creating "Time Poverty."  
**Audit Task:** Identify the **PAGA Aerobic Rule** failure and propose an **Environmental Solve**.

## 🏗️ Case Study B: The Rural Desert
**Location:** Remote Agricultural Region  
**Population:** Migrant farming community.  
**Pattern:** Seasonal high-intensity labor followed by periods of extreme sedentarism.  
**Barrier:** Geographical isolation; lack of transit; no indoor movement facilities for high-heat months.  
**Audit Task:** Identify the **PAGA Muscle-Strengthening Rule** failure and propose a **Cultural Solve**.

## 🏗️ Case Study C: The Suburban Stagnation
**Location:** Sprawling Car-Centric Suburb  
**Population:** White-collar remote workers.  
**Pattern:** Low daily step count (<3,000 steps); high sitting time (>10 hours).  
**Barrier:** Cul-de-sac architecture; lack of sidewalks; "Car Sovereignty" norm.  
**Audit Task:** Identify the **PAGA Sedentary Behavior Rule** failure and propose a **Structural Solve**.
