Review the following clinical intake. This is the "Pattern." Before you figure out how to help him (The Solve), you need to understand exactly what his physiological baseline is doing during a standard shift in Yuma.

Patient Profile: Mateo

Mateo is a 42-year-old field supervisor for a local agricultural harvesting operation near the Imperial Valley border. He reached out to a local clinic complaining of chronic lower back pain, extreme leg fatigue, and feeling "worn out" on his days off. He has no prior history of organized physical training.

Primary Objective (The Solve)

Your task as the Pilot is to assess Mateo. You know that simply telling him to "work out harder" is a failure condition. Based on the Physical Activity Guidelines (PAGA) and the Yuma Thermal Safety Gate, write a brief, practical intervention plan.
