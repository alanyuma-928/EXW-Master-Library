### 🏗️ Mission Task
1. **The Post:** Share your **Community Audit Table** (from Unit 2.1). Identify one structural redline you discovered in the I-8 corridor (e.g., lack of safe transit or environmental barriers).
2. **The Audit (Reply to ONE peer):** Perform a "Sociological Sovereignty Audit" on a classmate's post. 
   * Did they correctly apply the **PAGA Environmental Gate**?
   * Is their interpretation of **Self-Efficacy** (Unit 2.4) accurate for the case study?
