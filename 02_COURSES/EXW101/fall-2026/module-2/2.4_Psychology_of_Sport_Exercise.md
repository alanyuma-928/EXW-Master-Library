### 🏗️ Psychological Pillars (PAGA Standards)
1. **The Self-Efficacy Gate:** Auditing a client's belief in their ability to perform a specific task (e.g., meeting the 150-minute aerobic mandate).
2. **The Intrinsic Motivation Rule:** Identifying patterns of "Autonomous Motivation" vs "Controlled Motivation" to predict adherence redlines.
3. **The Transtheoretical Logic:** Understanding the stages of change (Pre-contemplation to Maintenance) to ensure the intervention is psychologically synchronized with the client's state.
