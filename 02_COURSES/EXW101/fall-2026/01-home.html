Welcome to the study of human movement.

Welcome to EXW101: Concepts of Kinesiology

Welcome, students. I am Coach Alan, and this semester we will explore the foundational principles of Kinesiology and Physical Education. This course is designed to move beyond the surface level of "exercise" and dive into the physiological, sociological, and historical patterns that define human movement.

⚓ Single Source of Truth: Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) 2018, 2nd Ed.

The Mission Loop Framework

In this course, we don't just memorize facts; we solve problems. We will utilize the Mission Loop to navigate our curriculum:

  • Pattern: We identify the current state of physical inactivity and health trends in our community.
  • Rule: We apply the federal standards defined in the PAGA to establish safety and efficacy.
  • Solve: We design interventions that promote health and movement for all.

Course Objectives

  • Analyze the history and philosophy of Physical Education and Kinesiology.
  • Identify the physiological benefits of movement based on the 2018 PAGA standards.
  • Explore career pathways within the Exercise Science and Health sectors.
  • Develop community-level health intervention strategies for the Yuma region.
