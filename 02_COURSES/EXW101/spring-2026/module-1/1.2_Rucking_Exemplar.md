### 🏗️ Rucking Pillars (ACSM/Military Standards)
1. **The Load Gate:** Mandating a starting load of 10-15% of body weight to ensure musculoskeletal sovereignty and prevent joint-laxity logic collapses.
2. **The Cadence Rule:** Auditing for a steady, sustainable pace (e.g., 15-20 min/mile) to maximize aerobic-protective sovereignty without triggering orthopedic redlines.
3. **The Footwear Pattern:** Prioritizing supportive, broken-in footwear and moisture-wicking socks to maintain dermal sovereignty and bypass the blister-failure redline.
