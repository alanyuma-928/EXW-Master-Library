EXW 101: MOD 4 - LIPID OPTIMIZATION AUDIT

MUFAs, PUFAs, and Performance

🎯 THE MISSION: Phase 1.4

Scenario: A 50-year-old cyclist in Yuma is following a high-fat "Keto" style diet but is consuming 40% of his daily calories from saturated fats.

⚠️ THE HIDDEN TRAP: To further "optimize" his energy, the cyclist plans to replace all whole-food lipids with a proprietary "MCT-Only" oil supplement, claiming it will provide "instant ATP" without the need for carbohydrates or essential fatty acids.

Your mission is to use the Socratic AI Tutor to audit his lipid profile against the **DGA 2025-2030** and identify the morbidity risk of his proposed supplement-only trap. Propose a balanced MUFA/PUFA strategy that supports heart health and systemic inflammation modulation.

🧪 Laboratory/Practical Application

  1. The P-T-C-F Prompt: Draft a prompt for Gemini to identify three plant-based lipid sources available in Yuma that provide a 2:1 ratio of Omega-6 to Omega-3.
  2. The Clinical Audit: Verify the AI's "healthy fat" claims against the **DGA SSoT** chapter on fats and oils.
  3. The Presence Defense: Defend why "Saturated Fat" remains a critical clinical redline even in lean, active athletes.
