### 🏗️ Literacy Pillars (W.11-12.6 Standards)
1. **The Iterative Gate:** Using technology (AI) to produce, publish, and update individual or shared writing products in response to ongoing feedback.
2. **The Defense Rule:** Mandating a 3-sentence human-in-the-loop validation (Unit 1.8) to prove "Presence" and clinical sovereignty over the AI engine.
3. **The Global Pattern:** Integrating and evaluating multiple sources of information (SSoT) to solve complex clinical problems through technologically-driven collaborative writing.
