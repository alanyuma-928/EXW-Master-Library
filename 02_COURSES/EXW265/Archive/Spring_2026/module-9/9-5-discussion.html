9.5: Clinical Peer Audit

The "Near-Real-Time" Teachable Moment

The Discussion Mission

Before submitting your final **Module 9** Clinical Defense, you must clear the **Peer Audit** safety gate. In a professional clinical setting, transparency is the key to institutional agility; we learn by catching errors in a controlled environment before they reach a client.

STEP 1: Initial Post (Due Thursday)

Post the exercise prescription you developed in the **9.4 Tool Box**. Include a brief note on any "Safety Redlines" (like weighted spinal flexion) that you audited using your AI assistant.

STEP 2: Peer Audit (Due Sunday)

Select one peer's post. Act as the **Pilot**. Verify their prescription against the **PAGA 2018 SSoT**. If you find a safety violation or a conflict with the **Lab** data, flag it respectfully as a "Structural Failure."

Note: You cannot see peer posts until you make your initial submission. All grading feedback is posted directly to this thread for leverage and transparency.
