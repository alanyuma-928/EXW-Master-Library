9.1: SSoT - Clinical Safety (Strict)

Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans

The Clinical Baseline

According to the Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) 2018, older adults and those with chronic musculoskeletal conditions must prioritize multicomponent physical activity. This is the "Single Source of Truth" for all Module 9 prescriptions.

Table 9.1: Clinical Safety (Strict) Standards for Older Adults
Domain Requirement Clinical Goal
Balance Training 3+ days per week. Fall prevention and stability.
Muscle Strength 2+ days per week. Maintain bone density/mass.
