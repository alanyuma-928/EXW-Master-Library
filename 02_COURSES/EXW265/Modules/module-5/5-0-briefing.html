Key Concept: Multicomponent Physical Activity

For active older adults, the PAGA 2018 (Chapter 4) requires Multicomponent Physical Activity. This includes:

  • Balance Training (essential for fall prevention).
  • Aerobic Activity (150-300 minutes moderate).
  • Muscle-Strengthening (2+ days per week).
