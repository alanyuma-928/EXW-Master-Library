Key Concept: Guideline Hallucination

A guideline hallucination occurs when the AI misinterprets or "averages" clinical recommendations. Common errors include:

  • Recommending 300 minutes for populations where 150 is the ceiling for safety.
  • Skipping the "Balance Training" requirement for adults 65+.
  • Confusing "Muscle-Strengthening" frequency for those with type 2 diabetes.
