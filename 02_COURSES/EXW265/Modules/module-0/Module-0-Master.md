## EXW 265: AI Usage Policy
**"AI for All" Guiding Principles & EXW265 AI Usage Transparency**

### Arizona Western College "AI for All" Guiding Principles
Arizona Western College (AWC) recognizes Artificial Intelligence (AI) as a powerful, enduring technology that will shape learning, operations, and innovation for years to come.

AI tools—such as ChatGPT, Bard, CoPilot, and others—analyze information, generate content, and support decision-making. AWC's goal is to use AI responsibly to advance our mission of *Transforming Lives through Education and Partnerships* while upholding the College's Strategic Objectives of *Access, Disruption,* and *Excelencia*.

These principles guide the responsible use of AI across all areas of the College—academic, operational, and community.

**Our Guiding Principles**
1.  **Safe and Secure**
    We protect institutional and personal data. All use of AI at AWC must safeguard privacy, prevent misuse, and comply with federal and state laws, including FERPA and HIPAA. We use only trusted, approved tools that protect sensitive information.
2.  **Human-Centered**
    We believe AI should enhance—not replace—human intelligence. AI is used to strengthen learning, creativity, and professional judgment. Final decisions in academics and operations always remain the responsibility of people.
3.  **Ethical and Legal**
    We act with integrity. AI use at AWC must follow laws, institutional policies and procedures, and our professional ethics. We respect intellectual property, academic honesty, and the creative ownership of our students, faculty, and staff.
4.  **Inclusive and Accessible**
    We ensure access to AI tools and learning opportunities. AI must serve everyone—students, employees, and community members—by supporting inclusion, innovation, and opportunity. Training and open communication help close digital and knowledge gaps.
5.  **Transparent and Accountable**
    We communicate clearly when AI is used. Students, Faculty, staff, and departments should be open about how AI supports their work, learning, and operations. Outputs from AI tools must be reviewed for accuracy, bias, and compliance before use.
6.  **Evolving and Responsible**
    We keep pace with change. AI technologies evolve rapidly; our policies and practices will be reviewed regularly to reflect new laws, tools, and institutional needs. Feedback from students, employees, and partners will guide updates to ensure these principles remain relevant and trusted.

### EXW265 AI Usage Transparency
In EXW 265, we embrace the "Centaur Protocol," where you, the student, are the **Architect/Pilot** (providing judgment and critical thinking), and the AI is the **Engine/Clinical Calculator** (providing data processing and analysis).
