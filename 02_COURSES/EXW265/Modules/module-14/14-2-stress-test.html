🛡️ 14.2: REDLINE HARDENING

Clinical Safety Verification & Error Detection

Enforcing Absolute Redlines (LO 2.4)

As the final human firewall, you must demonstrate 100% detection of clinical redline violations. This stress-test simulation introduces randomized "Hidden Traps" where the AI engine intentionally drifts into unsafe territory. You must identify and ground these breaches using the PAGA 2018 (2nd Ed) guidelines.

⚠️ MANDATORY: Initialize Clinical Radar

Before initiating the stress-test, you must anchor the Engine to the **SSoT** to prevent hallucinations during multi-system collisions:

  1. Upload the **PAGA 2018 (2nd Ed)ition Guidelines** and the **PAGA 2nd Edition** to your AI tool.
  2. Verify that the AI recognizes the localized Yuma thermal variables (112°F Peak).

📖 The Adversarial Simulation

In this phase of the **Centaur Protocol**, the AI acts as an 'Adversarial Proctor.' It will attempt to sneak a high-risk intensity recommendation past your audit. Your role is to catch the "Performance over Safety" drift.

🤖 Stress-Test Initialization Prompt

###
HARDEN_PROTOCOL: "Act as the Adversarial Clinical Proctor. I am the Chief Medical Auditor. Generate a multi-morbidity case involving Type 2 Diabetes and Beta-blockers in 105°F heat. Intentionally insert one ACSM Redline violation (e.g., BP > 250/115 or unmonitored glycemic drop). Do not flag the error. My goal is to detect and ground the simulation based on SSoT evidence."
###

🧪 Laboratory: Redline Detection Audit

1. The P-T-C-F Audit: Using the case generated above, identify the specific ACSM redline that was violated. You must cite the chapter and page logic for the 12th Edition.

2. The Clinical Audit: Write a 150-word critique of why the AI prioritized 'aerobic gain' over 'patient safety.' Specifically address how the medication blunted the heart rate response, masking the physiological stress.

3. The Presence Defense: Submit a 150-word Verbatim Text Defense justifying your decision to "Ground the Sortie." In your own clinical voice, explain why "Verification is the only currency of a Chief Auditor" in the Yuma/AWC Resident network.

⚠️ Safety Gate: Redline Detection is binary. If you miss one breach, the audit is a total failure. Trust is a liability.
