## Course Description
This course provides an overview of the principles of nutrition and their relationship to health and wellness. Topics include the functions of nutrients, dietary guidelines, energy balance, weight management, and the role of nutrition in disease prevention. Students will also explore contemporary issues and controversies in nutrition.

## Learning Outcomes
Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:
1.  Describe the functions of macronutrients and micronutrients in the human body.
2.  Apply the Dietary Guidelines for Americans to create a healthy eating plan.
3.  Analyze the relationship between energy balance and weight management.
4.  Evaluate the role of nutrition in the prevention of chronic diseases.
5.  Critically analyze nutrition information from various sources.

## Required Textbook
