# 🕵️‍♂️ Phase 2: Technical Audit (Hallucination Hunt)
## Course: EXW265 | Population: Pregnancy & Postpartum

### 🎯 The Mission
In this "Guided Learning" lab, you are the **Lead Clinical Auditor**. A junior trainer used a generic AI to generate an exercise prescription for a pregnant client. Your job is to perform a "Hallucination Hunt" to ensure the plan meets the **PAGA (2018)** and **ACOG** safety standards.

---

### 🔬 The "Draft" Prescription (AI-Generated)
**Client Profile:** 28 years old, 24 weeks pregnant (2nd Trimester), previously active.
**Proposed Plan:**
1.  **Warm-up:** 10 minutes of supine (lying on back) abdominal stretching.
2.  **Aerobic:** 30 minutes of jogging at 85% Max Heart Rate.
3.  **Resistance:** High-intensity power cleans and box jumps.
4.  **Environment:** Outdoor boot camp in Yuma, AZ (Current temp: 102°F).
