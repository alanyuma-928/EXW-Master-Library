### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article related to **Adaptive Exercise for Seniors** or **Environmental Barriers in Fitness**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform an "Inclusion Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the inclusion barrier or adaptive success story?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with PAGA Adaptive Standards?
   * **Solve:** How does this research help you transition from the Medical Model to the Social Model?

&nbsp;
