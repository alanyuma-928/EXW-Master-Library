## 📡 The Vulnerability Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Thermal Ceiling</strong><br>
  For seniors and adaptive populations, the environment is a binary safety gate. We audit the "Thermal Ceiling" to prevent heat-related clinical collapses.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Vulnerability Pillars (PAGA Standards)
1. **The Thermoregulation Rule:** Seniors have a reduced ability to dissipate heat due to decreased sweat gland sensitivity and skin blood flow.
2. **The Medication Redline:** Identifying medications (e.g., beta-blockers, diuretics) that impair the body's cooling response.
3. **The Humidity Gate:** Understanding how vapor pressure (humidity) lowers the effective Thermal Ceiling in the desert Southwest.
