## 📡 The Population Audit
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Adaptive Mandate</strong><br>
  We do not generalize health. We audit the specific physiological realities of seniors and those with chronic disabilities.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Population Pillars (PAGA Standards)
1. **The Senior Rule:** Chronic conditions (T2D, HTN) are the norm, not the exception. We solve for multicomponent health.
2. **The Adaptive Rule:** Modifying the movement pattern to accommodate functional limitations while maintaining PAGA intensity.
3. **The Independence Rule:** Every intervention must directly support Activities of Daily Living (ADLs) and fall prevention.

&nbsp;

### 📂 Operational Sequence
* **The Pattern:** Identifying the specific functional gap (e.g., balance, mobility, strength).
* **The Rule:** Applying the PAGA "Relative Intensity" scale (0-10) for sedentary adults.
* **The Solve:** Architecting a "Silver Sneakers" protocol that bridges the inactivity gap safely.
