        <p>
            Your mission is to design an <strong>Adaptive Aerobic</strong> program for a client 6 months post-stroke with left-side hemiparesis.
        </p>

        <div style="background-color: #ffffff; padding: 25px; border: 2px solid #003366; border-radius: 4px; margin: 25px 0;">
            <h4 style="color: #003366; margin-top: 0;">Prescription Template (Stroke Recovery)</h4>
            <ul style="margin-bottom: 0;">
                <li><strong>Mode:</strong> Recumbent Stepper (NuStep) or Adaptive Cycling.</li>
                <li><strong>Protocol:</strong> High-repetition, low-resistance movements to drive motor relearning.</li>
                <li><strong>Duration:</strong> Gradual build to 150 minutes, utilizing 10-minute bouts to manage central fatigue.</li>
                <li><strong>Safety Check:</strong> Ensure the unaffected limb is not overcompensating to the point of structural strain.</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
