### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article from the last 24 months related to **Parkinson's Motor Control**, **MS Fatigue Management**, or **Vestibular Rehabilitation**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform a "Neuro-Sensory Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the specific neurological trend or motor outcome identified?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with the ACSM 12th Ed standards?
   * **Solve:** How does this research improve the accuracy of your neuro-sensory audits?

&nbsp;

### 🌟 10/10 Student Exemplar (Neuro-Sensory Research Audit)
> "**Source:** Williams, R. et al. (2024). Efficacy of High-Frequency Auditory Cueing in MS Gait. *MSSE*.  
> **Pattern:** Rhythmic auditory stimulation resulted in a 12% improvement in gait velocity for MS patients with sensory deficits.  
> **Rule:** ACSM 12th Ed mandates sensory integration (Unit 4.3) for populations with motor control failures.  
> **Solve:** To maintain motor sovereignty, all neuro-sensory audits for MS patients must evaluate the potential for auditory cueing to bypass sensory-motor logic collapses."
