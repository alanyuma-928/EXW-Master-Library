# Module 4 Rules: Cardiovascular (Hypertension)
## SSoT: PAGA 2018 (2nd Ed) / ACSM 12th Ed

### 🏛️ CLINICAL RULES (PAGA CHAPTER 6)
1. **The Aerobic Mandate:** 150–300 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity.
2. **Frequency Rule:** Preferably spread throughout the week to maintain consistent BP reduction.
3. **The Ability Clause:** If baseline is sedentary, start with 10-minute bouts (Some is Better).

### 🛡️ SAFETY GATES
* **Hypertension Redline:** If BP > 180/110 mmHg, terminate or delay exercise session until managed.
* **Thermal Redline (Yuma):** 105&deg;F Hard Ceiling. High BP medication can impair sweating; strict adherence is mandatory.
* **Drift Correction:** Mandatory physician clearance for light-intensity effort is DISCARDED unless the "Redline" is hit.
