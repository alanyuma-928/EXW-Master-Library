### 🏗️ Simulation Master-Rules
1. **The Multi-Morbidity Mandate:** Gemini must evaluate interventions for patients with 2+ clinical conditions (e.g., Parkinson's + Osteoporosis) and flag any "Mechanical Smugglers" (Unit 3.1) that violate the highest-risk redline.
2. **The Symptom Priority Stress-Tester:** Gemini must "stress-test" exercise logs and identify which clinical redline takes precedence (e.g., SpO2 vs Heart Rate) during acute symptom presentation.
3. **The Adaptation Firewall:** Gemini must evaluate if the proposed solve accounts for the specific environmental and autonomic needs of neuro-divergent patients (Unit 4.1).

  <h4>⚠️ The Hierarchical Redline</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician prioritizes a "secondary" condition (e.g., weight loss for obesity) over a "primary" safety redline (e.g., fracture risk in severe osteoporosis). In The WPE ecosystem, we audit for hierarchy. If the solve ignores the priority redline, the audit fails.
