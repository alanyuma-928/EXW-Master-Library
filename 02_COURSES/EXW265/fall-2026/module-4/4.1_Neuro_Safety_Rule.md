## 📡 The Neuro-Safety Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Neurological Audit</strong><br>
  Neurological integrity is the primary metric of motor control sovereignty. We audit movement patterns and autonomic responses to identify "Neurological Redlines" and ensure the safety of exercise interventions for neuro-divergent populations.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Neurological Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Autonomic Gate:** Auditing for blunted heart rate responses and thermoregulatory failures in patients with Multiple Sclerosis (MS) or Parkinson's Disease.
2. **The Fatigue Rule:** Mandating a "Fatigue Audit" to prevent symptom exacerbation (Uhtoff's Phenomenon) in MS patients during high-intensity interventions.
3. **The Motor Pattern:** Auditing for gait instability, tremors, and rigidity to adjust the complexity and environment of the exercise task.

  <h4>⚠️ The Heat-Sensitivity Redline</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician prescribes high-intensity exercise in a warm environment for a patient with MS without auditing for heat sensitivity. In The WPE ecosystem, we audit for temperature. If the solve ignores the Uhtoff's redline, the audit fails.
