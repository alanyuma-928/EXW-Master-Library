The Centaur Protocol

Professional Philosophy: Human Intuition + AI Precision

The Clinical Safety Gate

In EXW 265, working with special populations demands absolute precision. We utilize the Centaur Approach: a methodology where the human provides the clinical oversight and identifies contraindications (the "Head"), while the AI provides rapid auditing of clinical safety markers (the "Hooves").

This Protocol ensures you maintain professional control over high-risk exercise prescriptions while utilizing AI as a high-fidelity secondary auditor.

Rules of Engagement

Every interaction within this clinical space must adhere to these three standards:

01. The Auditor

You are the final Auditor. You are legally responsible for client safety. You must audit every AI-generated suggestion against the SSoT (PAGA 2018).

02. Socratic Scaffolding

AI is your Clinical Scaffolder. Use it to verify your diagnosis-to-intensity logic—not to bypass the clinical reasoning required for special populations.

03. Data Integrity

Wrap all medical diagnoses, mobility restrictions, or biometric inputs in the ### Delimiter Protocol. This protects the integrity of your clinical safety audits.
