The Single Source of Truth (SSoT)

Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) 2018

Operational Authority

When prescribing exercise for individuals with chronic conditions or unique physiological needs, safety is the primary professional mandate. For EXW 265, our exclusive Single Source of Truth (SSoT) is the Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA), 2nd Edition (2018).

These federal standards provide the specific clinical Rules for adjusting intensity, frequency, and type of movement based on clinical conditions. Any audit performed in this course—whether for a case study or a clinical simulation—must be anchored strictly in these guidelines.

Technical Requirement: The ### Protocol
