## I. Course Overview (The SSoT)
**Single Source of Truth (SSoT):** This course is strictly anchored to the **Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) 2018 (2nd Edition)**. We focus on the evidence-based relationship between regular physical activity and its impact on longevity, morbidity, and quality of life for unique populations.

**Course Goal:** Develop a clinical understanding of fitness programming conducive to a healthy lifestyle for specific populations, enhancing total wellness at any point in the lifespan.

## II. The Mission Loop Framework
We utilize the **Pattern / Rule / Solve** architecture to ensure clinical safety and efficacy:

* **Pattern (Identification):** Demonstrating pre-activity screening processes to determine appropriate activities for participation (AWC Outcome 2.1).
* **Rule (Standardization):** Explaining theories of exercise and nutrition across all life stages and discussing how disease states are affected by exercise (AWC Outcome 2.2, 2.3).
* **Solve (Application):** Designing appropriate, individualized exercise programs that account for a client's specific abilities and physiological challenges (AWC Outcome 2.4).
