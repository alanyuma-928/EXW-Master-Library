# EXW265 Course Architecture: Exercise for Special Populations

## Single Source of Truth (SSoT)
The **SSoT** for EXW265 is strictly the **PAGA 2018 (2nd Ed)**. External clinical standards (e.g., ACSM) are only utilized when they align with PAGA mandates.

## Course Framework: The Mission Loop
All assignments, labs, and clinical audits must follow the **Mission Loop** framework:
1. **Pattern:** Identify the demographic, clinical status, and current activity level of the individual.
2. **Rule:** Apply the specific federal guidelines from the PAGA 2018.
3. **Solve:** Design an evidence-based exercise prescription or nutritional intervention that prioritizes safety and adherence.

## Standardized Inclusive Taxonomy & Clinical Focus
To maintain institutional agility and pedagogical integrity, the following taxonomy is enforced across all course materials and AI-driven tools (including the Clinical Audit Box).

| Legacy Term | Standardized Inclusive Taxonomy | Clinical/Pedagogical Focus | SSoT Reference |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Youth** | **Children and Adolescents** | Bone-strengthening and muscle-strengthening growth phases. | PAGA Chapter 3 |
| **Pregnancy** | **Prenatal & Postpartum** | Full cycle of maternal health and fetal development. | PAGA Lifespan Approach |
| **Wheelchair** | **Adaptive / Limited Mobility** | Spinal cord injuries, cerebral palsy, or temporary mobility restrictions. | PAGA Adults with Disabilities |
| **Senior Citizen** | **Older Adults** | Multicomponent physical activity (balance, aerobic, and muscle-strengthening). | PAGA Chapter 5 |
| **Handicapped** | **Accessible / Adaptive** | Prioritizing WCAG 2.1 AA and OER accessibility standards. | OER / WCAG 2.1 AA |

## Instructional Design Standards
* **Mobile-First:** All content must be optimized for mobile responsiveness using Markdown.
* **Accessibility:** Maintain WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance with a minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio (Preferred 9:1 for AWC Navy on Creme).
* **OER/ZTC:** Ensure zero-cost barrier to entry by using Open Educational Resources.
* **Delimiter Protocol:** All clinical case data, client profiles, or medical inputs must be wrapped within triple-hashes (###) delimiters for AI auditing.

## Grading & Evaluation
* **Mastery Scale (10/7/0):**
    * **10 (Exemplary):** Full clinical adherence to SSoT.
    * **7 (Competent):** Minor recalibration needed.
    * **0 (Recalibrate):** Safety violation or non-adherence to PAGA rules.
