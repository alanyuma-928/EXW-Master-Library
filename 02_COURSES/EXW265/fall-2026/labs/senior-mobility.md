## 📡 The Safety Mission
Welcome to the Senior Mobility & Safety Audit. In this lab, you will use the **DTK Diagnostic Testing Kernel** to evaluate an older adult's structural sovereignty. You must identify "Safety Smugglers" and clinical redlines in mobility assessments.

### 🏗️ Mission Constraints (The Redlines)
1. **The PAR-Q+ Mandate:** Every senior assessment MUST start with the **PAR-Q+ 2024** screening. If any "Yes" response is recorded, medical clearance is the mandatory safety gate.
2. **The Falls-Risk Rule:** You must audit the subject's balance data (e.g., Single-Leg Stance) to identify "Gravity Smugglers" and high-risk mobility logic collapses.
3. **The Safety Box Rule:** Binary Stop/Go Logic. If the subject fails the PAR-Q+ or shows acute hypertensive signs, the mission is terminated.
