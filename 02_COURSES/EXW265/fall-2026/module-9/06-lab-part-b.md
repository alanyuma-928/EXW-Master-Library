            Your mission is to design a <strong>Pulmonary Conditioning</strong> program for a client with moderate COPD. Your solve must prioritize <strong>Interval Timing</strong>.
        </p>

        <div style="background-color: #FFFDD0; padding: 25px; border: 2px solid #000080; border-radius: 4px; margin: 25px 0;">
            <h4 style="color: #000080; margin-top: 0;">Prescription Template (Pulmonary)</h4>
            <ul style="margin-bottom: 0;">
                <li><strong>Mode:</strong> Walking or Cycling (Lower-body focus to minimize upper-body metabolic demand).</li>
                <li><strong>Intervals:</strong> 2-3 minutes of activity followed by 1-2 minutes of rest/diaphragmatic breathing.</li>
                <li><strong>Technique:</strong> Integration of "Pursed-Lip Breathing" during active bouts to manage CO2 retention.</li>
                <li><strong>Safety Check:</strong> Continuous SpO2 monitoring via pulse oximeter.</li>
            </ul>
