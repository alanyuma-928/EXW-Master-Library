    <section id="clinical-rules">
        <h3 style="color: #003366; border-left: 5px solid #003366; padding-left: 15px;">Pulmonary Guardrails</h3>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The 90% O2 Gate:</strong> Exercise should be reduced or terminated if arterial oxygen saturation (SpO2) drops below 88-90% (SSoT: ACSM/PAGA).</li>
            <li><strong>The Borg Dyspnea Scale:</strong> Utilize a 0–10 scale specifically for breathlessness. Missions should stay within the 3–5 range (Moderate to Strong) to build conditioning without triggering panic.</li>
            <li><strong>The Supplemental Rule:</strong> Clients using supplemental oxygen must maintain their prescribed flow rates during all physical activity.</li>
        </ul>
        
        <div style="background-color: #fef2f2; padding: 20px; border-left: 5px solid #dc2626;">
            <strong>Redline Warning:</strong> Environmental factors (Dust, Heat, Humidity) are critical triggers for COPD and may require shifting missions to indoor, climate-controlled environments.
