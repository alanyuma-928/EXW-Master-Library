  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Social Model Audit</strong><br>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Evolutionary Pillars
1. **The Medical Model:** Viewing disability as a deficit to be cured or corrected (The Legacy Perspective).
2. **The ADA Redline:** The 1990 Americans with Disabilities Act and the codification of accessibility as a legal mandate.
3. **The Social Model Turn:** The modern perspective that disability is caused by societal barriers, not physiological differences.
