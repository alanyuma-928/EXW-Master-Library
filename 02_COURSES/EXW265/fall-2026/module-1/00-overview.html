Operational Objective

By the end of this Module, you will be able to audit physical activity prescriptions for individuals with metabolic conditions (Type 2 Diabetes) using the SSoT (PAGA 2018). You will identify medical Patterns and apply the federal safety Rules to Solve for a low-risk, high-efficacy movement plan.
