## 🧭 The Clinical Mission
Welcome to Phase 1 of Clinical Exercise Physiology. In this module, we establish the foundational protocols for auditing specialized populations, environmental redlines, and historical evolution in kinesiology.

### 🏗️ Learning Scaffolds
1. **Defining Populations (Unit 1.1):** Identifying clinical and specialized population categories.
2. **Functional Calibration (Unit 1.2):** Auditing baseline movement patterns against PAGA standards.
3. **Thermal Ceiling Audit (Unit 1.3):** Evaluating environmental barriers in Southwest regional contexts.
4. **Phase Mastery (Unit 1.8):** Defending your foundational clinical logic.
