Phase 2: Applying the Dual-Authority SSoT

Operational Authority

Working with Special Populations requires a dual-audit of clinical and environmental risk. For Mr. Hernandez, you must apply the safety gates defined in PAGA 2018 Chapter 6 and the Yuma Thermal Safety Gate.

Individuals with chronic conditions often have impaired thermoregulation. In the Yuma environment, the temperature "Redline" is an absolute safety gate that overrides all clinical exercise goals to prevent thermal failure.

Rules for Chronic Condition Audit

PAGA Chapter 6 and Local Thermal safety gates for auditing exercise prescriptions for individuals with chronic conditions.
Risk Category PAGA / Local Rule
