### 🏗️ Remodeling Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Strain Gate:** Mandating a specific magnitude and rate of strain (e.g., dynamic loading) to trigger osteoblast activity and structural sovereignty.
2. **The Vector Rule:** Auditing for varied loading directions (e.g., multi-planar movement) to identify the "Diversity Pattern" that maximizes whole-bone remodeling sovereignty.
3. **The Rest Pattern:** Mandating "Recovery Gates" (e.g., 24-48 hours) to allow for cellular adaptation and bypass the overtraining-fragility redline.
