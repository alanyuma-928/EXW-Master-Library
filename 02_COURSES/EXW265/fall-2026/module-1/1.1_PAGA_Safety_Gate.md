&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Safety Pillars (PAGA Standards)
1. **The Readiness Gate:** Identifying the specific screening requirements for individuals with chronic conditions or disabilities before starting an exercise program.
2. **The Progression Rule:** Mandating a "Start Low and Go Slow" approach to intensity and volume to prevent musculoskeletal and cardiovascular logic collapses.
3. **The Symptom Pattern:** Auditing for signs of exercise intolerance (e.g., chest pain, dizziness, excessive fatigue) during the initial phases of movement.

  <h4>⚠️ The Overload Redline</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician prescribes a high-intensity overload for a patient with a chronic condition without auditing for their specific clinical redlines (e.g., blood pressure, glycemic control). In The WPE ecosystem, safety is the first gate. If the solve ignores the PAGA safety redline, the audit fails.
