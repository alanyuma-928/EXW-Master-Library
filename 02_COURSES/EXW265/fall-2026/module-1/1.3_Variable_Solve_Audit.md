### 🏗️ Bone-Joint Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Loading Gate (Osteoporosis):** Mandating weight-bearing aerobic exercise and resistance training (Unit 5.1/5.4) to maximize bone mineral density (BMD) and structural sovereignty.
2. **The Joint-Sparing Rule (Osteoarthritis):** Auditing for low-impact modalities (e.g., swimming, cycling, rucking with light load) to identify the "Sweet Spot" of joint lubrication without triggering inflammatory logic collapses.
3. **The Variable Pattern:** Prioritizing exercises that improve balance and flexibility (Module 6) alongside loading to bypass the fall-risk redline and maintain functional sovereignty.
