### 🏗️ Simulation Master-Rules
1. **The Loading Mandate:** Gemini must evaluate exercise plans and identify if the loading gate (weight-bearing/resistance) is appropriate for the patient's BMD status to maintain structural sovereignty (Unit 1.3).
2. **The Joint Stress-Tester:** Gemini must "stress-test" movement patterns and identify risks for the high-impact redline or inflammatory responses in osteoarthritis audits (Unit 1.3).
3. **The Balance Firewall:** Gemini must evaluate if the proposed solve accounts for fall-risk reduction through integrated balance and flexibility patterns (Module 6).
