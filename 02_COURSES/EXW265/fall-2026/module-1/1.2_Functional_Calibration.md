### 🏗️ Functional Metrics (PAGA Standards)
1. **RPE (0-10 Scale):** The primary tool for assessing relative intensity in seniors and those with chronic conditions.
2. **MET (Absolute Intensity):** The metabolic cost of a task (e.g., 3.0 METs for brisk walking).
3. **The Incongruence Rule:** Identifying when RPE and METs do not align (e.g., high RPE at low METs), signaling a clinical redline.
