## 📡 The Hemodynamic Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Pressure Audit</strong><br>
  Blood pressure is the primary metric of cardiovascular risk. We audit hemodynamic patterns to identify clinical redlines and ensure the safety of movement interventions for hypertensive populations.

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Pathophysiological Pillars (Clinical Standards)
1. **The Stage 1 Gate:** Identifying the AHA/ACC 2017 threshold for Stage 1 Hypertension (Systolic: 130-139 mmHg or Diastolic: 80-89 mmHg).
2. **The Aerobic Rule:** Auditing the PAGA mandate for 150-300 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity as a non-pharmacological solve for blood pressure reduction.
3. **The Resistance Pattern:** Understanding the role of muscle-strengthening activity (Unit 1.2) in improving vascular resistance and metabolic sovereignty.
