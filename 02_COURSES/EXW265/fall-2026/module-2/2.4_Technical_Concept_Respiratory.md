## 📡 The Respiratory Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Respiratory Audit</strong><br>
  Gas exchange is the primary metric of metabolic sovereignty. We audit respiratory patterns (FVC, FEV1) to identify obstructive and restrictive redlines and ensure the safety of movement interventions for pulmonary populations.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Pathophysiological Pillars (Clinical Standards)
1. **The Obstruction Gate:** Identifying the specific FEV1/FVC ratios that indicate obstructive lung diseases like Asthma or COPD.
2. **The Ventilation Rule:** Auditing for exercise-induced bronchospasm (EIB) and the need for rescue inhalers during high-intensity interventions.
3. **The Dyspnea Pattern:** Using the Dyspnea Scale (0-10 or 1-4) to monitor and audit the patient's subjective breathing difficulty.
