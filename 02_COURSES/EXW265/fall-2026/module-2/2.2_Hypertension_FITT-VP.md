### 🏗️ Prescription Pillars (ACSM/PAGA Standards)
1. **The Frequency Gate:** Prescribing aerobic activity on most, preferably all, days of the week (minimum 5-7 days) to capitalize on post-exercise hypotension.
2. **The Intensity Rule:** Focusing on moderate intensity (40-59% VO2R/HRR or RPE 12-13) as the clinical sweet spot for blood pressure reduction.
3. **The Volume/Progression Pattern:** Targeting 150-300 minutes per week (PAGA mandate) with a conservative "Step-Wise" progression to avoid hemodynamic redlines.
