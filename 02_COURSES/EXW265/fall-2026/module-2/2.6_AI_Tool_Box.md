### 🏗️ Simulation Master-Rules
1. **The Beta-Blocker Mandate:** Gemini must evaluate hemodynamic responses for Beta-Blocker compliance and flag any "Chronotropic Smugglers" if the blunted heart rate response is ignored.
2. **The ACE-Inhibitor Stress-Tester:** Gemini must "stress-test" post-exercise blood pressure logs and identify risks for hypotension or syncopal collapses in hypertensive patients.
3. **The Pulmonary Firewall:** Gemini must evaluate if the proposed prescription for a respiratory patient (Unit 2.4) accounts for SpO2 desaturation and rescue inhaler timing.
