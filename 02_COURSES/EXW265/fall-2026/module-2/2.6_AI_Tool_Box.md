### 🏗️ Simulation Master-Rules
1. **The Med-Logic Mandate:** Gemini must identify all antihypertensive medications in a profile and flag their specific exercise interactions (e.g., blunted HR, PEH risk).
2. **The FITT-VP Stress-Tester:** Gemini must "stress-test" a proposed exercise prescription by identifying any parameter (Frequency, Intensity, etc.) that violates ACSM safety standards.
3. **The RPE Firewall:** Gemini must force the use of RPE-based monitoring if a Beta-Blocker is detected in the patient's medication linter.
