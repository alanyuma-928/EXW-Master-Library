### 🏗️ Mission Task
1. **The Post:** Share your **Hemodynamic Risk Table** (from Unit 2.1). Identify one "Pressure Redline" or "Medication Interaction" you discovered (e.g., Stage 2 Hypertension or Beta-Blocker blunting).
2. **The Audit (Reply to ONE peer):** Perform a "Clinical Sovereignty Audit" on a classmate's post. 
   * Did they correctly apply the **AHA/ACC 2017 BP Gates**?
   * Is their **FITT-VP Prescription** (Unit 2.2) safe for the identified medication profile (Unit 2.3)?
   * Identify one potential **Valsalva Redline** or **RPE Logic Collapse** they may have overlooked.

&nbsp;

### 🌟 10/10 Student Exemplar (Clinical Peer Audit)
> "**Audit of John Doe's Clinical Solve:** I have evaluated your hypertension prescription. While your aerobic volume (150 min) meets the PAGA gate, your use of the Karvonen Formula for intensity is a **Logic Collapse** because the patient is on a Beta-Blocker (Metoprolol). Pattern: Blunted HR response. Rule: ACSM 12th Ed mandates RPE-based monitoring for Beta-Blockers. Solve: Recalibrate the intensity to RPE 12-13 to maintain clinical sovereignty."
