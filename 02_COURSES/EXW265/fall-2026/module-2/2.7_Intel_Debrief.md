### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article from the last 24 months related to **Hypertension Pathophysiology** or **Exercise Prescription (FITT-VP) for CVD**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform a "Clinical Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the specific hemodynamic trend or medication interaction identified?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with the AHA/ACC 2017 standards?
   * **Solve:** How does this research improve the safety of your clinical hand-offs?

> "**Source:** Miller, K. et al. (2024). Post-Exercise Hypotension in ACE-Inhibitor Populations. *Circulation*.  
> **Pattern:** ACE-Inhibitor usage significantly increases the risk of symptomatic hypotension during the 30-60 minute recovery window.  
> **Rule:** ACSM 12th Ed mandates a prolonged cool-down for patients on vasodilators to prevent syncope.  
> **Solve:** To maintain clinical sovereignty, all prescriptions for patients on Lisinopril must include a mandatory 15-minute active cool-down to bypass the PEH redline."
