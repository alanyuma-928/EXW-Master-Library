## 🧭 The Clinical Mission
Welcome to Phase 2 of the Clinical Exercise Physiology audit. In this module, we transition from foundational history to the pathophysiology of the cardiovascular system, specifically focusing on **Hypertension** and the clinical management of pressure redlines.

### 🏗️ Learning Scaffolds
1. **The Hemodynamic Audit (Unit 2.1):** Identifying Stage 1 and Stage 2 hypertension thresholds.
2. **The Prescription Audit (Unit 2.2):** Mastering the FITT-VP parameters for blood pressure reduction.
3. **The Interaction Audit (Unit 2.3):** Evaluating the clinical impact of antihypertensive medications.
4. **The Phase Mastery (Unit 2.8):** Defending your clinical hand-off to a supervising physician.
