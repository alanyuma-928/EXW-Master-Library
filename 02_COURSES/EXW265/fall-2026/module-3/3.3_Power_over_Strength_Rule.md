## 📡 The Power Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Sarcopenia Audit</strong><br>
  Muscular power (force × velocity) is the primary metric of functional sovereignty and fall prevention in older adults. We audit resistance prescriptions to identify "Velocity Failures" and ensure the safety of power-based interventions.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Power Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Velocity Gate:** Prioritizing high-velocity movements (e.g., explosive concentric phase) at 30-60% of 1-RM to improve power output.
2. **The Sarcopenia Rule:** Mandating multi-joint resistance exercises at least 2 days per week to combat muscle mass loss and functional decline.
3. **The Functional Pattern:** Aligning power tasks with activities of daily living (ADLs) such as rising from a chair or climbing stairs to ensure movement sovereignty.
