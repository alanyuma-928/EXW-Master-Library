## 📡 The Joint Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Joint Audit</strong><br>
  Joint integrity is the primary metric of movement sovereignty for arthritic and orthopedic populations. We audit mechanical loading patterns (Non-Weight Bearing vs Weight-Bearing) to identify pain redlines and ensure the safety of movement interventions.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Joint Pillars (Clinical Standards)
1. **The Buoyancy Gate:** Prioritizing aquatic exercise or cycling to reduce mechanical stress on weight-bearing joints (e.g., knees, hips) during acute flare-ups.
2. **The Pain Rule:** Auditing for post-exercise pain that lasts more than 2 hours, indicating a logic collapse in the exercise dosage.
3. **The ROM Pattern:** Prioritizing full range of motion (Unit 4.4) over high-impact loading to maintain joint lubrication and function.

  <h4>⚠️ The High-Impact Redline</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician prescribes high-impact running or jumping for a patient with severe osteoarthritis of the knee. In The WPE ecosystem, we audit for joint safety. If the solve ignores the mechanical loading redline, the audit fails.
