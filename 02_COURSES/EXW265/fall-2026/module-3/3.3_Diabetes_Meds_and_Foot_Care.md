## 📡 The Sensory Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Sensory Audit</strong><br>
  Clinical exercise for diabetes requires a dual audit: metabolic response to medications and physical protection of insensitive extremities. We audit for neuropathy and footwear integrity to ensure patient sovereignty.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Sensory Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Insulin Gate:** Identifying the timing and injection site of insulin to avoid rapid absorption and hypoglycemia during exercise (Unit 3.2).
2. **The Neuropathy Rule:** Auditing for Peripheral Neuropathy (numbness/loss of sensation) which increases the risk of undetected foot injuries and ulcers.
3. **The Footwear Pattern:** Mandating proper footwear (e.g., moisture-wicking socks, well-fitting athletic shoes) and daily foot inspections as a non-negotiable safety solve.
