## 📡 The Thermal Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Thermal Audit</strong><br>
  Environmental heat is a clinical redline for obese populations. We audit thermoregulatory patterns to identify thermal ceilings and ensure the safety of movement interventions in high-heat Southwest regional contexts.

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Thermal Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Insulation Gate:** Identifying how excess adipose tissue acts as an insulator, hindering heat dissipation and increasing the risk of exertional heat illness.
2. **The Hydration Rule:** Auditing for increased sweat rates and electrolyte loss in obese individuals to maintain hemodynamic and metabolic sovereignty.
3. **The WBGT Pattern:** Using the Wet-Bulb Globe Temperature (WBGT) to set environmental redlines for outdoor physical activity sessions in Yuma.
