&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Skeletal Pillars (Clinical Standards)
1. **The Impact Gate:** Mandating weight-bearing aerobic activities and high-intensity resistance training to stimulate osteoblast activity.
2. **The Fracture Rule:** Auditing for activities that involve excessive trunk flexion, twisting, or high-impact jumping that increase the risk of vertebral or hip fractures.
3. **The Balance Pattern:** Integrating neuromotor tasks (Unit 4.4) to improve coordination and prevent falls, which are the primary cause of osteoporotic fractures.
