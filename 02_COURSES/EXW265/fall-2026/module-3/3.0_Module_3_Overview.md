## 🧭 The Clinical Mission
Welcome to Phase 3 of the Clinical Exercise Physiology audit. In this module, we transition to the pathophysiology of the metabolic system, specifically focusing on **Diabetes Mellitus** and the clinical management of glycemic and sensory redlines.

### 🏗️ Learning Scaffolds
1. **The Glucose Audit (Unit 3.1 & 3.2):** Mastering glycemic safety and the 15/15 Rule.
2. **The Sensory Audit (Unit 3.3):** Evaluating neuropathy and footwear integrity.
3. **The Metabolic Double-Redline (Unit 3.5):** Peer-auditing complex metabolic interactions.
4. **The Crisis Mastery (Unit 3.8):** Defending your crisis management logic.
