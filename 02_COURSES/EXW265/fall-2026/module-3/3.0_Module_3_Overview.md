## 🧭 The Clinical Mission
Welcome to Phase 3 of the Special Populations audit. In this module, we transition to **Musculoskeletal and Geriatric Pathophysiology**, specifically focusing on **Osteoporosis**, **Arthritis**, and **Sarcopenia** management.

2. **The Joint Audit (Unit 3.2):** Evaluating non-weight bearing solves and pain redlines.
3. **The Power Audit (Unit 3.3):** Mastering high-velocity training and fall prevention.
4. **The Functional Mastery (Unit 3.8):** Defending your functional programming logic.
