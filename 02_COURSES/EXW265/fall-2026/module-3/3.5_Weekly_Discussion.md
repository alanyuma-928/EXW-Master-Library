### 🏗️ Mission Task
1. **The Post:** Share your **Power Audit Table** (from Unit 3.3). Identify one "Fall Risk Redline" you discovered (e.g., a failure in the 30-second chair stand test or a lack of neuromotor challenge in the proposed prescription).
2. **The Audit (Reply to ONE peer):** Perform a "Functional Sovereignty Audit" on a classmate's post. 
   * Did they correctly apply the **ACSM Neuromotor Rule** (Unit 3.3)?
   * Is their **Impact/Non-Weight Bearing Solve** (Units 3.1/3.2) technically sound?
   * Identify one potential **Fracture Redline** or **Velocity Logic Collapse** they may have overlooked.
