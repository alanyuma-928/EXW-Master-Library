### 🏗️ Mission Task
1. **The Post:** Share your **Glycemic Risk Table** (from Unit 3.2). Identify one "Metabolic Double-Redline" you discovered (e.g., Hypoglycemia risk combined with Peripheral Neuropathy).
2. **The Audit (Reply to ONE peer):** Perform a "Metabolic Sovereignty Audit" on a classmate's post. 
   * Did they correctly apply the **15/15 Safety Rule**?
   * Is their **FITT-VP Prescription** (Unit 3.2) safe for the identified neuropathy profile (Unit 3.3)?
   * Identify one potential **Ketone Redline** or **Insulin Timing Logic Collapse** they may have overlooked.

### 🌟 10/10 Student Exemplar (Metabolic Peer Audit)
> "**Audit of Jane Smith's Metabolic Solve:** I have evaluated your diabetes prescription. While your '15/15 Rule' implementation is correct for the 95 mg/dL reading, your choice of high-impact treadmill running is a **Structural Logic Collapse**. Pattern: Severe peripheral neuropathy identified in the sensory audit. Rule: ACSM 12th Ed mandates low-impact modalities (e.g., cycling/swimming) for patients with loss of sensation to prevent ulcers. Solve: Recalibrate the 'Type' to a non-weight-bearing modality to maintain metabolic sovereignty."
