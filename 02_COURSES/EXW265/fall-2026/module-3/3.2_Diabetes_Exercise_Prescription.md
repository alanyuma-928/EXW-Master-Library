## 📡 The Metabolic Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Glucose Audit</strong><br>
  Glucose management is the primary metric of metabolic risk. We audit glycemic patterns to identify clinical redlines and ensure the safety of movement interventions for diabetic populations.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Prescription Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Frequency Gate:** Prescribing aerobic activity on at least 3 days per week, with no more than 2 consecutive days without activity to maintain insulin sensitivity.
2. **The Intensity Rule:** Focusing on moderate (40-59% VO2R/HRR) to vigorous intensity (60-89% VO2R/HRR) for optimal glycemic control.
3. **The 15/15 Safety Rule:** Auditing for hypoglycemia. If pre-exercise blood glucose is <100 mg/dL, the patient must consume 15g of fast-acting carbohydrates and re-test after 15 minutes.
