## 📡 The Metabolic Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Metabolic Audit</strong><br>
  Insulin sensitivity is the primary metric of metabolic health. We audit glycemic patterns to identify clinical redlines and ensure the safety of movement interventions for Type 1 and Type 2 diabetic populations.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Pathophysiological Pillars (Clinical Standards)
1. **The Type 1 Gate:** Identifying absolute insulin deficiency and the critical risk of exercise-induced hypoglycemia.
2. **The Type 2 Rule:** Auditing for insulin resistance and the role of muscular contraction (GLUT-4 translocation) in non-insulin-mediated glucose uptake.
3. **The HbA1c Pattern:** Understanding the 3-month glycemic average as a metric for long-term metabolic sovereignty.

  <h4>⚠️ The Ketone Redline</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician permits vigorous activity during a state of uncontrolled hyperglycemia (>300 mg/dL) or when ketones are present. In The WPE ecosystem, we audit for diabetic ketoacidosis (DKA) risks. If the solve ignores the ketone redline, the audit fails.
