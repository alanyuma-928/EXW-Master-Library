### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article from the last 24 months related to **Osteoporosis Loading**, **Arthritis Pain Management**, or **Sarcopenia Power Training**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform a "Musculoskeletal Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the specific functional trend or physiological outcome identified?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with the ACSM 12th Ed standards?
   * **Solve:** How does this research improve the accuracy of your musculoskeletal audits?

&nbsp;

<div style="background-color: #FFFDD0; color: #000080; padding: 15px; border: 2px solid #000080; border-radius: 8px;">
  <h4>🛠️ The Skeletal Linter</h4>
  Prioritize research that evaluates the efficacy of high-velocity power training in frail populations. If the study suggests a new clinical redline for fracture risk, flag its relevance to your Bone Audit (Unit 3.1).
</div>

### 🌟 10/10 Student Exemplar (Musculoskeletal Research Audit)
> "**Source:** Thompson, J. et al. (2024). Efficacy of Explosive Resistance Training in Sarcopenic Adults. *MSSE*.  
> **Pattern:** High-velocity power training resulted in a 15% reduction in fall incidence compared to traditional slow-speed strength training.  
> **Rule:** ACSM 12th Ed mandates power-based auditing (Unit 3.3) for older adults at high fall risk.  
> **Solve:** To maintain functional sovereignty, all resistance audits for sarcopenic patients must prioritize the explosive concentric phase over slow tempo movements to bypass the functional decline redline."

&nbsp;

<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border: 2px solid #FFC72C; border-radius: 8px;">
  <h4>⚠️ The Functional Sovereignty Gate</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when we rely on "safety-first" low-intensity movements for populations that require high-velocity stimuli to prevent falls. If your research audit ignores the power mandate (Unit 3.3), it fails the sovereignty check.
