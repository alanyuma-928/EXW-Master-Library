# Module 6 Rules: Bone Health (Osteoporosis)
## SSoT: PAGA 2018 (2nd Ed)

### 🏛️ CLINICAL RULES (PAGA CHAPTER 6)
1. **Weight-Bearing Mandate:** Walking, climbing stairs, or resistance training to stimulate bone density.
2. **Muscle Strengthening:** 2+ days/week; targeting all major muscle groups.
3. **Balance Rule:** Integrate balance training to prevent falls (the primary source of fracture risk).

### 🛡️ SAFETY GATES
* **Fracture Risk Guard:** Avoid high-impact jumping or extreme spinal twisting if bone density is severely low.
* **SSoT Sync:** Maintain the "Some is Better" stance for sedentary seniors.

*Mission Loop: Pattern (Low BMD) | Rule (Weight-Bearing/Balance) | Solve (Fall Prevention)*
