        <p>
            Your mission is to design a movement plan for a client with Osteoarthritis who is currently sedentary. Your solve must prioritize <strong>Volume</strong> without triggering a <strong>Pain Gate</strong> response.
        </p>

        <div style="background-color: #ffffff; padding: 25px; border: 2px solid #003366; border-radius: 4px; margin: 25px 0;">
            <h4 style="color: #003366; margin-top: 0;">Prescription Template (Low-Impact)</h4>
            <ul style="margin-bottom: 0;">
                <li><strong>Mode:</strong> Swimming, Cycling, or Elliptical (Minimizing joint reaction forces).</li>
                <li><strong>Frequency:</strong> 3-5 sessions per week to meet PAGA Volume goals.</li>
                <li><strong>Intensity:</strong> RPE 3-4 (Moderate).</li>
                <li><strong>Safety Check:</strong> Proper warm-ups to "thaw" morning stiffness.</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
