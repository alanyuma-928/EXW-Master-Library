### 🏗️ Research Task

### 🌟 10/10 Student Exemplar (Inhibition Research Audit)
> "**Source:** Miller, K. et al. (2024). Neuro-Physiological Response to Varying PNF Contraction Intensities. *MSSE*.  
> **Pattern:** Sub-maximal contractions at 50% MVC resulted in greater ROM gains and reduced pain compared to maximal contractions (100% MVC).  
> **Rule:** ACSM 12th Ed mandates sub-maximal contractions (Unit 6.1) during PNF to trigger the Inverse Myotatic Reflex without injury.  
> **Solve:** To maintain musculoskeletal sovereignty, all flexibility audits must prioritize sub-maximal PNF intensity to bypass the muscle-guarding redline."
