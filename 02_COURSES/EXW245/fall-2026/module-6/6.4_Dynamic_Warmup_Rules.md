### 🏗️ Warm-up Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Gradual Gate:** Mandating a 5-10 minute light-to-moderate intensity aerobic activity to increase core temperature and facilitate enzyme activity.
2. **The Dynamic Rule:** Auditing for movement-specific dynamic stretching (e.g., leg swings, arm circles) that mimics the upcoming exercise task to improve ROM sovereignty.
3. **The Sympathetic Pattern:** Progressively increasing intensity to prime the autonomic nervous system for the planned exercise load (Unit 6.3 symmetry).
