## 📡 The PNF Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Flexibility Audit</strong><br>
  Range of Motion (ROM) is the primary metric of musculoskeletal sovereignty. We audit PNF (Proprioceptive Neu neuromuscular Facilitation) techniques to identify "Inhibition Failures" and ensure the safety and efficacy of flexibility interventions.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ PNF Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Autogenic Gate:** Utilizing a 3-6 second sub-maximal contraction (e.g., of the hamstrings) to trigger the Golgi Tendon Organ (GTO) and drive muscle relaxation.
2. **The 10-Second Rule:** Mandating a 10-30 second static stretch following the contraction to capitalize on the neuro-physiological "inhibition window."
3. **The Reciprocal Pattern:** Integrating the contraction of the antagonist muscle to further inhibit the target muscle and improve ROM sovereignty.

  <h4>⚠️ The Over-Stretch Redline</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician prescribes PNF stretching for an acutely injured muscle or ignores the patient's pain-limit redline. In The WPE ecosystem, we audit for safety. If the solve ignores the injury redline, the audit fails.
