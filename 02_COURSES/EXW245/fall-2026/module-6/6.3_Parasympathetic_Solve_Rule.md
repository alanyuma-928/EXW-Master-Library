&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Cooldown Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Gradual Gate:** Mandating a 5-10 minute gradual reduction in intensity (e.g., light aerobic activity) to prevent blood pooling and facilitate venous return.
2. **The Parasympathetic Rule:** Auditing for HR and BP recovery to ensure the autonomic nervous system is transitioning toward parasympathetic dominance.
3. **The Static Pattern:** Integrating low-intensity static stretching (Unit 6.1 symmetry) during the cooldown to improve ROM and reduce post-exercise muscle tension.
