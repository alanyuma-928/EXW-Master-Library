## 📡 The Neuromotor Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Neuromotor Audit</strong><br>
  Postural stability and motor control are the primary metrics of neuromotor sovereignty. We audit "Proprioceptive Redlines" to identify "Balance Failures" and ensure the safety and efficacy of agility and coordination interventions.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Neuromotor Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Frequency Gate:** Mandating at least 2-3 days per week of neuromotor exercise (balance, agility, coordination) for older adults and those at high risk of falls.
2. **The Duration Rule:** Auditing for 20-30 minutes of neuromotor activity per session to maximize neuro-plasticity and maintain movement sovereignty.
3. **The Progressive Pattern:** Progressively increasing the complexity of motor tasks (e.g., reducing base of support, moving the center of mass) to drive adaptation without equilibrium logic collapse.
