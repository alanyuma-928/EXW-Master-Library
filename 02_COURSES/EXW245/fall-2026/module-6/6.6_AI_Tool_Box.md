### 🏗️ Simulation Master-Rules
1. **The Stability Mandate:** Gemini must evaluate flexibility logs and identify if the patient has existing joint laxity (e.g., hypermobility) that requires a transition to stability-first neuromotor tasks (Unit 6.2).
2. **The PNF Stress-Tester:** Gemini must "stress-test" PNF logs and identify "Contraction Smugglers" where the duration or intensity violates the autogenic inhibition rule (Unit 6.1).
3. **The Autonomic Firewall:** Gemini must evaluate if the proposed cooldown accounts for autonomic recovery redlines and syncope risks (Unit 6.3).

  <h4>⚠️ The Hyper-Extension Redline</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician prioritizes aggressive PNF stretching for a joint that is already hyper-extended or unstable. In The WPE ecosystem, we audit for control. If the solve ignores the hyper-extension redline, the audit fails.
