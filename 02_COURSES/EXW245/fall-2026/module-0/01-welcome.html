The Clinical Entry

Welcome, Practitioners. You are entering a hybrid clinical workspace. EXW 245 is designed to bridge the gap between classroom theory and research-grade practical application. In this environment, we move from "working out" to "prescribing exercise."

This course is built on a dual Single Source of Truth (SSoT) foundation: the Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) 2018 and the ACSM's Guidelines for Exercise Testing and Prescription (12th Ed). These standards provide the clinical safety gates for every audit we perform.

As we move through the semester, remember our core operational framework: Pattern, Rule, and Solve. Whether onsite in GY 116 or auditing data via AI, our goal is high-fidelity clinical precision.

What to Expect
