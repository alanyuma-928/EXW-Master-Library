Professional Identity & Philosophy

In this course, my role is to serve as your Narrator and Auditor. My background in Exercise Science and Clinical AI integration is dedicated to ensuring you develop the literacy required to lead in a modern, technology-driven health and wellness workforce.

My teaching philosophy is built on the Centaur Approach—the powerful combination of human intuition and AI-driven precision. I expect high-fidelity work, clinical safety, and a rigorous commitment to the dual standards of the SSoT (PAGA 2018 & ACSM 12th Ed).

We do not guess in this course; we audit patterns against rules to solve complex human performance problems.
