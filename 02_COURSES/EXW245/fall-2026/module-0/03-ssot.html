The Single Source of Truth (SSoT)

ACSM 12th Ed & PAGA 2018

Operational Authority

In clinical exercise testing, adherence to standards is a matter of client safety. For EXW 245, we utilize a dual Single Source of Truth (SSoT): the ACSM's Guidelines for Exercise Testing and Prescription (12th Ed) and the PAGA 2018.

These authorities provide the Rules for pre-participation screening, metabolic math verification, and safety gate identification. No prescription should be issued without an audit against these benchmarks.

Clinical Safety Pillars

Your testing audits must prioritize these primary clinical safety gates:

ACSM 12th Ed Clinical Standards
Audit Point Clinical Rule
Pre-Participation Audit current activity, known diseases, and signs/symptoms.
HR/BP Monitoring Continuous monitoring during exercise testing is mandatory for high-fidelity audits.
Test Termination Identify specific physiological indicators to halt a test immediately.
