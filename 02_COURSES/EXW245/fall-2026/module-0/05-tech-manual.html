SOP: The Gemini AI Guided Learning Personas

The AI Learning Model

In the EXW Master Library, we utilize a custom AI Guided Learning model. You engage with three distinct professional personas designed to simulate a clinical laboratory environment. Mastery of these roles is essential for safely navigating the hybrid labs in GY 116.

1. Socratic Tutor

Location: 04 Lab Part A

Your Clinical Assistant. It will challenge your metabolic math, audit biometric markers, and ensure your testing protocols align with the SSoT (ACSM 12th Ed).

2. Quiz Master

Location: 07 Readiness Gate

The Technical Gatekeeper. Verifies your mastery of clinical safety gates, contraindications, and delimiter protocols (###) before you touch onsite equipment.

3. Master Tutor

Location: 08 Summary

The Career Mentor. Provides "Workplace Intelligence" on clinical professionalism, HIPAA compliance during testing, and high-fidelity reporting standards.

Operational Protocols

To ensure safety and clinical precision, all AI interactions must follow these rules:

  • The "Add File (+)" Rule: You must upload the official ACSM 12th Ed and PAGA 2018 PDFs to Gemini. This anchors the AI in research-grade safety standards.
  • The ### Delimiter Protocol: Wrap all raw biometric data—VO2 Max, Resting HR, BP, or client history—within triple-hashes (###).

Example Prompt Structure

"Socratic Tutor, audit this client for testing contraindications using the Add File (+) ACSM PDF:

###
Client: 58yo Male
BP: 164/96 mmHg
Symptoms: Dyspnea on mild exertion
###"

The Einstein Protocol (Presence Defense)

For every graded AI assignment, you must provide a Presence Defense at the TOP of your submission. This proves that you (the "Head") exercised clinical judgment over the AI's output (the "Hooves").

Submission Requirement: All assignments are Text-Only. You must copy and paste the full AI Conversation Transcript below your defense.
