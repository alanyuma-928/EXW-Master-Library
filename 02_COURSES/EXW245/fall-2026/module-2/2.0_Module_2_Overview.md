## 🧭 The Screening Mission
Welcome to Phase 2 of Exercise Testing & Prescription. In this module, we transition from foundational history to the critical first gate of clinical kinesiology: **The ACSM Pre-Participation Screening Algorithm**.

### 🏗️ Learning Scaffolds
1. **The Screening Algorithm (Unit 2.1):** Mastering the 3-item logic gate for medical clearance.
2. **Risk Factor Audit (Unit 2.2):** Identifying positive and negative CVD risk factors.
3. **Clinical Interpretation (Unit 2.3):** Evaluating PAR-Q+ and health history evidence.
4. **Phase Mastery (Unit 2.8):** Defending your screening logic to a clinical supervisor.
