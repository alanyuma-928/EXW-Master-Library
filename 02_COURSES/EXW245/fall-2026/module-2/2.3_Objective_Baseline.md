### 🏗️ Clinical Pillars
1. **The Resting Vital Gate:** Auditing heart rate and blood pressure for hypertensive redlines.
2. **The Body Comp Rule:** Measuring BMI, waist circumference, and body fat percentage per ACSM protocols.
3. **The Baseline Threshold:** Establishing the metabolic "Floor" for the client's prescription.
