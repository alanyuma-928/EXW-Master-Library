### 🏗️ Clinical Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Hypertensive Gate:** Auditing resting blood pressure against the 130/80 mmHg threshold (Stage 1 Hypertension).
2. **The Tachycardia Redline:** Identifying resting heart rates above 100 bpm as a contraindication for immediate high-intensity exercise.
3. **The Anthropometric Rule:** Establishing baseline BMI and waist circumference to identify metabolic risk clusters.
