## 📡 The Narrative Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Subjective Audit</strong><br>
  The client's story is the first line of clinical data. We audit the subjective history to identify self-reported barriers and goals before the physical engine is engaged.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Clinical Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Medical History Gate:** Auditing past cardiovascular, metabolic, and renal events.
2. **The PAR-Q+ Protocol:** Using the 2024 Physical Activity Readiness Questionnaire for Everyone as the primary screening linter.
3. **The Lifestyle Redline:** Identifying "Hidden Barriers" (e.g., sedentary behavior, tobacco use, and medication adherence) that impact clinical safety.

&nbsp;

<div style="background-color: #FFFDD0; color: #000080; padding: 15px; border: 2px solid #000080; border-radius: 8px;">
  <h4>🛠️ Subjective Audit Drill (P-T-C-F)</h4>
  <strong>Engine:</strong> PAR-Q+ Narrative Linter<br>
  <strong>Prompt:</strong> "Act as a Clinical Intake Specialist (Persona). Evaluate the following patient interview transcript (Context) against the 2024 PAR-Q+ seven fundamental questions (Task). Output a Subjective Summary Table identifying any 'YES' responses and flagging the specific clinical follow-up required (Format)."
</div>

&nbsp;

<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border: 2px solid #FFC72C; border-radius: 8px;">
  <h4>⚠️ The PAR-Q+ Redline</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician skips the subjective gate. If a client answers "YES" to any of the fundamental questions and the audit fails to flag it for professional clearance, the mission is aborted. If the solve ignores the lifestyle redline, it fails the sovereignty check.
</div>
