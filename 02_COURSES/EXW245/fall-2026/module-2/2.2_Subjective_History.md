### 🏗️ Clinical Pillars
1. **The Medical History:** Identifying past injuries, surgeries, and chronic conditions.
2. **The PAR-Q+ Standard:** Using the Physical Activity Readiness Questionnaire for Everyone to flag potential risks.
3. **The Lifestyle Redline:** Auditing sedentary behavior, sleep hygiene, and stress levels.
