### 🏗️ Simulation Master-Rules
1. **The Symptom Smuggler:** Gemini must evaluate patient health histories for subtle mentions of "indigestion", "dizziness", or "ankle swelling" and flag them as specific "Big 9" redlines.
2. **The Algorithm Stress-Tester:** Gemini must "stress-test" a proposed screening solve against the ACSM Algorithm and force the "Medical Clearance" gate if symptoms are present.
3. **The Consent Firewall:** Gemini must evaluate if the informed consent was signed *before* any testing or data collection began in the simulation.
