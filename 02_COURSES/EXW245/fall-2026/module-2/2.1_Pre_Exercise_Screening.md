### 🏗️ Clinical Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Three-Variable Gate:** Current physical activity level, presence of signs/symptoms, and known disease status.
2. **The Medical Clearance Rule:** Identifying when a client requires professional clearance based on the algorithm's clinical logic.
3. **The Symptom Redline:** Recognizing the "Big 9" signs/symptoms of cardiovascular, metabolic, or renal disease.
