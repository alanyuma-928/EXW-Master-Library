### 🏗️ Ethical Pillars
1. **The Disclosure Rule:** Ensuring the client understands the risks and benefits of the testing protocol.
2. **The Voluntary Gate:** Confirming that participation is voluntary and can be terminated at any time.
3. **The HIPAA Firewall:** Protecting patient privacy and ensuring zero data leaks during the intake process.
