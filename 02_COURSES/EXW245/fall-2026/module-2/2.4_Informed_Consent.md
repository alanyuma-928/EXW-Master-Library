### 🏗️ Ethical Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Full Disclosure Rule:** Ensuring the client understands the specific risks, benefits, and purposes of the exercise testing protocols.
2. **The Voluntary Consent Gate:** Confirming that the client has provided consent without coercion and understands they can terminate the test at any time.
3. **The HIPAA Firewall:** Protecting patient privacy (PHI) and ensuring zero data leaks during the intake and consenting process.

  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician treats consent as a "Signature Only" event. In the The WPE ecosystem, informed consent is a continuous audit.
