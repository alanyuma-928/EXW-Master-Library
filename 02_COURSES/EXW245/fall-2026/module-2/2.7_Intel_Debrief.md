  <strong>Sovereignty System: Screening Intel Audit</strong><br>
  Pre-exercise screening is an evolving clinical science. We audit the *Medicine & Science in Sports & Exercise (MSSE)* archives to ensure our screening protocols are grounded in the latest evidence-based safety standards.

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article from the last 36 months related to **Exercise Pre-Participation Screening** or **Risk Stratification for Clinical Populations**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform a "Screening Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the specific screening trend or risk factor identified?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with the ACSM 12th Ed Pre-Participation Algorithm (Unit 2.1)?
   * **Solve:** How does this research improve the accuracy of your clinical intake?
