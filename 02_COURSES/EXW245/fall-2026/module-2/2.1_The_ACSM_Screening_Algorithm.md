### 🏗️ Screening Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Activity Gate:** Identifying if the individual currently participates in regular exercise (3-3-3 rule: 3 days/week, 30 min, last 3 months).
2. **The Symptom Rule:** Auditing for signs or symptoms of Cardiovascular, Metabolic, or Renal (CMR) disease.
3. **The Medical Rule:** Determining if medical clearance is required based on the presence of disease and intended exercise intensity.
