### 🏗️ The Big 9 (ACSM Standards)
1. **Pain/Discomfort:** In the chest, neck, jaw, or arms suggestive of ischemia.
2. **Shortness of Breath:** At rest or with mild exertion (Dyspnea).
3. **Dizziness or Syncope:** Specifically during or immediately after exercise.
4. **Orthopnea or PND:** Shortness of breath when lying flat or during sleep.
5. **Ankle Edema:** Suggestive of heart failure or venous insufficiency.
6. **Palpitations or Tachycardia:** Unpleasant awareness of rapid/irregular heartbeat.
7. **Intermittent Claudication:** Pain in the lower extremities brought on by exercise (ischemia).
8. **Known Heart Murmur:** Suggestive of valvular or other CVD.
9. **Unusual Fatigue:** Or shortness of breath with usual activities.
