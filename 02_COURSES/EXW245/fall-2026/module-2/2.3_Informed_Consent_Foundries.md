&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Consent Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Knowledge Gate:** Ensuring the participant fully understands the purpose, risks, and benefits of the exercise test or program.
2. **The Voluntary Rule:** Confirming that the participant is free to withdraw consent and stop the test at any time without penalty.
3. **The Signature Pattern:** Mandating that the consent document is signed and dated by the participant (and a witness if required) before *any* data collection begins.
