&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Recovery Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The 48-Hour Gate:** Mandating at least 48 hours of recovery between resistance training sessions for the same muscle group to allow for protein synthesis and tissue repair.
2. **The Frequency Rule:** Auditing for a frequency of 2-3 days per week for each major muscle group to maximize adaptation without logic collapse.
3. **The Deload Pattern:** Integrating scheduled deload weeks or lower-intensity sessions to prevent systemic over-reaching and maintain metabolic sovereignty.
