## 📡 The Endurance Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Endurance Audit</strong><br>
  Local muscle fatigue resistance is the primary metric of metabolic-muscular sovereignty. We audit repetition patterns and rest intervals to identify "Intensity Smugglers" and ensure the efficacy of muscular endurance interventions.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Endurance Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Repetition Gate:** Mandating loads of <50% 1-RM with 15-25 repetitions per set to maximize local muscular endurance adaptations.
2. **The Rest Rule:** Auditing for short rest intervals (e.g., <60 seconds) to maintain metabolic stress and drive endurance sovereignty.
3. **The Frequency Rule:** Ensuring 2-3 days per week of endurance-focused resistance training to combat sarcopenia and improve functional longevity.

  <h4>⚠️ The Load-Endurance Redline</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician prescribes 70-80% 1-RM for an "endurance" goal. In The WPE ecosystem, we audit for load. If the solve ignores the intensity redline for endurance (must be <50%), the audit fails.
