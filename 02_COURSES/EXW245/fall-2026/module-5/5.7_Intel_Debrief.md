### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article from the last 24 months related to **Velocity Based Training (VBT)**, **Predictive 1-RM Modeling**, or **Hypertrophy vs. Strength Loadings**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform a "Resistance Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the specific neuromuscular trend or performance outcome identified?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with the ACSM 12th Ed standards?
   * **Solve:** How does this research improve the accuracy of your resistance audits?

&nbsp;

### 🌟 10/10 Student Exemplar (Resistance Research Audit)
> "**Source:** Brown, L. et al. (2024). Velocity Loss as a Predictor of Neuromuscular Fatigue. *MSSE*.  
> **Pattern:** Sets with >20% velocity loss resulted in significantly longer recovery times compared to those terminated at 10% loss.  
> **Rule:** ACSM 12th Ed mandates monitoring movement quality and velocity (Unit 5.1) for performance sovereignty.  
> **Solve:** To maintain neuromuscular sovereignty, all resistance audits must prioritize velocity maintenance over total repetition volume to bypass the systemic over-reaching redline."
