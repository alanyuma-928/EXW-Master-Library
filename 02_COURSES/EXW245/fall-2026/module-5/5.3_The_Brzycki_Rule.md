## 📡 The Predictive Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The 1-RM Audit</strong><br>
  Estimated strength (1-RM) is the primary metric of resistance sovereignty for populations where maximal testing is unsafe. We audit sub-maximal performance logs using the Brzycki Formula to identify "Intensity Smugglers" and ensure mathematical precision.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Calculation Pillars (Brzycki Standards)
1. **The Brzycki Formula:** Estimating 1-RM using the rule: $1\text{-RM} = \text{Weight} / (1.0278 - (0.0278 \times \text{Reps}))$.
2. **The Sub-Maximal Gate:** Mandating testing at 10 reps or fewer to maintain the accuracy of the predictive algorithm and avoid metabolic fatigue logic collapses.
3. **The Intensity Rule:** Using the predicted 1-RM to audit the accuracy of prescribed %-loads (Unit 5.1) and ensure physiological sovereignty.
