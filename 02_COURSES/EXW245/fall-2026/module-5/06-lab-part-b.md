        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-left: 5px solid #000080; padding-left: 15px;">The Solve</h3>
        <p>
            Your mission is to perform a submaximal endurance test (Push-up) and a 1-RM Bench Press estimation.
        </p>

        <div style="background-color: #FFFDD0; padding: 25px; border: 2px solid #000080; border-radius: 4px; margin: 25px 0;">
            <h4 style="color: #000080; margin-top: 0;">Prescription Logic: Benchmarks</h4>
            <ul style="margin-bottom: 0;">
                <li><strong>Strength:</strong> 1-RM Bench Press (Estimated). Calculate based on a 3-5 rep-max.</li>
                <li><strong>Endurance:</strong> ACSM Push-up Test (Max repetitions without rest, chin-to-mat).</li>
                <li><strong>Safety:</strong> Spotting required for Bench Press; "Abdominal Bracing" for Push-ups.</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
    </section>
