    <section id="clinical-rules">
        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-left: 5px solid #000080; padding-left: 15px;">Safety Redlines</h3>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The Ischemic Threshold Rule:</strong> Exercise intensity must be set at least **10 beats per minute below** the heart rate at which angina or significant ST-segment depression was observed during clinical testing.</li>
            <li><strong>The Hypoglycemia Safety Gate:</strong> Blood glucose must be checked pre-exercise. If glucose is **< 70 mg/dL**, exercise is redlined. If **< 100 mg/dL**, 15-30g of carbohydrates must be consumed before starting the mission.</li>
            <li><strong>The BP Termination Gate:</strong> Exercise must stop immediately if systolic BP (SBP) exceeds **250 mmHg** or diastolic BP (DBP) exceeds **115 mmHg**, or if SBP fails to rise with increasing workload.</li>
        </ul>
