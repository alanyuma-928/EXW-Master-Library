### 🏗️ Historical Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Metric Gate:** Identifying the evolution of heart rate, blood pressure, and VO2max as the primary metrics of clinical fitness.
2. **The Safety Rule:** Recognizing the historical development of contraindications and safety protocols for exercise testing.
3. **The Holistic Pattern:** Auditing the shift from "Testing for Athletes" to "Testing for Clinical Populations" (CVD, Diabetes, Obesity).
