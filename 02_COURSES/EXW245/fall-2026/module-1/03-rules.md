        <h3 style="color: #003366; border-left: 5px solid #003366; padding-left: 15px;">The Precision Mandate</h3>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The Hyperbolic Rule:</strong> For submaximal testing, the Lode Corival must be set to "Hyperbolic" mode. This ensures that the Workload (Watts) remains constant regardless of the subject's pedaling speed (RPM).</li>
            <li><strong>The Calibration Gate:</strong> Every testing session must begin with a console zero-check. If the ergometer is not "leveled" and zeroed, the data is Redlined as invalid.</li>
            <li><strong>The Seat Height Factor:</strong> The seat must be adjusted so that at the bottom of the pedal stroke, the subject's knee retains 5-10 degrees of flexion to ensure safety and mechanical efficiency.</li>
        </ul>
