  <strong>Sovereignty System: Clinical Intel Audit</strong><br>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article from the last 24 months related to **Exercise Testing Protocols** or **Cardiovascular Drift**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform a "Clinical Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the clinical observation or diagnostic trend?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with ACSM 12th Ed algorithms?
   * **Solve:** How does this research improve the precision of a treadmill stress-test?

&nbsp;

<div style="background-color: #FFFDD0; color: #000080; padding: 15px; border: 2px solid #000080; border-radius: 8px;">
  <h4>🛠️ The Precision Linter</h4>
  Verify the sample size and methodology. If the research relies on a self-reported survey for clinical metrics, it fails the precision audit.
</div>

&nbsp;

<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border: 2px solid #FFC72C; border-radius: 8px;">
  <h4>⚠️ The Clinical Redline</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when we apply general fitness research to a clinical population. Ensure your research audit identifies the specific population tested.
</div>
