### 🏗️ Definitional Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **Physical Activity vs Exercise:** Distinguishing between bodily movement (PA) and planned, structured activity (Exercise).
2. **Physical Fitness:** Identifying the health-related (e.g., Aerobic, Muscle Strength) vs skill-related components.
3. **METs (Metabolic Equivalents):** Understanding the metric used to quantify the absolute intensity of physical activity.
