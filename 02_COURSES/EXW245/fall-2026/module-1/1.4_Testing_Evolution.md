### 🏗️ Inclusion Pillars (PAGA Standards)
1. **The Representation Gate:** Identifying the historical lack of data for women, elderly, and individuals with disabilities in exercise testing research.
2. **The Adaptation Rule:** Recognizing the need for modified testing modalities (e.g., arm ergometry for mobility-impaired individuals).
3. **The Equity Pattern:** Auditing for socioeconomic and environmental barriers (Unit 1.3) that impact access to clinical testing facilities.
