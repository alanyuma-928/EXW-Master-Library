### 🏗️ Inclusive Rucking Pillars
1. **The Equity Load Gate:** Mandating a starting load based on individual structural readiness. 
   - **Beginners/Adolescents:** 5-10% of body weight.
   - **Masters Athletes (50+):** Audit for joint impact; prioritize volume over heavy load.
   - **Inclusive Solve:** Any backpack with household items (books, water) is a valid ruck. No tactical gear required.
2. **The Gender-Aware Fit Rule:** Auditing for pack ergonomics. 
   - **Anatomical Shift:** Ensuring chest straps and hip belts are adjusted for pelvic width and upper-body geometry to prevent nerve impingement or hip-scouring redlines.
3. **The PAGA 2018 Cadence Pattern:** Prioritizing a steady "Conversation Pace" (Z2) to maximize cardiovascular-protective sovereignty without triggering oxidative or orthopedic logic collapses.
