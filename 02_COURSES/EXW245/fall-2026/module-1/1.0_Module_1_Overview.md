## 🧭 The Testing Mission
Welcome to Phase 1 of Exercise Testing & Prescription. In this module, we establish the foundational protocols for testing specialized populations, safety gates, and the historical evolution of clinical assessment.

### 🏗️ Learning Scaffolds
1. **History of Testing (Unit 1.1):** Identifying the evolution of clinical metrics.
2. **SSoT Definitions (Unit 1.2):** Mastering the ACSM terminology for exercise testing.
3. **Safety Protocols (Unit 1.3):** Evaluating absolute and relative contraindications.
4. **Phase Mastery (Unit 1.8):** Defending your foundational testing logic.
