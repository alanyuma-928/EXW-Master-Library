### 🏗️ Safety Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Contraindication Gate:** Distinguishing between Absolute (e.g., Acute MI) and Relative (e.g., Severe Hypertension) contraindications for testing.
2. **The Termination Rule:** Identifying the specific ACSM criteria for stopping an exercise test (e.g., Angina, drop in SBP, patient request).
3. **The Emergency Pattern:** Auditing for the presence of emergency equipment and trained personnel (Unit 1.8) during all clinical assessment sessions.

  <h4>⚠️ The Hemodynamic Redline</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician continues a test despite a drop in systolic blood pressure (>10 mmHg) or an excessive rise in BP (>250/115 mmHg). In The WPE ecosystem, we audit for acute risk. If the solve ignores the termination redline, the audit fails.
