The Rules: Clinical & Environmental Gates

Phase 2: Applying the Triple-Authority SSoT

Operational Authority

In Phase 1, you identified the "Pattern" of Mr. Jackson's health history. Now, you must apply the **Rules** to determine if he is "Flight Ready" for exercise testing. In EXW 245, your professional audit is governed by three specific authorities:

  1. Abridged ACSM 12th Ed: Defines the clinical clearance gates.
  2. PAGA 2018: Defines the activity volume thresholds (Step 1 of the algorithm).
  3. Yuma Thermal Safety Gate: Local authority that overrides clinical clearance in extreme heat.

Clinical Safety Gates (ACSM)

Client Profile ACSM Rule
Sedentary + Known Disease Medical clearance IS recommended.
Sedentary + ANY Symptom Medical clearance IS recommended.
Environmental Conflict Apply Yuma Thermal Safety Gate if outdoor WBGT > 90°F.
