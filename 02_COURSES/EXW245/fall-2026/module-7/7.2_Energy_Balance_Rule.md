&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Energy Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The RMR Gate:** Mandating the calculation of Resting Metabolic Rate (RMR) using validated equations (e.g., Mifflin-St Jeor) to establish the metabolic floor.
2. **The TEF Rule:** Auditing for the Thermic Effect of Food (TEF), typically 10% of total intake, and its role in metabolic sovereignty.
3. **The EAT Pattern:** Prioritizing Exercise Activity Thermogenesis (EAT) and Non-Exercise Activity Thermogenesis (NEAT) to drive adaptation without metabolic logic collapse.
