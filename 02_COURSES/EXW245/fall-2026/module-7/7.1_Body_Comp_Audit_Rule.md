### 🏗️ Body-Comp Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Energy Balance Gate:** Mandating a caloric deficit of 500-1000 kcal/day for weight loss sovereignty, while auditing for adequate protein (Unit 5.4/4.2) to maintain lean mass.
2. **The Duration Rule:** Auditing for >250 minutes per week of moderate-intensity physical activity to drive long-term weight maintenance sovereignty.
3. **The Composition Pattern:** Prioritizing resistance training (Module 5) alongside aerobic interventions to maximize lean mass retention during caloric restriction.
