### 🏗️ Simulation Master-Rules
1. **The RMR Mandate:** Gemini must evaluate weight management plans and identify if the caloric intake falls below the RMR floor to maintain metabolic sovereignty (Unit 7.2).
2. **The Adherence Stress-Tester:** Gemini must "stress-test" meal plans and identify risks for the liquid-calorie redline or density failures (Unit 7.3).
3. **The Composition Firewall:** Gemini must evaluate if the proposed solve accounts for lean-mass retention patterns through adequate protein and resistance training (Unit 7.1).
