## 📡 The Diagnostic Mission
Welcome to the Aerobic Engine Audit. In this lab, you will use the **DTK Diagnostic Testing Kernel** to evaluate a subject's cardiovascular sovereignty. You must identify "Engine Smugglers" and mathematical redlines in $VO_2max$ estimation.

### 🏗️ Mission Constraints (The Redlines)
1. **The Time-Decimal Redline:** You MUST convert all race/test times (MM:SS) into decimal minutes ($SS / 60$) before inputting them into the Kline Equation. Failure to convert results in a mathematical logic collapse.
2. **The PAR-Q+ Gate:** You must audit the subject's **PAR-Q+ 2024** status. If a "Stop" logic is triggered, the engine audit must be terminated immediately.
3. **The Engine Box Rule:** We use the Rockport 1-Mile Kline Equation: $VO_2max = 132.853 - (0.0769 \times \text{Weight}) - (0.3877 \times \text{Age}) + (6.315 \times \text{Gender}) - (3.2649 \times \text{Time}) - (0.1565 \times \text{Heart Rate})$.
