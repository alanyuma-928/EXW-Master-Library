## 📡 The Prescription Mission
Welcome to the Exercise Prescription Audit. In this lab, you will use the **DTK Diagnostic Testing Kernel** to evaluate a subject's metabolic and aerobic sovereignty. You must identify "Prescription Smugglers" and environmental redlines in FITT-VP design.

### 🏗️ Mission Constraints (The Redlines)
1. **The Zone 10 Redline:** You MUST audit the ambient temperature of the training environment. If the Heat Index exceeds **Zone 10 (100°F+)**, all high-intensity aerobic prescriptions must be downgraded to a "Safety Maintenance" protocol to prevent thermal logic collapses.
2. **The Karvonen Mandate:** Every intensity prescription MUST use the Karvonen formula ($Target HR = ((MHR - RHR) \times \%Intensity) + RHR$). Failure to include the RHR adjustment results in a cardiovascular sovereignty failure.
3. **The Engine Box Rule:** We use the Rockport-derived $VO_2max$ (Lab 1) to establish the initial intensity gates.
