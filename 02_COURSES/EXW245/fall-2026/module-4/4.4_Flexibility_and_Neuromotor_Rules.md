## 📡 The Neuromotor Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Neuromotor Audit</strong><br>
  Functional mobility and balance are the primary metrics of fall prevention and longevity. We audit flexibility and neuromotor prescriptions to identify mechanical redlines and ensure movement sovereignty.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Neuromotor Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Flexibility Gate:** Prescribing stretching activities (static, dynamic, PNF) for at least 2-3 days per week to improve ROM.
2. **The Neuromotor Rule:** Incorporating balance, agility, and coordination exercises (e.g., Tai Chi, Yoga) for at least 20-30 minutes per day for older adults.
3. **The Intensity Pattern:** Ensuring stretching is performed to the point of "slight discomfort" and neuromotor tasks are sufficiently challenging to induce adaptation without fall risk.
