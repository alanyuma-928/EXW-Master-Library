  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Prescription Audit</strong><br>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Prescription Pillars (PAGA Standards)
1. **The 150-Minute Gate:** Ensuring all healthy adults achieve at least 150-300 minutes of moderate-intensity (or 75-150 minutes of vigorous-intensity) aerobic activity per week.
2. **The FITT-VP Rule:** Auditing prescriptions for Frequency, Intensity, Time, Type, Volume, and Progression to ensure a holistic dosage.
3. **The Muscle Rule:** Mandating muscle-strengthening activities of at least moderate intensity involving all major muscle groups on 2 or more days per week.

  <h4>⚠️ The Volume Redline</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician prescribes a FITT-VP plan that fails to meet the 150-minute aerobic gate or ignores the muscular strength mandate. In The WPE ecosystem, we audit for dosage sufficiency. If the solve ignores the 150-minute redline, the audit fails.
