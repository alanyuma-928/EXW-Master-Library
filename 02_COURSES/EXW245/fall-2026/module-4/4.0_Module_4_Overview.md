## 🧭 The Prescription Mission
Welcome to Phase 4 of Exercise Testing & Prescription. In this module, we transition from assessment execution to the actual design of **Exercise Prescriptions (ExRx)**, specifically focusing on **Intensity Calculations**, **FITT-VP Volume**, and **Clinical Programming**.

### 🏗️ Learning Scaffolds
1. **Intensity Rules (Unit 4.1):** Mastering the Karvonen and HRR accuracy gate.
2. **PAGA Standards (Unit 4.2):** Achieving the 150-minute aerobic volume redline.
3. **Metabolic Rules (Unit 4.3):** Evaluating MET-to-VO2 precision.
4. **Prescription Mastery (Unit 4.8):** Defending your ExRx logic.
