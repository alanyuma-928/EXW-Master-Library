### 🏗️ Intensity Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The HRR Gate:** Calculating the available range between Resting Heart Rate (RHR) and Maximal Heart Rate (MHR).
2. **The Karvonen Rule:** Applying the formula: `Target HR = [(MHR - RHR) × % Intensity] + RHR` to identify specific training zones.
3. **The Metabolic Pattern:** Aligning intensity % with the patient's clinical risk profile (Unit 2.1) and fitness level (Unit 3.3).
