### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article from the last 24 months related to **Exercise Intensity (Karvonen vs %HRmax)** or **FITT-VP Compliance**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform an "ExRx Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the specific prescription trend or physiological outcome identified?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with the ACSM 12th Ed standards?
   * **Solve:** How does this research improve the accuracy of your prescription audits?

> **Rule:** ACSM 12th Ed mandates clinical medication audits before intensity prescription.  
> **Solve:** To maintain prescription sovereignty, all ExRx audits for hypertensive patients must use the 'Talk Test' as a secondary safety gate to bypass the formulaic over-prescription redline."
