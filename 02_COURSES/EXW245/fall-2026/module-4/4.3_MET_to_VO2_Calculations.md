### 🏗️ Metabolic Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The MET Gate:** Identifying 1 MET as the resting metabolic rate (3.5 mL/kg/min).
2. **The VO2 Rule:** Converting between absolute (L/min) and relative (mL/kg/min) VO2 to identify caloric expenditure.
3. **The Equation Pattern:** Applying ACSM metabolic equations for walking, running, and cycling to predict oxygen cost and exercise intensity.

  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician fails to account for patient body weight when converting between absolute and relative VO2, or ignores the 3.5 mL/kg/min resting constant. In The WPE ecosystem, we audit for mathematical precision. If the solve fails the MET-to-VO2 gate, the audit fails.
