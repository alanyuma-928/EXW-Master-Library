        <h3 style="color: #003366; border-left: 5px solid #003366; padding-left: 15px;">The Metabolic Audit</h3>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The 3,500 kcal Rule:</strong> A deficit of 3,500 kcal is traditionally required to lose 1 lb of fat. However, modern caveats (metabolic adaptation) require a "Slow & Low" approach—targeting a loss of only 1-2 lbs per week.</li>
            <li><strong>The TTM Gate (Stages of Change):</strong> You must identify if the client is in Pre-contemplation, Contemplation, Preparation, Action, or Maintenance. Prescribing "Action" goals to a "Contemplation" client is a clinical failure.</li>
            <li><strong>The Volume Mandate (Weight Loss):</strong> For weight loss maintenance, PAGA/ACSM standards shift to high-volume targets: 250-300+ minutes per week of moderate activity.</li>
        </ul>
