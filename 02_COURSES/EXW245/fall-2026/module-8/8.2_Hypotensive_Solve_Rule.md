## 📡 The Hypotensive Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Cardiovascular Audit</strong><br>
  Arterial tension is the primary metric of vascular sovereignty. We audit "Hypotensive-Solve Rules" and post-exercise responses to identify "Vascular Failures" and ensure the efficacy of exercise-mediated blood pressure interventions.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Vascular Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Post-Exercise Gate:** Mandating a monitored "Cool-Down Audit" to identify the Post-Exercise Hypotensive (PEH) response, which can lower systolic BP by 5-7 mmHg for up to 24 hours.
2. **The Intensity Rule:** Auditing for moderate-intensity aerobic exercise (40-60% VO2R) to maximize cardiovascular-protective sovereignty without triggering hypertensive logic collapses.
3. **The Frequency Pattern:** Mandating daily aerobic activity to maintain a chronic hypotensive stimulus and vascular-regulatory sovereignty.

  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician allows excessive breath-holding (Valsalva maneuver) during resistance training for a patient with stage 2 hypertension. In The WPE ecosystem, we audit for breathing. If the solve ignores the hypertensive redline, the audit fails.
