  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Lipid-Profile Audit</strong><br>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Lipid Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Volume Gate:** Mandating a high volume of aerobic exercise (e.g., >2000 kcal/week) to maximize HDL-C density and arterial-protective sovereignty.
2. **The Intensity Rule:** Auditing for aerobic intensities at the higher end of the moderate range (50-70% VO2R) to optimize triglyceride-lowering effects and metabolic sovereignty.
3. **The Consistency Pattern:** Mandating consistent, high-frequency aerobic activity (5+ days/week) to maintain the "Lipid-Transporter" effect and arterial-regulatory sovereignty.
