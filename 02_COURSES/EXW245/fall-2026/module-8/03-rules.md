    <section id="programming-rules">
        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-left: 5px solid #000080; padding-left: 15px;">The Dose-Response Audit</h3>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The HRR (Karvonen) Rule:</strong> Target HR range must be calculated using the Karvonen formula:  = [(MHR - RHR) \times \%Intensity] + RHR$. This accounts for the individual's resting heart rate (RHR) and "Heart Rate Reserve."</li>
            <li><strong>The Resistance Volume Rule:</strong>
                <ul style="margin-top: 10px;">
                    <li><strong>Hypertrophy:</strong> 8-12 reps per set, 2-3 sets per exercise.</li>
                    <li><strong>Strength:</strong> < 6 reps per set, high resistance.</li>
                    <li><strong>Endurance:</strong> > 15-20 reps per set, low resistance.</li>
                </ul>
            </li>
            <li><strong>The Rest Period Mandate:</strong> Rest periods should correspond to the goal (3-5 min for Strength/Power; 30-60 sec for Hypertrophy/Endurance).</li>
        </ul>
        
        <div style="background-color: #eef4f8; padding: 20px; border-left: 5px solid #000080;">
            <strong>Decision logic:</strong> If a client's 1-RM (Mod 5) is unknown, we default to the <strong>RPE 1-10 Scale</strong> to anchor resistance intensity while the student masters the site accuracy.
        </div>
    </section>
