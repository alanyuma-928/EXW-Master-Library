### 🏗️ Endocrine Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Postprandial Gate:** Mandating 15-30 minutes of light-to-moderate physical activity within 30-60 minutes post-meal to maximize GLUT4 translocation and glycemic sovereignty.
2. **The Interval Rule:** Auditing for high-intensity interval training (HIIT) efficacy in improving whole-body insulin sensitivity for 24-48 hours post-exercise.
3. **The Frequency Pattern:** Mandating at least 150 minutes of aerobic activity spread across at least 3 days per week (no more than 2 consecutive days without activity) to maintain glucose-regulatory sovereignty.
