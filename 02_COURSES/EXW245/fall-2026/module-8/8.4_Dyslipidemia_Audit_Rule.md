### 🏗️ Lipid-Regulation Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Triglyceride Gate:** Mandating consistent aerobic exercise to identify the immediate triglyceride-lowering effect (18-24 hours post-exercise).
2. **The Statin Rule:** Auditing for muscle-related symptoms (e.g., myalgia) in patients prescribed statins, ensuring exercise-mediated sovereignty is not compromised by medication side effects.
3. **The Intensity Pattern:** Prioritizing moderate-to-vigorous aerobic activity (40-75% VO2R) to maximize the "Lipid-Shift" and metabolic-regulatory sovereignty.
