### 🏗️ Simulation Master-Rules
1. **The Glycemic Mandate:** Gemini must evaluate exercise plans and identify if the post-meal activity gate (30-60 min) is met to maintain endocrine sovereignty (Unit 8.1).

3. **The Transport Firewall:** Gemini must evaluate if the proposed aerobic volume accounts for the HDL-transporter redline (>2000 kcal/week) (Unit 8.3).
