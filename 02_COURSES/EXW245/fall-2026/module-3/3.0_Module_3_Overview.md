## 🧭 The Assessment Mission
Welcome to Phase 3 of Exercise Testing & Prescription. In this module, we transition to the actual execution of health-related fitness assessments, specifically focusing on **Body Composition**, **Aerobic Capacity**, and **Muscular Strength**.

### 🏗️ Learning Scaffolds
1. **Body Composition Rules (Unit 3.2):** Mastering the 2 mm skinfold accuracy gate.
2. **Aerobic Testing (Unit 3.3):** Achieving steady-state heart rate redlines.
3. **Strength Protocols (Unit 3.4):** Evaluating 1-RM mechanical safety.
4. **Data Audit Mastery (Unit 3.8):** Defending your assessment accuracy.
