    <section id="skinfold-rules">
        <h3 style="color: #003366; border-left: 5px solid #003366; padding-left: 15px;">The Precision Audit</h3>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The Right-Side Rule:</strong> All skinfold measurements must be taken on the right side of the body to maintain data consistency and reliability.</li>
            <li><strong>The Caliper Gate:</strong> Calipers must be placed 1cm away from the thumb and finger, perpendicular to the skinfold, and read within 1-2 seconds.</li>
            <li><strong>The Duplication Clause:</strong> Retest if duplicate measurements are not within 1-2mm. Rotate through sites to allow the skin to regain normal thickness between measures.</li>
        </ul>
        
        <div style="background-color: #fef2f2; padding: 20px; border-left: 5px solid #dc2626;">
            <strong>Technical Warning:</strong> Skinfold testing has a <strong>Standard Error of Estimate (SEE) of +/- 3.5%</strong>. This math must be disclosed to every client to ensure a realistic clinical portrait.
