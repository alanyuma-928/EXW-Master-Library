### 🏗️ Testing Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Steady-State Gate:** Identifying the "Steady State" heart rate (two consecutive readings within 5 bpm) as the non-negotiable requirement for progressing testing stages.
2. **The Intensity Rule:** Ensuring the heart rate stays between 110 bpm and 70% of heart rate reserve (HRR) or 85% of age-predicted maximal heart rate (APMHR) during submaximal testing.
3. **The Modality Pattern:** Selecting the appropriate testing modality (e.g., treadmill vs cycle) based on the patient's population profile and mechanical efficiency.
