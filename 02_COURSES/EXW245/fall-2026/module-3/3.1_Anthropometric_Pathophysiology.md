## 📡 The Anthropometric Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Anthropometric Audit</strong><br>
  Adiposity is the primary metric of chronic disease risk. We audit anthropometric patterns (BMI, Waist Circumference) to identify clinical redlines and ensure the safety of movement interventions for obese populations.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Pathophysiological Pillars (Clinical Standards)
1. **The BMI Gate:** Identifying the specific cut-points for Overweight (25-29.9 kg/m²) and Obesity (≥30 kg/m²) as mandated by the WHO.
2. **The Waist Rule:** Auditing for android (apple-shaped) vs gynoid (pear-shaped) obesity and identifying the increased risk of CMR disease with high waist circumferences.
3. **The Metabolic Pattern:** Understanding the relationship between excess visceral fat and insulin resistance (Unit 3.2 of EXW265).
