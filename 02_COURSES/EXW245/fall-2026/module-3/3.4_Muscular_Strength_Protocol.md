## 📡 The Strength Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Strength Audit</strong><br>
  Muscular strength is a primary metric of health-related fitness. We audit 1-RM testing patterns to identify mechanical redlines and ensure the safety of maximal force production interventions.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Strength Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Warm-Up Gate:** Completing a specific warm-up (e.g., 5-10 reps at 40-60% estimated 1-RM) before attempting maximal loads.
2. **The Progression Rule:** Increasing the load by 5-10% for upper body or 10-20% for lower body until the subject cannot complete a full repetition.
3. **The Rest Pattern:** Allowing 3-5 minutes of rest between attempts to ensure full ATP-CP recovery and maintain instructional sovereignty.
