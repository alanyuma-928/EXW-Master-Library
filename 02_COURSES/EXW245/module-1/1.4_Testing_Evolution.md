1. **The Observation Mandate:** Early manual pulse-taking and visual stress cues (Pre-ACSM).
2. **The Metric Redline:** The standardization of the VO2 Max test and the emergence of the ACSM guidelines.
3. **The Digital Turn:** The integration of wearable telemetry and real-time metabolic auditing (The Modern Clinical Era).
