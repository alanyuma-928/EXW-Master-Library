### 🏗️ Clinical Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **Cardiovascular Drift:** The gradual increase in heart rate during steady-state exercise, exacerbated by fluid loss and core temperature rise.
2. **The Stroke Volume Rule:** Understanding how decreased plasma volume leads to compensatory heart rate increases.
3. **The Thermal HR Gate:** Adjusting HR target zones when ambient temperatures exceed the clinical "Comfort Zone."
