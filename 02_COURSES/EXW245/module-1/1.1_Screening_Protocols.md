### 🏗️ Clinical Mandates (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Clearance Rule:** Determining the need for medical clearance based on current activity levels and known conditions.
2. **The Symptom Check:** Identifying "Major Signs and Symptoms" (e.g., chest pain, dizziness) that trigger a hard stop.
3. **The Risk Stratification Rule:** Categorizing clients based on metabolic, renal, or cardiovascular disease presence.

&nbsp;

### 📂 Operational Sequence
* **The Pattern:** Identifying high-risk markers in the PAR-Q+ or medical history.
* **The Rule:** Applying the ACSM Algorithm to determine the intensity ceiling.
* **The Solve:** Securing clinical clearance or adjusting the FITT-VP dosage for safety.
