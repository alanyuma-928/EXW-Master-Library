### 🏗️ Clinical Metrics (ACSM Standards)
1. **MET (Metabolic Equivalent):** 1 MET = 3.5 mL/kg/min (Resting Metabolic Rate).
2. **Wattage:** A measure of mechanical power output, critical for cycle ergometry and resistance audits.
3. **The VO2 Rule:** Converting oxygen consumption into caloric expenditure and intensity categories.
