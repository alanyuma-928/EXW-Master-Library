🔄 THE MISSION LOOP: Lipid Logic

🔍 The Pattern: Identifying excessive saturated fat intake and insufficient consumption of anti-inflammatory omega-3 fatty acids.

📏 The Rule: ACSM 12th Ed: Limit saturated fat to less than 10% of total calories; prioritize replacement with unsaturated lipid sources.

🛠️ The Solve: Substituting pro-inflammatory lipid sources with heart-healthy MUFAs and PUFAs to support cardiovascular and metabolic health.
