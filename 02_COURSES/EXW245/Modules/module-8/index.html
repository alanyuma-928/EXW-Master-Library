🔄 THE MISSION LOOP: Glycogen Economy

🔍 The Pattern: Identifying premature depletion of glycogen stores and metabolic "bonking" during prolonged aerobic activity exceeding 90 minutes.

📏 The Rule: PAGA 2018 / PAGA 2018 (2nd Ed) (Hard Training): Adherence to specific carb-loading and intra-event carbohydrate fueling requirements.

🛠️ The Solve: Calculating hourly carbohydrate requirements (30-60g/hr) to maintain blood glucose and performance pace in endurance scenarios.
