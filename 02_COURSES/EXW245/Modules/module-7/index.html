🔄 THE MISSION LOOP: Body Comp Engineering

🔍 The Pattern: Observing rapid weight fluctuations that result in the detrimental loss of Lean Body Mass (LBM) during training cycles.

📏 The Rule: ACSM 12th Ed / PAGA: Achieve safe body composition flux via sustained, moderate caloric deficits or surpluses supported by resistance training.

🛠️ The Solve: Engineering a high-protein, moderate-deficit nutritional plan to preserve LBM and performance during periods of body weight adjustment.
