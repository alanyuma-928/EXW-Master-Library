### 🏗️ Ag-Worker Pillars (DGA Standards)
1. **The Beverage Protocol:** Prioritizing water and unsweetened beverages over sugar-laden sports drinks.
2. **The Electrolyte Rule:** Balancing sodium and potassium intake to offset heavy sweat losses in the field.
3. **The Sugar Gate:** Identifying "Invisible Sugars" (Module 1.1) in commercial hydration products that violate the 10% DGA limit.
