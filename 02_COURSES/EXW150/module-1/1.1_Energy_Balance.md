### 🏗️ The Rules of Engagement (DGA Standards)
1. **The Energy Rule:** Calories are the currency of movement. Total Energy Expenditure (TEE) must balance against nutrient intake.
2. **The Nutrient Density Rule:** Prioritizing "Real Food" (Module 0) to ensure the 85/15 ratio (85% nutrient-dense vs. 15% discretionary).
3. **The Sugar Gate:** Maintaining the strict 10% limit on added sugars to protect insulin sensitivity.

&nbsp;

### 📂 Operational Sequence
* **The Pattern:** Identifying "Empty Calorie" signatures in dietary logs.
* **The Rule:** Applying the 2,000-calorie reference pattern adjusted for activity.
* **The Solve:** Architecting a nutrient-dense meal plan that clears the metabolic deficit.
