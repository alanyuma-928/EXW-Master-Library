### 🏗️ Evolutionary Pillars
1. **The Forager Mandate:** Diverse, nutrient-dense, whole-food consumption (Pre-Agriculture).
2. **The Agricultural Redline:** The shift to monocropping and grain-heavy diets (The Neolithic Revolution).
3. **The Industrial Turn:** The emergence of ultra-processed foods, refined sugars, and seed oils in the late 20th century.
