Module 9.0: Nutrition for Strength/Power Athletes

"Maximizing Hypertrophy & Force."

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Analyze the metabolic demands of high-intensity, anaerobic resistance training.
  • Evaluate the synergistic role of carbohydrates and protein in post-workout recovery.
  • Apply **Athlete's Plate (Moderate to Hard)** standards to optimize nutrient timing for hypertrophy.
  • Identify culturally relevant, nutrient-dense meal patterns that support lean mass gains in Yuma athletes.

📖 Instructional Content

Strength and power athletes require a high degree of precision in both caloric surplus management and nutrient timing. In this module, we will explore the "Anabolic Window" and why the co-ingestion of carbohydrates and high-quality protein is vital for maximal muscle protein synthesis (MPS) following resistance training. Using the **DGA 2025-2030** as our guide for whole-food nutrient density, we will investigate how to scale intake to support high force output and structural adaptation while avoiding excessive adipose tissue gain.
