Module 8.0: Nutrition for Endurance Athletes

"Managing the Glycogen Economy."

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Analyze the metabolic demands of prolonged aerobic exercise (>90 minutes).
  • Explain the physiological mechanism of "hitting the wall" (glycogen depletion).
  • Apply PAGA 2018 and Athlete's Plate standards for intra-event carbohydrate fueling (30-60g/hr).
  • Develop evidence-based carbohydrate loading protocols for competitive endurance events.

📖 Instructional Content

Endurance performance is often limited by the body's finite capacity to store glycogen. This module focuses on the specialized nutritional strategies required to maintain blood glucose and muscle fuel during prolonged training and competition. Grounded in the **PAGA 2018** standards for aerobic intensity, we will explore how to maximize carbohydrate availability through pre-event loading and strategic intra-event delivery, particularly in the dehydrating heat of the Yuma desert.
