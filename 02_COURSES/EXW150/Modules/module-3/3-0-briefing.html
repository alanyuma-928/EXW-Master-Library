Module 3.0: Protein & Amino Acid Logic

"The Structural Framework."

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Evaluate the Biological Value (BV) and PDCAAS of diverse protein sources.
  • Define the Leucine Threshold and its role in triggering Muscle Protein Synthesis (MPS).
  • Apply the **Athlete's Plate** framework to scale protein distribution across 4-6 daily feeding windows.
  • Identify culturally appropriate, high-quality protein sources for the Yuma/Imperial Valley region.

📖 Instructional Content

While carbohydrates fuel the engine, **protein** builds and repairs the machinery. In this module, we move beyond simple "grams per day" and focus on **Protein Quality** and **Timing**. Grounded in the **DGA 2025-2030** focus on nutrient density, we will analyze why the *distribution* of protein is often more critical for recovery than the total amount consumed. We will explore the "Anabolic Window" and why meeting the leucine threshold in each meal is vital for maintaining lean body mass under high-intensity training loads.
