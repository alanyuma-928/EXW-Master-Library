Module 4.0: Lipid & Fat Logic

"The Efficiency Fuel."

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Differentiate between saturated, monounsaturated (MUFA), and polyunsaturated (PUFA) fats.
  • Analyze the role of Omega-3 fatty acids in systemic inflammation and recovery.
  • Apply DGA 2025-2030 limits for saturated fat (<10%) to athlete meal planning.
  • Evaluate the caloric density of lipids compared to other macronutrients.

📖 Instructional Content

Lipids are the most energy-dense macronutrient, providing 9 calories per gram. While often maligned in popular media, dietary fats are essential for hormone production, cell membrane integrity, and the absorption of fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, K). In this module, we will use the **DGA 2025-2030** to distinguish between fats that support cardiovascular health and those that increase morbidity risk. We will also examine how Omega-3 intake can be optimized to modulate the inflammatory response in high-volume athletes.
