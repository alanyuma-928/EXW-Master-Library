Module 2.0: Carbohydrate Logic

"The Primary Performance Fuel."

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Differentiate between simple sugars and complex fiber-rich carbohydrates.
  • Analyze the physiological impact of glycemic index and glycemic load on blood glucose stability.
  • Apply DGA 2025-2030 standards for limiting added sugars to <10% of total calories.
  • Correlate carbohydrate availability with endurance and high-intensity performance.

📖 Instructional Content

Carbohydrates are the preferred fuel source for both the brain and the working muscle. However, not all carbohydrates are created equal in the context of performance. This module examines the **Carbohydrate Hierarchy**, moving from refined "empty" sugars to nutrient-dense, fiber-rich whole grains and starches. We will use the **DGA 2025-2030** to define the safety limits for added sugars and leverage **Athlete's Plate** logic to scale carbohydrate intake based on daily training volume.
