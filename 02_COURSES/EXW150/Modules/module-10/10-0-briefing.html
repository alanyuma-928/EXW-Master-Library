Module 10.0: Hydration & Electrolytes

"Fluid Dynamics in the Yuma Heat."

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Calculate individual sweat rates and fluid replacement requirements for extreme heat environments.
  • Analyze the clinical risks of Hyponatremia vs. Dehydration.
  • Apply DGA 2025-2030 sodium and potassium standards to athletic hydration protocols.
  • Evaluate the efficacy of various electrolyte replacement solutions (oral rehydration salts vs. sports drinks).

📖 Instructional Content

In the 110°F+ environment of Yuma, hydration is a matter of survival, not just performance. This module explores the science of fluid dynamics and electrolyte balance. Using the **PAGA 2018** heat safety protocols and the **DGA 2025-2030** mineral guidelines, we will investigate why "Water Only" hydration can be clinically dangerous during prolonged, high-intensity exertion. We will pay special attention to the balance between Sodium and Potassium in maintaining plasma volume and cardiac output.
