The Yuma Heat Challenge

🎯 The Mission

A Yuma high school football team is practicing in 112°F heat.

⚠️ THE HIDDEN TRAP: The coaching staff proposes a "Water Only" plan to build mental toughness.

Your mission is to use the Socratic AI Tutor to analyze the physiological risks of this approach. Grounded in the **PAGA 2018** and **DGA 2025-2030** SSoTs, you must identify why "Water Only" increases the risk of exercise-associated hyponatremia (EAH) and propose a safer electrolyte replacement strategy that includes specific sodium-to-fluid ratios.

🧪 Laboratory/Practical Application

  1. The P-T-C-F Prompt: Draft a prompt for Gemini to calculate the fluid loss of a 100kg athlete sweating at a rate of 2.5L per hour in extreme heat.
  2. The Clinical Audit: Verify the AI's "sodium loss" estimates against the ACSM sweat-rate standards found in your module resources.
  3. The Presence Defense: Defend why "Electrolyte Replacement" is a non-negotiable safety gate for outdoor athletes in the Southwest region.
