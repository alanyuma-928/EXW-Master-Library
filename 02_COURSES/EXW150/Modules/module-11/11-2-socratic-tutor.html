I need to explain the "interference patterns" between his macronutrient intake and his conditions, and develop a personalized, culturally appropriate meal plan based on the 2025-2030 Dietary Guidelines. Guide him to understand how his diet contributes to inflammation, insulin resistance, and joint pain. Do not give me the answer directly. Start by asking me to define insulin resistance and how his current carbohydrate intake might be influencing it."
