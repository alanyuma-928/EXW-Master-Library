🔄 THE MISSION LOOP (Pattern / Rule / Solve)

Every assignment in this course follows a specific clinical auditing framework:

  • Pattern: Identify physiological or nutritional trends.
  • Rule: Audit against the 2025-2030 Dietary Guidelines (SSoT).
  • Solve: Propose a safe, evidence-based corrective action.
