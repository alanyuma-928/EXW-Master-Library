"The Centaur Protocol: Engineering Nutrition"

The Single Source of Truth (SSoT)

In EXW 150, we do not guess. We do not use "vibes" or "influencer trends." Every clinical nutritional claim must be grounded in the 2025-2030 Dietary Guidelines for Americans. This is our Single Source of Truth.

The Centaur Protocol

We utilize a collaborative human-AI workflow designed to maximize clinical precision:

  1. The Architect (You): Designs the P-T-C-F prompt. You are the pilot who provides the judgment and cultural context (e.g., localized Yuma demographics).
  2. The Engine (Gemini): Provides the raw processing power. It can calculate macronutrient ratios, cross-reference glycemic indices, and summarize dense research in seconds.
  3. The Auditor (You): Verifies every claim against the SSOT. You never accept an AI output without a manual clinical audit.
