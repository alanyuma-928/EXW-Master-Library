EXW150 AI Usage Transparency

In EXW 150, we embrace the "Centaur Protocol," where you, the student, are the **Architect/Pilot** (providing judgment and critical thinking), and the AI is the **Engine/Clinical Calculator** (providing data processing and analysis). This course is designed to teach you how to leverage AI as a powerful tool while maintaining the highest standards of academic integrity and professional ethics.

💡 AI Disclosure

AI-generated content is used in this course. As a student in the Matador Wellness Center Clinical AI Residency, you are expected to audit all clinical claims against the Sources of Truth. AI can make mistakes, and it is your responsibility to verify all information before applying it in a clinical context.

All assignments in this course are designed with "High-Friction" assessments that require your unique human presence, voice, and critical thinking. You will be asked to provide verbatim text and audio defenses of your work to ensure that you are the one learning, not just offloading tasks to an AI.
