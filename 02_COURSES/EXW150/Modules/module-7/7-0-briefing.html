Module 7.0: Weight Management & Body Composition

"Engineering the Composition."

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Differentiate between scale weight and functional body composition (LBM vs. Fat Mass).
  • Analyze the metabolic impact of rapid vs. gradual weight flux on athletic performance.
  • Apply DGA 2025-2030 caloric deficit standards while maintaining protein-to-calorie ratios.
  • Develop evidence-based strategies for safe Lean Body Mass (LBM) preservation during weight loss phases.

📖 Instructional Content

Weight management in a performance context is a delicate engineering challenge. Unlike general weight loss, athletic composition management must prioritize the preservation of Lean Body Mass (LBM) at all costs. In this module, we use the **DGA 2025-2030** and **PAGA 2018** to define the "Safe Redlines" for caloric deficits. We will explore how to calculate the optimal protein-to-calorie ratio to prevent muscle wasting while achieving body fat reduction targets.
