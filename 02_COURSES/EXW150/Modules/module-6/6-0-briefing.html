Module 6.0: Digestion & Absorption

"Nutrient Availability & Gut Integrity."

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Map the anatomical pathway of macronutrient digestion from mouth to small intestine.
  • Analyze common ergolytic GI events (e.g., bloating, cramping) and their nutritional triggers.
  • Apply DGA 2025-2030 fiber standards to optimize gut microbiome health and transit time.
  • Evaluate the timing of nutrient delivery to maximize absorption during and after physical activity.

📖 Instructional Content

You are not what you eat; you are what you **absorb**. This module examines the biological engine's processing plant: the digestive system. We will explore how different food textures, fiber types, and hydration levels impact the speed and efficiency of nutrient uptake. Using the **DGA 2025-2030** as our guide for fiber intake, we will investigate how to maintain gut integrity under the stress of high-intensity training, particularly in extreme environmental conditions that divert blood flow away from the gut to the working muscles and skin.
