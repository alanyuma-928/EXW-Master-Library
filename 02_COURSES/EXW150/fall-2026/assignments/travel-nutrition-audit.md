## 📡 The Logistics Mission
Welcome to the Travel Nutrition Audit. In this assignment, you will evaluate an athlete's fueling sovereignty during away-game travel. You must identify "Logistics Smugglers" and hydration redlines.

### 🏗️ Mission Constraints (The Redlines)
1. **The Hydration-Travel Rule:** Athletes MUST maintain a hydration protocol of 8-12 oz per hour during air travel to prevent "Altitude Dehydration" logic collapses.
2. **The Sourcing Gate:** You must audit the available food sources at the travel destination (e.g., hotels, convenience stores) against the USOPC "Better-Best" choices.
3. **The Timing Rule:** Maintaining the "Mission Loop" of meal timing across time zones is a mandatory sovereignty gate.
