## 📡 The Nutritional Mission
Welcome to the Fuel Audit. In this assignment, you will use the **DTK Diagnostic Testing Kernel** to evaluate an athlete's metabolic sovereignty. You must identify "Fuel Smugglers" and mathematical redlines in caloric estimation.

### 🏗️ Mission Constraints (The Redlines)
1. **The RMR Mandate:** Every Resting Metabolic Rate (RMR) calculation MUST use the Revised Harris-Benedict Equation before applying activity multipliers.
2. **The PAGA Multiplier Rule:** You must audit the subject's weekly activity volume against **PAGA** aerobic and muscle-strengthening mandates to select the correct multiplier (1.2 - 1.9).
3. **The Fuel Box Rule:** Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) = RMR $\times$ PAGA Multiplier. Failure to align the multiplier with the PAGA gate results in a metabolic logic collapse.
