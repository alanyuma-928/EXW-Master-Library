## 📡 The Performance Mission
Welcome to the Athlete Plate Audit. In this assignment, you will use the **USOPC (United States Olympic & Paralympic Committee) Athlete Plates** to evaluate an athlete's fueling sovereignty. You must identify "Fuel Smugglers" based on training intensity.

### 🏗️ Mission Constraints (The Redlines)
1. **The Intensity-Plate Mandate:** You MUST match the plate design (Easy, Moderate, Hard) to the athlete's training intensity for the day. Using an "Easy Day" plate for a "Hard Training" day results in a performance logic collapse.
2. **The Periodization Rule:** You must audit the athlete's periodization phase (e.g., Off-season, Pre-season, Competition) to determine the baseline fueling strategy.
3. **The USOPC Rule:** Half-plate vegetables for Easy/Rest; Half-plate grains for Hard/Competition.
