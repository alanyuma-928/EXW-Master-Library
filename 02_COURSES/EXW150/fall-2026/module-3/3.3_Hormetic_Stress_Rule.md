### 🏗️ Hormetic Pillars (DGA Standards)
1. **The Phytochemical Gate:** Mandating a "Rainbow Audit" (consuming a variety of plant colors) to ensure the intake of diverse phytochemicals (e.g., anthocyanins, carotenoids, sulforaphane).
2. **The Hormesis Rule:** Auditing for low-dose stressors (e.g., specific plant compounds) that trigger endogenous antioxidant defenses and improve cellular sovereignty.
3. **The Bioactive Pattern:** Prioritizing whole-food sources over isolated supplements to maintain the synergistic hormetic effect of the food matrix.
