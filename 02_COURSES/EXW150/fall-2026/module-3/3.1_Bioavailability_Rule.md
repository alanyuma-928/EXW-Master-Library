### 🏗️ Bioavailability Pillars (DGA Standards)
1. **The Heme Gate:** Prioritizing heme iron (animal sources) over non-heme iron (plant sources) to maximize absorption sovereignty, or auditing for Vitamin C pairing to facilitate non-heme uptake.
2. **The Fat-Soluble Rule:** Mandating the presence of dietary fats to facilitate the absorption of Vitamins A, D, E, and K.
3. **The Anti-Nutrient Pattern:** Auditing for phytates, oxalates, and tannins that may inhibit the bioavailability of key minerals (e.g., Calcium, Zinc).
