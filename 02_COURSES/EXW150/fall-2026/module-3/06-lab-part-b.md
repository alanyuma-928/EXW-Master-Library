        <ol>
            <li><strong>The Pattern:</strong> Identify the total body weight (kg) and activity level for this athlete.</li>
            <li><strong>The Rule:</strong> Use the <strong>Protein Dose Mandate</strong> (recommend using 1.6 - 2.0g/kg for this level) to calculate the daily grams of protein required.</li>
            <li><strong>The Solve:</strong> Propose a "Distribution Solve" (4 pulses) that ensures the athlete hits their total while respecting the <strong>Anabolic Window Rule</strong> (20-40g per dose).</li>
