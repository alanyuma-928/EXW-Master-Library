### 🏗️ Simulation Master-Rules
1. **The Bioavailability Mandate:** Gemini must evaluate meal plans and identify if micronutrient pairing (e.g., Vitamin C + Iron) maximizes absorption sovereignty or if "Absorption Smugglers" are present (Unit 3.1).
2. **The Hydration Stress-Tester:** Gemini must "stress-test" hydration logs and identify risks for the 2% mass loss redline during environmental heat stress (Unit 3.2).
3. **The Whole-Food Firewall:** Gemini must evaluate if the proposed solve accounts for phytonutrient diversity and whole-food synergy (Unit 3.3).
