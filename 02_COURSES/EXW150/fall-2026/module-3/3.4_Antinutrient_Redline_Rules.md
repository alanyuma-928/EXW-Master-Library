&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Antinutrient Pillars (DGA Standards)
1. **The Phytate Gate:** Mandating the understanding that phytates (found in grains/legumes) can inhibit mineral absorption (e.g., Zinc, Calcium).
2. **The Oxalate Rule:** Auditing for high-oxalate intake (e.g., spinach, rhubarb) that may interfere with calcium bioavailability sovereignty.
3. **The Preparation Pattern:** Identifying food preparation techniques (e.g., soaking, sprouting, cooking) that reduce antinutrient density and improve metabolic-protective sovereignty.
