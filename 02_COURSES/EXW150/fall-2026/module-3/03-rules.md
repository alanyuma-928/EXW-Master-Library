    <section id="protein-rules">
        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-left: 5px solid #000080; padding-left: 15px;">The Structural Filter</h3>
        <p>Your protein strategy is governed by these "Rules" of tissue remodeling:</p>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The Amino Acid Rule:</strong> To repair hardware, the body requires a complete profile of 9 essential amino acids (EAAs). Animal sources are typically complete; plant sources must be strategically paired to clear the "Completeness Gate."</li>
            <li><strong>The Protein Dose Mandate (zsh.8 - 2.0g/kg$):</strong> The daily requirement scales with the "Mission Intensity." Sedentary adults require 0.8g/kg; high-performance athletes require up to 2.0g/kg to repair damage and build new hardware.</li>
            <li><strong>The Anabolic Window Rule:</strong> While total daily intake is paramount, protein timing (20-40g pulses every 3-4 hours) maximizes muscle protein synthesis (MPS) and keeps the "Repair Gates" open.</li>
        </ul>
        
        <div style="background-color: #eef4f8; padding: 20px; border-left: 5px solid #000080;">
            <strong>Hardware Logic:</strong> Protein is not "Fuel." If you use Protein for energy (Module 2 of EXW101), you are "Burning the Furniture" to heat the house.
        </div>
