## 📡 The Density Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Density Audit</strong><br>
  Nutrient density is the core metric of clinical nutrition. We audit food choices against the 85/15 nutrient density gate to ensure that every calorie contributes to physiological sovereignty and chronic disease prevention.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Density Pillars (DGA Standards)
1. **The 85/15 Gate:** Identifying the requirement that 85% of total calories must come from nutrient-dense foods (Vegetables, Fruits, Grains, Dairy, Protein).
2. **The Discretionary Rule:** Limiting "extra" calories (Added Sugar, Saturated Fat) to the remaining 15% of the total caloric budget.
3. **The Density Pattern:** Auditing for food choices that provide vitamins, minerals, and other health-promoting components with little to no added sugars or saturated fats.
