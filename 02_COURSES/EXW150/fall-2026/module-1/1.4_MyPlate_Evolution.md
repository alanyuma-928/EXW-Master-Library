### 🏗️ Visual Pillars (MyPlate Standards)
1. **The Proportion Gate:** Identifying the specific plate ratios (e.g., half fruits/veggies, one quarter grains, one quarter protein).
2. **The Dairy Rule:** Recognizing the shift toward low-fat or fat-free dairy (or fortified soy alternatives) as the primary fluid accompaniment.
3. **The Variety Pattern:** Auditing for diverse colors and sources within each food group to ensure micronutrient (Unit 2.3 of EXW150) sovereignty.
