    <section id="carb-rules">
        <h3 style="color: #003366; border-left: 5px solid #003366; padding-left: 15px;">The Metabolic Filter</h3>
        <p>Your carbohydrate strategy is governed by these "Rules":</p>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The Glycemic Response Pattern:</strong> Simple carbohydrates provide immediate fuel but high spikes; complex carbohydrates provide sustained delivery. Strategic fueling requires matching the "Rate of Release" to the intensity of the mission.</li>
            <li><strong>The Fiber Mandate:</strong> PAGA and DGA standards require a high-fiber intake to slow the glycemic response and protect the metabolic engine from sugar-spikes.</li>
            <li><strong>The Storage Capacity Rule (Glycogen):</strong> The "Fuel Tank" in the muscles and liver is limited. To fuel a long-duration mission, the tank must be "Topped Off" 24-48 hours prior (Carb Loading).</li>
        </ul>
        
        <div style="background-color: #eef4f8; padding: 20px; border-left: 5px solid #003366;">
            <strong>Fueling Logic:</strong> If the mission is <strong>High Intensity</strong>, the body demands <strong>High-Velocity Fuel</strong>.
        </div>
