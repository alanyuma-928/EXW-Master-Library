## 🧭 The Nutritional Mission
Welcome to Phase 1 of Nutrition for Sports & Exercise. In this module, we establish the foundational standards for nutritional health, the historical evolution of dietary guidelines, and the core rules of nutrient density.

### 🏗️ Learning Scaffolds
1. **History of Nutrition (Unit 1.1):** Identifying the evolution of dietary standards.
2. **SSoT DGA Standards (Unit 1.2):** Mastering the 2025–2030 Dietary Guidelines for Americans.
3. **Nutrient Density (Unit 1.3):** Evaluating the 85/15 rule for nutritional sovereignty.
4. **Phase Mastery (Unit 1.8):** Defending your foundational nutritional logic.
