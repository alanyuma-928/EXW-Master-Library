## 📡 The Nutritional Research Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: Nutritional Intel Audit</strong><br>
  Nutritional science is the ground truth of public health. We audit the *American Journal of Clinical Nutrition (AJCN)* archives to ensure our interventions are grounded in the latest evidence-based metabolic standards.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article from the last 24 months related to **Nutrient Density** or **Plant-Based Dietary Patterns**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform a "Nutritional Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the specific nutritional trend or metabolic outcome identified?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with the DGA 2025–2030 standards?
   * **Solve:** How does this research improve the accuracy of your nutritional audits?

&nbsp;

<div style="background-color: #FFFDD0; color: #000080; padding: 15px; border: 2px solid #000080; border-radius: 8px;">
  <h4>🛠️ The Density Linter</h4>
  Prioritize research that evaluates the efficacy of the 85/15 rule. If the study suggests a new clinical redline for added sugars, flag its relevance to your Density Audit (Unit 1.3).
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🌟 10/10 Student Exemplar (Nutritional Research Audit)
> "**Source:** Miller, K. et al. (2024). Bioavailability of Protein in Plant-Based Patties. *AJCN*.  
> **Pattern:** Highly processed plant-based meat alternatives often exceed the DGA 10% saturated fat and sodium redlines.  
> **Rule:** DGA 2025–2030 mandates limiting saturated fat and sodium to maintain hemodynamic sovereignty.  
> **Solve:** To maintain nutritional sovereignty, plant-based interventions should prioritize whole food sources (legumes, nuts) over processed alternatives to stay within the 85/15 density gate."

&nbsp;

<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border: 2px solid #FFC72C; border-radius: 8px;">
  <h4>⚠️ The Nutritional Sovereignty Gate</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when we rely on "Diet Trends" rather than clinical research. If your research audit ignores the 85/15 nutrient density rule (Unit 1.3), it fails the sovereignty check.
</div>
