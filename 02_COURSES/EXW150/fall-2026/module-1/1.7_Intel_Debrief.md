### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article related to **Ultra-Processed Foods** or **The Atwater Factor Precision**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform a "Nutritional Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the metabolic outcome identified in the research?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with DGA 2025-2030 standards?
   * **Solve:** How does this research help a client navigate the "Sugar Firewall"?
