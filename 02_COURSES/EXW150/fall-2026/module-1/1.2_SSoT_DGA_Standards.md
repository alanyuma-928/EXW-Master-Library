### 🏗️ Guideline Pillars (DGA 2025–2030)
1. **The Life Stage Gate:** Tailoring nutrition to the specific needs of infants, toddlers, adolescents, adults, and older adults.
2. **The 85/15 Rule:** Mandating that 85% of calories come from nutrient-dense sources, leaving only 15% for discretionary items (Added Sugar, Saturated Fat).
3. **The Pattern Rule:** Focusing on the "Total Diet" rather than individual foods to identify chronic disease risk patterns.
