### 🏗️ Historical Pillars (DGA Standards)
1. **The Deficiency Gate:** Identifying the early focus on essential vitamins and minerals to prevent acute clinical conditions.
2. **The Chronic Rule:** Recognizing the shift in the 1980s toward limiting "excess" (Saturated Fat, Sodium, Sugar) to manage long-term health.
3. **The Holistic Pattern:** Auditing the modern move toward "Dietary Patterns" rather than isolated nutrients as the primary metric of health.
