SOP: The Gemini AI Guided Learning Personas

The AI Learning Model

In the EXW Master Library, we utilize a custom AI-guided learning model. You do not simply "chat" with a chatbot; you engage with three distinct professional personas designed to simulate a clinical nutrition environment. Mastery of these roles is a prerequisite for your first audit in Module 1.

1. Socratic Tutor

Location: 04 Lab Part A

Your Clinical Assistant. This persona will not give you answers. It will challenge your logic, audit your metabolic math, and ensure your prescriptions align with the SSoT (DGA 2025-2030).

2. Quiz Master

Location: 07 Readiness Gate

The Technical Gatekeeper. This persona verifies your mastery of federal guidelines and the technical protocols (###) before allowing you to move to the next mission.

3. Master Tutor

Location: 08 Summary

The Career Mentor. This persona provides "Workplace Intelligence" briefings that connect your weekly nutritional solve to career skills such as HIPAA, privacy, and data ethics.

Operational Protocols

To ensure institutional agility and clinical precision, all AI interactions must follow these two rules:

  • The "Add File (+)" Rule: You must always upload the official DGA 2025-2030 PDF to the Gemini interface. This ensures the AI's logic is grounded in federal standards, not internet noise.
  • The ### Delimiter Protocol: Wrap all raw nutritional data—caloric logs, macronutrient grams, or client biometric data—within triple-hashes (###).

Example Prompt Structure

"Socratic Tutor, audit this intake pattern using the Add File (+) DGA PDF:

###
Client: 45yo Male
Current Intake: 3200 kcal, 15g Saturated Fat, 3500mg Sodium
###"

The Einstein Protocol (Presence Defense)

For every graded AI assignment, you must provide a Presence Defense at the TOP of your submission. This is a 2-3 sentence justification proving that you (the "Head") exercised clinical judgment over the AI's output (the "Hooves").

Submission Requirement: All assignments are Text-Only. You must copy and paste the full AI Conversation Transcript below your defense.

10/10 Student Exemplar: Presence Defense

This is how your final Canvas submission should look in SpeedGrader:

[PRESENCE DEFENSE]

I reviewed the Socratic Tutor’s audit of the client’s sodium intake. While the AI correctly identified the 2,300mg limit, I had to override its initial suggestion of a high-sodium recovery drink. Based on the SSoT (DGA Chapter 1), I adjusted the "Solve" to prioritize whole-food electrolyte sources to ensure the client remains under the chronic disease risk threshold for Sodium.

[THE SOLVE]

Final Prescription: 2,400 kcal/day, Sodium target <2,300mg. Swapped processed sports salts for baked potato and banana post-session.
