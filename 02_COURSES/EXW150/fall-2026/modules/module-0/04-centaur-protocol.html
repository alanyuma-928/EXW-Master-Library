The Centaur Protocol

Professional Philosophy: Human Intuition + AI Precision

The Hybrid Workforce

In the modern Exercise Science workforce, you are no longer competing against AI; you are learning to lead with it. We utilize the Centaur Approach: a methodology where the human provides the clinical intuition and ethical oversight (the "Head"), while the AI provides metabolic math and data-processing speed (the "Hooves").

This Protocol ensures that you do not become dependent on technology, but rather master it to provide higher-fidelity care for your clients.

Rules of Engagement

Every interaction within this course must adhere to these three clinical standards:

01. The Auditor

You are the final Auditor. You are legally and professionally responsible for every data point you submit. Never "blindly" trust an AI output.

02. Socratic Scaffolding

AI is your Scaffolder, not your answer key. Use it to verify logic, audit patterns, and simulate client scenarios—not to bypass thinking.

03. High Fidelity

We demand High-Fidelity results. If the AI provides a generic or non-SSoT compliant response, you must recalibrate and audit again.
