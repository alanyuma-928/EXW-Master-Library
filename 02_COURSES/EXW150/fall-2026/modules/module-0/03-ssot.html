The Single Source of Truth (SSoT)

Dietary Guidelines for Americans (DGA) 2025-2030

Operational Authority

In Nutrition Science, accuracy is the difference between performance and metabolic failure. For EXW 150, our primary Single Source of Truth (SSoT) is the Dietary Guidelines for Americans (DGA) 2025-2030, supported by the PAGA 2018.

This authority provides the clinical Rules we use to audit nutrient density, metabolic health, and performance fueling. Any claim made in this course—whether in a discussion or a clinical simulation—must be traceable back to these federal standards.

Technical Requirement: The ### Protocol

When auditing dietary logs or metabolic data, you must wrap all medical inputs, nutrient counts, or client profiles within triple-hashes (###). This ensures the AI Auditor accurately processes the clinical parameters.
Example: ### Intake: 25g Fiber, 1500mg Sodium, 2200 total calories ###
