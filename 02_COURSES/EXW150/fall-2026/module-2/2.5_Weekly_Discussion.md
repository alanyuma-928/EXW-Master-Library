### 🏗️ Mission Task
1. **The Post:** Share your **Macronutrient Breakdown Table** (from Unit 2.1). Identify one "Metabolic Redline" you discovered (e.g., AMDR violation or discretionary calorie excess).
2. **The Audit (Reply to ONE peer):** Perform a "Nutritional Sovereignty Audit" on a classmate's post. 
   * Did they correctly apply the **4-4-9 Atwater Factors**?
   * Is their interpretation of the **85/15 Nutrient Density Rule** accurate?
   * Identify one potential **Absorption Redline** (Unit 2.2) or **Elemental Deficiency** (Unit 2.3) they may have overlooked.
