### 🏗️ Elemental Pillars (NIH Standards)
1. **The RDA/AI Gate:** Ensuring daily intake meets the Recommended Dietary Allowance (RDA) or Adequate Intake (AI) for critical vitamins (e.g., D, B12) and minerals (e.g., Iron, Calcium).
2. **The Toxicity Redline (UL):** Identifying the Tolerable Upper Intake Level (UL) to prevent clinical toxicity from over-supplementation.
3. **The Trace Element Rule:** Auditing essential trace elements (e.g., Zinc, Selenium, Iodine) that regulate metabolic and enzymatic sovereignty.
