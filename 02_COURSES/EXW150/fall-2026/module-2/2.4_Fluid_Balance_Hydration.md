### 🏗️ Hydration Pillars (DGA/ACSM Standards)
1. **The Fluid AI Gate:** Ensuring total water intake meets the Adequate Intake (AI) standards for age and sex (approx. 3.7L for men, 2.7L for women from all sources).
2. **The Electrolyte Rule:** Auditing the Sodium-Potassium pump. Identifying high-sodium patterns (>2,300mg/day) and the need for potassium-rich buffers.
3. **The Sweat Rate Logic:** Calculating fluid replacement needs based on pre- and post-exercise weight changes (e.g., 16-24 oz per pound lost).
