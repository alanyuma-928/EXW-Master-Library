## 📡 The Absorption Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Bioavailability Audit</strong><br>
  What you eat is not always what you absorb. We audit nutrient bioavailability to ensure that nutritional interventions are physiologically effective, not just mathematically present.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Absorption Pillars (Clinical Standards)
1. **The Enhancer Rule:** Identifying synergistic nutrient pairings (e.g., Vitamin C + Non-Heme Iron) that increase absorption efficiency.
2. **The Inhibitor Gate:** Flagging anti-nutrients (e.g., phytates in grains, oxalates in spinach) that block mineral absorption.
3. **The First-Pass Logic:** Understanding the role of the gastrointestinal barrier and the liver in determining systemic nutrient availability.
