### 🏗️ Supplemental Pillars (NIH/ACSM Standards)
1. **The Evidence Gate:** Distinguishing between evidence-based supplements (e.g., Creatine, Caffeine, Beta-alanine) and non-evidence-based marketing claims.
2. **The Safety Rule:** Identifying the risk of contamination (WADA/NSF) and the potential for clinical toxicity or interactions with medications.
3. **The Performance Pattern:** Auditing the timing and dosage of ergogenic aids to maximize performance sovereignty without compromising health.
