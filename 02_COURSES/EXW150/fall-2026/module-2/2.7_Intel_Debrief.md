## 📡 The Research Dialect

  <strong>Sovereignty System: Metabolic Intel Audit</strong><br>
  Nutritional science is an evolving clinical field. We audit the *American Journal of Clinical Nutrition (AJCN)* archives to ensure our interventions are grounded in the latest evidence-based metabolic standards.

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article from the last 36 months related to **Macronutrient Metabolism** or **Nutrient Bioavailability**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform a "Metabolic Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the specific metabolic trend or nutrient interaction identified?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with the DGA 2025-2030 standards?
   * **Solve:** How does this research improve the accuracy of your nutritional interventions?

&nbsp;

<div style="background-color: #FFFDD0; color: #000080; padding: 15px; border: 2px solid #000080; border-radius: 8px;">
  <h4>🛠️ The Metabolic Linter</h4>
  Prioritize research that evaluates the efficacy of the AMDR ranges. If the study suggests a new clinical redline for added sugars, flag its relevance to your "Metabolic Audit" (Unit 2.1).
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🌟 10/10 Student Exemplar (Research Audit)
> "**Source:** Smith, J. et al. (2024). Bioavailability of Non-Heme Iron in Plant-Based Diets. *AJCN*.  
> **Pattern:** High phytate intake in legume-heavy diets significantly inhibits iron absorption by up to 50%.  
> **Rule:** DGA 2025-2030 identifies Iron as a 'Shortfall Nutrient' for reproductive-age women.  
> **Solve:** To maintain clinical sovereignty, all plant-based Iron solves must include a Vitamin C enhancer (e.g., citrus) to bypass the phytate inhibitor gate."

&nbsp;

  <h4>⚠️ The Clinical Sovereignty Gate</h4>
  A logic collapse occurs when we rely on nutritional advice that has been scientifically superseded. If your research audit ignores the 85/15 nutrient density rule, it fails the sovereignty check.
