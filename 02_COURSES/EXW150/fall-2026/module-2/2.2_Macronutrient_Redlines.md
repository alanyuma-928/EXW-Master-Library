### 🏗️ Macronutrient Pillars (DGA Standards)
1. **The Added Sugar Gate:** Limiting added sugars to less than 10% of total daily calories to reduce the risk of CVD and Type 2 Diabetes.
2. **The Saturated Fat Rule:** Limiting saturated fats to less than 10% of total daily calories by substituting with unsaturated fats (MUFAs/PUFAs).
3. **The Sodium Pattern:** Auditing for sodium intake to remain below 2,300 mg/day (or lower for specialized populations) to maintain hemodynamic sovereignty.
