## 📡 The Nutritional Dialect
<div style="background-color: #000080; color: #FFFDD0; padding: 15px; border-left: 10px solid #FFC72C;">
  <strong>Sovereignty System: The Thermodynamic Audit</strong><br>
  Energy balance is the primary metric of nutritional health. We audit caloric intake against Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) to identify metabolic redlines and ensure the safety of sports nutrition interventions.
</div>

&nbsp;

### 🏗️ Energy Pillars (DGA Standards)
1. **The TDEE Gate:** Identifying the components of energy expenditure: Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), Thermic Effect of Food (TEF), and Physical Activity (EAT + NEAT).
2. **The Caloric Rule:** Auditing for energy surplus (weight gain) vs energy deficit (weight loss) based on the First Law of Thermodynamics.
3. **The Nutrient Density Pattern:** Ensuring that caloric targets are met through nutrient-dense sources as mandated by the **DGA 2025–2030**.

  A logic collapse occurs when a clinician prescribes a chronic energy deficit for an athlete without auditing for Relative Energy Deficiency in Sport (RED-S). In The WPE ecosystem, we audit for physiological safety. If the solve ignores the RED-S redline, the audit fails.
