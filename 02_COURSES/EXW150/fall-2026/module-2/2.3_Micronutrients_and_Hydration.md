### 🏗️ Precision Pillars (NIH/ACSM Standards)
1. **The Micronutrient Gate:** Ensuring daily intake meets the RDA/AI for critical vitamins and minerals (e.g., Iron, Vitamin D, B12).
2. **The Sweat Rate Rule:** Calculating fluid replacement needs based on pre- and post-exercise weight changes (e.g., 16-24 oz per pound lost).
3. **The Electrolyte Pattern:** Auditing for sodium-potassium symmetry to prevent hyponatremia during high-intensity interventions in heat-stressed environments.
