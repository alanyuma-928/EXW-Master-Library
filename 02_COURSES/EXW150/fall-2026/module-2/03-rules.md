    <section id="lipid-rules">
        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-left: 5px solid #000080; padding-left: 15px;">The Energy Filter</h3>
        <p>Your lipid intake is governed by the "Rules" of density and cardiovascular safety:</p>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The Energy Density Rule (9 kcal/g):</strong> Lipids provide more than double the energy per gram of carbohydrates or protein. This makes them the primary "Rule" for long-duration caloric survival.</li>
            <li><strong>The Saturated Fat Redline:</strong> Federal DGA standards mandate that saturated fats be limited to **less than 10%** of total daily calories to protect the "Pipeline" (Module 5) from long-term failure.</li>
            <li><strong>The Essential Fatty Acid Mandate:</strong> Omega-3 and Omega-6 fats are "essential" because the body cannot manufacture them. They are critical for the "Software" (Brain Health/Module 9) and inflammatory regulation.</li>
        </ul>
        
        <div style="background-color: #fefce8; padding: 20px; border-left: 5px solid #ca8a04;">
            <strong>Metabolic Logic:</strong> Fats are for **Endurance**. You burn them while walking across the AWC campus (Low Intensity) to spare your "High-Velocity" glycogen for later.
        </div>
    </section>
