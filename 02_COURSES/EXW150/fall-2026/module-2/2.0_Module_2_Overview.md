## 🧭 The Nutritional Mission
Welcome to Phase 2 of Nutrition for Sports & Exercise. In this module, we transition from the history of nutrition to the actual mechanics of **Metabolic Health**, **Energy Balance**, and **Macronutrient Sovereignty**.

### 🏗️ Learning Scaffolds
1. **Rules of Energy Balance (Unit 2.1):** Mastering the TDEE and RED-S safety audits.
2. **Macronutrient Redlines (Unit 2.2):** Evaluating Added Sugar and Saturated Fat thresholds.
3. **Micronutrient Precision (Unit 2.3):** Identifying deficiency and toxicity redlines.
4. **Metabolic Sovereignty (Unit 2.8):** Defending your nutritional logic.
