### 🏗️ Nutritional Pillars (DGA Standards)
1. **The AMDR Gate:** Identifying the healthy percentage ranges for Carbohydrates (45-65%), Fats (20-35%), and Protein (10-35%).
2. **The 4-4-9 Precision:** Auditing caloric density using the Atwater Factors (Carbs/Protein: 4 kcal/g, Fats: 9 kcal/g).
3. **The Nutrient Density Rule:** Ensuring that 85% of calories are nutrient-dense, leaving only 15% for added sugars and saturated fats.
