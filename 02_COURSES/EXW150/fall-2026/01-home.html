Welcome to the "Real Food" Reset

Welcome, Practitioners. In EXW 150, we discard the noise of commercial fad diets and supplement marketing. Instead, we anchor our practice in the Single Source of Truth (SSoT): the 2025-2030 Dietary Guidelines for Americans.

Our mission is simple but rigorous: to understand how nutrient timing, macronutrient density, and metabolic math drive human performance. Whether you are fueling an elite athlete or a community member starting a walking program, your prescriptions will be evidence-based and clinically audited.

The EXW Workflow: The Mission Loop

Throughout this course, we will utilize the Mission Loop framework to solve complex nutritional challenges:

01. PATTERN

Identify the markers. We look at client health data, biometric feedback, and current dietary intake to find the performance gaps.

02. RULE

Apply the SSoT. We audit the patterns against the federal guidelines and clinical standards. No guesswork—only the Rules.

03. SOLVE

Execute the plan. We design sustainable, high-performance interventions that lead to measurable health outcomes.

The Clinical Mastery Philosophy (Mastery Scale)

Success in this course is measured by precision and safety. We utilize a Exemplary/Competent/Recalibrate Scale:

  • 10 (Exemplary): Zero errors. You identified the "Safety Gate" and provided a clinical-grade audit.
  • 7 (Competent): Safe and functional. Meets all workforce baseline requirements.
  • 0 (Recalibrate): Critical safety failure or privacy risk. Requires a complete redo.
