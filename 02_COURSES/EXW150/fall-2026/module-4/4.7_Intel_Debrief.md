### 🏗️ Research Task
1. **The Search:** Use the AWC Library database to find one peer-reviewed article from the last 24 months related to **Glycogen Resynthesis Rates**, **Per-Meal Protein Distribution**, or **Omega-3 Anti-Inflammatory Efficacy**.
2. **The Audit:** Perform a "Macronutrient Intel Audit" using the following scaffold:
   * **Source:** (Full APA Citation)
   * **Pattern:** What is the specific metabolic trend or performance outcome identified?
   * **Rule:** How does this align with the ACSM and DGA standards (Units 4.1-4.3)?
   * **Solve:** How does this research improve the accuracy of your macronutrient audits?

&nbsp;

<div style="background-color: #FFFDD0; color: #000080; padding: 15px; border: 2px solid #000080; border-radius: 8px;">
  <h4>🛠️ The Metabolic Linter</h4>
  Prioritize research that evaluates the efficacy of timing (e.g., pre vs. post exercise fueling). If the study suggests a new clinical redline for leucine triggers or glycogen loading, flag its relevance to your Macronutrient Audit (Unit 4.1 or 4.2).
</div>

### 🌟 10/10 Student Exemplar (Macronutrient Research Audit)
> "**Source:** Smith, J. et al. (2024). Optimal Protein Distribution for Hypertrophy in Trained Individuals. *MSSE*.  
> **Pattern:** Spreading protein intake into four 0.4 g/kg doses resulted in 25% greater MPS gains compared to two large 0.8 g/kg doses.  
> **Rule:** ACSM mandates strategic protein distribution (Unit 4.2) to maintain anabolic sovereignty.  
> **Solve:** To maintain muscular sovereignty, all protein audits must prioritize distribution over total daily targets to bypass the anabolic resistance redline."
