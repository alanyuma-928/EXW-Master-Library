### 🏗️ Glycogen Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Loading Gate:** Mandating carbohydrate intakes of 6-10 g/kg of body weight for high-intensity athletes to maximize glycogen storage sovereignty.
2. **The Timing Rule:** Auditing for pre-exercise carbohydrate ingestion (1-4 g/kg) in the 1-4 hours prior to exercise to prime the glycolytic pathway.
3. **The Repletion Pattern:** Mandating immediate post-exercise carbohydrate intake (1.0-1.2 g/kg/hr) to facilitate rapid glycogen resynthesis and recovery sovereignty.
