    <section id="micro-rules">
        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-left: 5px solid #000080; padding-left: 15px;">The Metabolism Filter</h3>
        <p>Your micronutrient inventory is governed by the "Rules" of biochemical catalysis:</p>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The Co-Enzyme Rule:</strong> Many vitamins (especially B-Vitamins) act as co-enzymes that are required to break down Carbohydrates and Lipids for energy. Without the spark, the fuel remains trapped in storage.</li>
            <li><strong>The Mineral Density Gate:</strong> Essential minerals (like Iron, Calcium, and Zinc) have a "Bioavailability Gate." Your body's ability to absorb them is dictated by their source and the presence of other nutrients.</li>
            <li><strong>The RDA/UL Redline:</strong> You must hit the Recommended Dietary Allowance (RDA) for survival, but you must never cross the Tolerable Upper Intake Level (UL) to avoid toxicity.</li>
        </ul>
        
        <div style="background-color: #fefce8; padding: 20px; border-left: 5px solid #ca8a04;">
            <strong>Metabolic Logic:</strong> If Macros are the **Fuel**, Micros are the **Oxygen and Heat** required to burn it.
        </div>
