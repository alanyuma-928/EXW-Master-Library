### 🏗️ Alcohol Pillars (DGA Standards)
1. **The Priority Gate:** Mandating the understanding that alcohol is metabolized as a priority over other macronutrients, potentially inhibiting fat oxidation and glycogen repletion sovereignty.
2. **The Moderation Rule:** Auditing for intake limits (≤2 drinks/day for men, ≤1 drink/day for women) to minimize metabolic-protective logic collapses.
3. **The Nutrient-Density Pattern:** Identifying "Empty Calorie Smugglers" where alcohol replaces nutrient-dense whole foods in the dietary audit.
