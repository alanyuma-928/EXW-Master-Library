### 🏗️ Simulation Master-Rules
1. **The Leucine Mandate:** Gemini must evaluate protein logs and identify if the leucine trigger (2.5-3.0g) is met per meal to maintain anabolic sovereignty (Unit 4.2).
2. **The Glycogen Stress-Tester:** Gemini must "stress-test" carbohydrate logs and identify risks for the bonking redline during high-intensity metabolic interventions (Unit 4.1).
3. **The Inflammatory Firewall:** Gemini must evaluate if the proposed lipid intake accounts for omega-3 redlines and pro-inflammatory risks (Unit 4.3).
