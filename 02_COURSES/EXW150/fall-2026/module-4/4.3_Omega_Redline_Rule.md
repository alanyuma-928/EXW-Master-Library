### 🏗️ Lipid Pillars (DGA Standards)
1. **The Omega Gate:** Mandating the intake of EPA and DHA (e.g., from fatty fish) to improve the Omega-3 to Omega-6 ratio and maximize anti-inflammatory sovereignty.
2. **The Saturated Rule:** Auditing for saturated fat intake to ensure it remains <10% of total caloric intake to maintain cardiovascular sovereignty.
3. **The Essential Pattern:** Prioritizing polyunsaturated and monounsaturated fats (e.g., nuts, seeds, avocados) to ensure the presence of all essential fatty acids (EFAs) for metabolic sovereignty.
