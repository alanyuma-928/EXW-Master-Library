### 🏗️ Protein Pillars (ACSM Standards)
1. **The Leucine Gate:** Mandating a dose of 2.5-3.0 grams of leucine per meal to trigger the mTOR pathway and maximize muscle protein synthesis (MPS) sovereignty.
2. **The Distribution Rule:** Auditing for protein doses (0.25-0.4 g/kg) spaced evenly every 3-5 hours throughout the day to maintain a positive nitrogen balance.
3. **The Quality Pattern:** Prioritizing high-quality, complete protein sources (e.g., whey, eggs, soy) to ensure the presence of all essential amino acids (EAAs) for anabolic sovereignty.
