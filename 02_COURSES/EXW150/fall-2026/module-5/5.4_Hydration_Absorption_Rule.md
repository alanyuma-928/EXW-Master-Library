### 🏗️ Absorption Pillars (DGA/MSSE Standards)
1. **The Sodium Gate:** Mandating the inclusion of sodium (e.g., 500-700mg per liter) in fluids during high-intensity exercise to maximize glucose-sodium co-transport and structural sovereignty.
2. **The Osmolarity Rule:** Auditing for beverage concentration (e.g., 6-8% carbohydrate solution) to identify the "Absorption Pattern" that minimizes gastrointestinal logic collapses.
3. **The Pre-Hydration Pattern:** Prioritizing fluid intake 2-4 hours before exercise (Unit 3.2) to establish a "Baseline Sovereignty" and bypass the acute-dehydration redline.
