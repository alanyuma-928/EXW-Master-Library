    <section id="hydration-rules">
        <h3 style="color: #000080; border-left: 5px solid #000080; padding-left: 15px;">The Hydraulic Filter</h3>
        <p>Your liquid inventory is governed by the "Rules" of osmotic pressure:</p>
        <ul style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
            <li><strong>The Osmotic Balance Rule:</strong> Water follows salt. To maintain "Fluid Integrity," the body requires a precise balance of electrolytes (Sodium, Potassium, Chloride) to keep water in the "Pipeline" (Blood) and the "Cells."</li>
            <li><strong>The 2% Dehydration Redline:</strong> A loss of fluid equal to **2% of body weight** triggers a performance crash. Heart rate rises, stroke volume drops, and the "Cooling System" (Sweat) begins to fail.</li>
            <li><strong>The Electrolyte Mandate:</strong> During high-intensity missions > 60 minutes, plain water is insufficient. Electrolyte replacement is a "Rule" of survival to prevent hyponatremia (Water Toxicity).</li>
        </ul>
