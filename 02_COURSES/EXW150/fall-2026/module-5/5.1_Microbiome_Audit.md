### 🏗️ Microbiome Pillars (DGA Standards)
1. **The Fiber Gate:** Mandating at least 14 grams of fiber per 1,000 calories to support microbial diversity and metabolic-protective sovereignty.
2. **The Fermentation Rule:** Auditing for fermented food intake (e.g., yogurt, kefir, kimchi) to identifies the "Probiotic Pattern" that minimizes systemic inflammatory logic collapses.
3. **The Plant-Diversity Pattern:** Prioritizing >30 unique plant-based foods per week to maximize the "Universal Diversity Solve" and bypass the metabolic-stagnation redline.
